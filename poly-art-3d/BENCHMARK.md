## Must-Have Features (from competitor consensus)
1. Hint system - nearly all competitors offer hints when stuck
2. Multiple difficulty levels or artwork complexity progression
3. Collection/gallery to view completed artworks
4. Progress saving (resume where you left off)
5. Daily challenges or new puzzles to drive engagement
6. Offline play capability
7. Smooth 3D rotation controls (touch/swipe)
8. Visual preview of target artwork
9. Zoom functionality for detailed work
10. Sound effects and satisfying feedback on piece placement

## Recommended Game Design
Based on competitor analysis, the game should include:
- **30+ unique artworks** with varying complexity (easy/medium/hard)
- **Daily challenge mode** with special artwork and leaderboard
- **Hint system** with limited hints per level or optional to purchase
- **Collection gallery** to showcase completed artworks with unlock animations
- **Timer/scoring mode** for competitive players
- **Canvas 3D rendering** using WebGL for smooth polygon rotation
- **Touch-friendly controls** with pinch-to-zoom and swipe-to-rotate
- **Progress sync** across browser sessions
- **Social sharing** of completed artworks
- **No install required** (browser-based like CrazyGames/Poki version)
- **Free-to-play model** with non-intrusive ads
- **Sound effects** for piece placement, completion, and achievements

## Notes
- Competitors emphasize the relaxing, meditative quality of the gameplay
- Low-poly art style is preferred over realistic for performance and aesthetic appeal
- Mobile-first design with touch controls is essential
- Regular content updates (new artworks) help retain players
