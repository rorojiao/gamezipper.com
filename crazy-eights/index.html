How to Play Crazy Eights

1Match Cards

Play a card matching the top discard by suit (color) OR rank (number). Example: Red 7 can be played on any Red card or any 7.

2Special Cards

Skip = next player loses turn. Reverse = reverses direction (acts as Skip in 2-player). Draw Two = next player draws 2 and loses turn.

3Wild Eight

Play anytime and choose the next suit. Any card can follow it.

4Wild Draw Four

Choose the next suit AND force next player to draw 4 and lose turn. Can only play if you have NO other valid plays!

5Crazy! Call

When you have ONE card left, tap the Crazy! button. If you forget and someone catches you, draw 2 penalty cards!

6Scoring

Number cards = face value. J/Q/K = 10. Ace = 15. Action cards = 20. Wilds = 50. First to 200 POINTS loses! Winner scores opponents' cards.
