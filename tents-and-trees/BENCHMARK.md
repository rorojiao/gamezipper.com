# Tents and Trees — Competitor Benchmark Report

> Phase 2 Research — gamezipper.com pipeline
> Date: June 8, 2026

## Executive Summary
Nikoli-style logic puzzle invented by Léon Balmaekers (Netherlands, 1989). Market dominated by **Frozax Games** (1M+ downloads). **No dedicated web version on Poki/CrazyGames** — major gap for gamezipper.com.

## Top Competitors

### 1. Tents and Trees Puzzles (Frozax Games) — MARKET LEADER
- Platform: Android, iOS, Steam, Switch, PS, Xbox
- Grid: 6×6 to 23×23, thousands of levels + daily puzzles
- Scoring: Coins (2/level), packs cost 40-250 coins
- Hints: Unlimited free with explanations
- Themes: Forest, Night, Desert, Space, seasonal
- Sound: Relaxing cozy soundtrack, nature ambiance
- Rating: 4.6★, 1M+ downloads

### 2. Trees and Tents (brennerd) — Android
- 4.7★, 50K+ downloads
- Hand-crafted levels, unlimited hints with explanation
- Dark mode + multiple themes
- Free with non-invasive ads

### 3. Tents & Trails (Abdullah Canakci) — iOS
- No ads, completely free
- Daily puzzles (3/day), Trail Adventures, Practice Mode
- 6 hand-crafted themes, streak system, global comparison

### 4-5. Web versions: puzzle-tents.com, GridPuzzle, queensgame.io
- Basic implementations, no polish
- Grid sizes 5×10 to 20×20
- Timer-based, minimal features

## Game Rules
1. Place exactly one tent orthogonally adjacent to each tree
2. Tents cannot touch each other, not even diagonally
3. Row/column numbers indicate tent count per row/column
4. Each puzzle has exactly one unique solution
