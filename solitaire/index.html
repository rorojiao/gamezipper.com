Solitaire - Free Classic Klondike Card Game

Play Klondike Solitaire online for free. The classic card game with smooth animations, undo, hints, and auto-scoring. Perfect for fans of solitaire, klondike, and classic card games.

How to Play Solitaire

  • Goal: Move all cards to the 4 foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.
  • Tableau columns: Build down in alternating colors (red on black, black on red).
  • Only Kings can be placed on empty tableau columns.
  • Click the stock pile to draw new cards. Use Undo to reverse moves and Hints for suggestions.
  • Move all 52 cards to the foundations to win.

Tips & Strategy

  • Always reveal hidden cards in the tableau before drawing from the stock.
  • Don't move cards to the foundation too quickly — you may need them for tableau moves.
  • Create empty columns whenever possible — they're powerful for rearranging.
  • Use Undo freely to explore different move sequences.
  • Aim to cycle the stock as few times as possible.
