How to Play Spectrometer Calibrate

Spectrometer Calibrate is an optics puzzle where you rotate diffraction gratings to disperse white light into colored spectral lines (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Cyan) and align each onto its target detector. A central white light source emits beams through grating dials, and each dial position determines where its spectral line lands on the detector arc. Your goal is to rotate each grating so every spectral line lands precisely on its dashed target circle.

Each level provides a set of grating dials (2 to 5), each controlling one spectral color. Rotating a dial cycles its position from 0 to N−1 (where N is 6 to 12). The target for each line is at (initial + offset) mod N. When a line is aligned, it glows green with a bright pulse.

Controls: Tap the ◀ / ▶ buttons on each dial to rotate its grating left or right. You can also tap a spectral line endpoint on the canvas to rotate that dial toward the target. Use ↶ Undo to reverse, ↻ Reset to restart, 💡 Hint for guidance, and ☰ Menu for level select.

Tips & Strategy

  • Watch for green pulses: When a spectral line aligns with its target, the endpoint glows green and pulses. Use this to track which lines still need work.
  • Use the Hint button: The 💡 Hint highlights the nearest unaligned dial and tells you exactly how many clicks (and which direction) to reach the target.
  • Stars reward par-beating: Three stars if your moves ≤ par, two stars if ≤ 1.5× par, one star otherwise. Each level has a specific "par" move count to beat.
  • Colors escalate by tier: Levels 1–5 use 2 colors (Red, Orange), 6–10 add Yellow, 11–20 add Green, and 21–30 add Cyan — up to 5 simultaneous spectral lines to calibrate.
  • Tap the endpoint for smart rotation: Clicking near a spectral line endpoint auto-rotates it in the direction closest to the target — faster than manual dial pressing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game free to play?

Yes! Spectrometer Calibrate is completely free to play in your browser with no download or sign-up required.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely. The game is fully optimized for touch controls — tap the dial buttons or tap spectral line endpoints on any phone or tablet.

How many levels are there?

There are 30 levels across 6 tiers: Apprentice (1–5), Journeyman (6–10), Calibrator (11–15), Analyst (16–20), Spectroscopist (21–25), and Grandmaster (26–30). Up to 90 collectible stars.

Does my progress save?

Yes — your current level and star ratings are saved to browser local storage automatically.
