HOW TO PLAY

Spiral Galaxy — Tentaisho symmetry puzzle
1Select a galaxy center. Tap one of the glowing dots. It lights up — that is now your active galaxy.
2Grow it symmetrically. Tap an empty cell next to your galaxy. Its 180° mirror partner is added automatically — every galaxy must be perfectly symmetric about its dot.
3Switch dots freely. Tap another dot to start filling a different galaxy. Tap a filled cell to erase it (its mirror erases too).
4Win. Cover every cell so each galaxy is symmetric, connected, and holds exactly one dot. Use Undo, Reset, and up to 3 Hints if you get stuck.
