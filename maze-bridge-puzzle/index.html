How to Play
Navigate the maze from Start (S) to the Goal (G).

Water gaps (~) block your path. Walk into a gap and you'll automatically place a bridge plank to cross it — but you only have a limited number of planks!

Swipe or use Arrow Keys / WASD to move. Undo to take back moves. Hint reveals a gap that needs bridging.

Earn 3 stars by finishing under par moves with planks to spare.
