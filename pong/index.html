About Pong — The Game That Started It All

Pong was originally released by Atari in 1972 and became one of the first commercially successful video games in history. This browser version faithfully recreates the classic gameplay with modern neon visuals, smooth animations, and responsive touch controls.

Whether you remember the original arcade cabinet or you're discovering Pong for the first time, this free online version delivers the same simple yet addictive gameplay that captivated millions. No download, no sign-up — just pure retro fun.

🎯 Tips to Beat the AI

  • Track the ball: Keep your paddle aligned with the ball's trajectory, not its current position.
  • Use angles: Hit the ball with the edge of your paddle to create sharp angles the AI struggles with.
  • Stay centered: Return to center position after each hit to be ready for the next return.
  • Start Easy: Warm up on Easy mode before challenging Medium or Hard difficulty.
