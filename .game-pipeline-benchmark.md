### Core Mechanics
- **Tap to pop**: Press each bubble, it inverts (pops), satisfying click sound
- **Pop all bubbles**: Complete the shape by popping every bubble
- **Auto-flip**: When all popped, shape flips to show unpopped side
- **Shapes**: Animals (unicorn, cat, heart), objects (square, circle), food (pizza, donut)
- **Sound**: ASMR pop sounds (crisp, satisfying click)
- **Visual**: Soft silicone material, smooth invert animation, vibrant colors

### Level/Progression Systems
- Shape gallery: Unlock shapes progressively
- Tier system: Easy (simple shapes) → Expert (complex multi-color)
- Timer mode: Pop as fast as possible for speed bonus
- Daily challenge: Special shape of the day
- Achievements: Complete X pops, speed run, perfect runs

### Monetization
- Rewarded video: Watch ad to unlock premium shapes
- Interstitial ads: Between shapes
- Skins/themes: Color palettes (rainbow, pastel, neon, gold)
- Premium: Ad-free experience

### Distinct from Existing GZ Games
- No existing fidget/ASMR toy game
- Unique sensory satisfaction category
- Complements (doesn't overlap) the puzzle catalog
