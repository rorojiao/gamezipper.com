Outside Clues

A digit printed outside the grid means that digit must appear in one of the first cells of that row or column from that edge.

5 33

The 5 above the column means 5 must be in one of the top 3 cells of that column. The 3 to the left means 3 must be in one of the first 3 cells of that row.

Four Edges

Clues can appear on all four sides: left & right of each row, top & bottom of each column. Not every row or column needs a clue.

A clue from the RIGHT means the digit must be in the last 3 cells of that row. A clue from the BOTTOM means the digit must be in the last 3 cells of that column.
