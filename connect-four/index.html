How to Play Connect Four

Objective

Be the first player to connect four of your discs in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Gameplay

  • Red moves first, then players alternate turns
  • Click or tap any column to drop your disc
  • Discs fall to the lowest available slot
  • You cannot drop a disc in a full column

Winning

  • Connect four discs in any direction to win
  • If the board fills with no winner, it's a draw

AI Difficulty

  • Easy: Relaxed play, good for beginners
  • Medium: Balanced challenge
  • Hard: Expert-level strategy
