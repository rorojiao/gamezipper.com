How to Play

1. Tap or click the dotted lines between cells to draw solid borders.

2. Each block must contain exactly two areas: one gray and one white.

3. The two areas must have the same shape and size. Rotation and mirroring are allowed.

4. A number tells you how many cells of that color are in the block.

5. The entire grid must be divided into valid blocks.

Tip: Start from cells with numbers — they constrain the block size.
