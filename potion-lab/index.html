❓ How to Play

1. Read the customer's requested potion & recipe.
2. Tap an ingredient on the shelf, then do its prep:

4. Tap 🥄 STIR the required times, then 🫙 DECANT into a bottle.
5. Tap ✨ SERVE before the patience bar empties.
