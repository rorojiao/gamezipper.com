Goods Sort

Triple Match Shelf Puzzle

Sort colorful goods on shelves by matching 3 identical items. How fast can you clear them all?

Select Level

How to Play

Goal

Sort all goods by matching 3 identical items on the same shelf.

Controls

  • Tap / Click an item to pick it up
  • Tap / Click an empty slot on another shelf to place it
  • You can also drag items between shelves

Matching

When 3 identical items are on the same shelf, they automatically match and clear, earning you points!

Scoring

  • 100 points per triple match
  • Combo multiplier for consecutive matches (up to x5)
  • Time bonus: remaining seconds x 10

Stars

  • 3 Stars: Complete with score above 500
  • 2 Stars: Complete with score above 300
  • 1 Star: Complete the level

Power-Ups

  • Shuffle - Rearrange all items randomly
  • Hint - Highlight a valid move
  • Freeze - Pause the timer for 15s
  • Undo - Reverse your last move

Tips

  • Plan ahead! Don't fill up shelves carelessly
  • Keep at least one empty slot for maneuvering
  • Use combos to boost your score
  • Save power-ups for when you're stuck
