How to Play Jelly Dye

Objective

Fill the entire grid with a single color by starting from the top-left corner.

How to Play

  • Tap a color from the palette below the grid
  • Your flooded area (top-left) absorbs all adjacent cells of that color
  • Keep choosing colors until the whole board is one color

Stars

  • 3 Stars: Solve well under the move limit
  • 2 Stars: Solve under the move limit
  • 1 Star: Barely make it within the limit

Power-ups

  • Bomb: Clears one ring of border cells around your area
  • Undo: Takes back your last color choice
  • +Moves: Adds 3 extra moves to the limit
