Bloxorz — Free Online Block Rolling Puzzle

Bloxorz is the legendary puzzle game where you roll a rectangular block across a floating platform to drop it through a hole. Navigate switches, bridges, and fragile tiles using logic and careful planning. Play all 33 levels free online in your browser — no download needed.

How to Play Bloxorz

  • Use arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to roll the block in any direction.
  • The block can be standing tall (covers 1 cell) or lying flat (covers 2 cells). Think about which orientation you need before each move.
  • Roll over switches to activate bridges or open new paths.
  • Watch out for fragile tiles — if the block stands on one, it will crumble and you'll fall!
  • Get the block to fall through the goal hole to complete each level.

Tips & Strategy

  • Before making your first move, study the layout and plan a rough path to the hole.
  • Pay attention to switches — some need the block standing tall, others need it lying flat to activate.
  • On fragile tiles, always keep the block lying flat so it spreads its weight across two tiles.
  • If you fall off the edge, don't worry — you can restart the level instantly and try a different approach.
  • Later levels require creative thinking — sometimes you need to backtrack to hit a switch you missed.
