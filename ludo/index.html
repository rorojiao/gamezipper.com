How to Play Ludo

Objective

Race your tokens from the home base to the center. First player to get all 4 tokens home wins!

Rolling Dice

  • Tap the dice to roll (1-6)
  • Roll a 6 to release a token from your base onto the board
  • Rolling a 6 gives you an extra turn
  • Three 6s in a row forfeit your turn

Movement

  • Move tokens clockwise around the outer track
  • Click a highlighted token to select it - it moves automatically
  • Valid moves are shown with a cyan highlight

Capturing

  • Land on an opponent's token to send it back to their base
  • Tokens on safe spots cannot be captured
  • Safe spots: start positions and star cells

Home Stretch

  • After one full lap, tokens enter the colored home stretch
  • You need exact rolls to advance through the home column
  • Reach the center to finish a token

Winning

First player to get all 4 tokens to the center wins!
