## Top Candidate: Rubber Band Puzzle — 22/25

### Mechanic
Stretch colorful rubber bands around pegs on a board to recreate target patterns.
Each level shows a pegboard with pegs and a target pattern (lines between specific pegs).
Player selects a peg, then another peg, to stretch a rubber band between them.
Bands can overlap, cross, and layer. Must match the target pattern exactly.
Progressive mechanics: colored bands, band ordering (which goes on top), minimum bands challenge.

### Why It's a Gap
- ZERO rubber/elastic/band/stretch/snap games in the 572-game catalog
- Unique tactile mechanic not covered by any existing game
- Simple Canvas implementation: draw lines between clickable pegs
- Perfect for touch (tap peg A, tap peg B)
- Novel puzzle type with good depth (color matching, crossing patterns, layer order)
- Popular physical puzzle toy (LOU GE RUBBER BAND TOY, 1M+ sales on Amazon)

### Score Breakdown (22/25)
- Market demand (4/5): Physical toy popularity, novel browser adaptation
- Unique mechanic gap (5/5): Zero overlap — confirmed by 5 keyword searches
- Buildable HTML5 (5/5): Low complexity — Canvas lines + click handlers, no physics
- Mobile friendly (5/5): Two-tap interaction, touch-first, no keyboard needed
- SEO value (3/5): "Rubber Band Puzzle" has moderate search volume
