How to Play
1
Tap a visible tile on the board. It flies to the next empty slot in your 7-slot tray at the bottom. Tiles covered by others cannot be tapped.
2
Match 3 identical tiles. When three of the same type land in the tray, they auto-clear with a pop. Each tile type appears in multiples of 3.
3
Use power-ups when stuck. Undo returns the last tile to the board. Shuffle rearranges all tiles. Hint highlights a valid match.
4
Clear the board to win. Don't let the tray fill to 7 — if it does, the level fails. Stars are awarded based on power-ups remaining.
