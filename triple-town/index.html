How to Play Triple Town

Place tiles onto the 6x6 grid. When 3 or more identical tiles are connected, they merge into the next-level tile!

Upgrade Chain

Special Tiles

Bear: Wanders after each move. Trap it (surround with tiles) to turn it into a Tombstone.

Tombstone: Merges like normal tiles. 3 tombstones = Church.

Crystal: Wild tile - acts as any tile when placed next to matching tiles.

Tools

Undo (3 per game): Reverse your last move.

Recycle (3 per game): Skip the current tile and get a new one.

Tips

Plan ahead and keep high-level tiles clustered. Use corners strategically. Chain merges for bonus points!
