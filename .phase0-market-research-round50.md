### Mechanic (spec for Phase 3 build)
- 11x11 hex grid (axial coordinates), player piece = cat in center.
- Player taps an empty hex → that hex becomes a wall.
- Cat (AI) then moves to one of the 6 adjacent hexes, choosing the path of least resistance to the grid border.
- Player wins if cat has no path to escape (all 6-directional neighbors blocked, or fully enclosed).
- Cat wins (player loses) if cat reaches any border hex.
- Standard grid: a few pre-placed obstacles for difficulty ramp.
- Levels: 30 handcrafted maps across 5 tiers (Beginner → Expert), with progressive obstacle counts and cat AI smartness.
