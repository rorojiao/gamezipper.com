How to Play

1. You see scrambled letters on screen

2. Click/tap letters in the correct order to spell the word

3. Use the Shuffle button to rearrange letters

4. Click Hint (costs 10 coins) to reveal a letter

5. Solve faster for time bonus and streak multipliers

6. Earn stars: ⭐ = solved, ⭐⭐ = fast, ⭐⭐⭐ = fast + no hints

7. Complete all words in a level to progress
