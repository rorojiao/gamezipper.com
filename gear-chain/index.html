How to Play

Goal: Make the target gear spin at the required RPM and direction.

Driver (orange): Always spinning at 60 RPM. Its teeth are fixed.

Target (green): Must reach the exact RPM and direction shown.

Gears: Tap empty gears to assign teeth from the palette. Tap again to cycle.

Locked: Some gears have pre-set teeth you cannot change.

Gear Physics: When two gears mesh, RPM_out = RPM_in x (Teeth_in / Teeth_out). Direction alternates each gear.

Example: A 12-tooth driver at 60 RPM meshing with an 8-tooth gear: 60 x 12/8 = 90 RPM, reversed direction.
