# Bus Traffic Fever — Competitive Benchmark

## Primary Game: Bus Traffic Fever! (GOODROID, Inc.)
- Package: jp.co.goodroid.hyper.busflow
- Rating: 4.5 stars, 24.3K reviews, 5M+ installs
- Rank: #6 top free Puzzle on Google Play

- Mechanic: Tap buses in correct order on color-coded grid parking lot to clear passengers
- Cross-store: iOS 4.6 stars (929 reviews)

## Common Systems
- **Scoring**: Binary clear/not-clear. 1-3 star rating by efficiency.
- **Level progression**: Linear endless pipeline. Themed breaks every 20-50 levels.
- **Power-ups**: Extra spot, undo, shuffle, skip-level, hint. Rewarded video or soft-currency.
- **Tutorial**: 3-5 interactive levels, replayable from help menu.
- **Daily rewards**: Login-streak calendar + daily challenge levels.
- **Achievements**: Clear-X-levels, clear-without-bonus, clear-single-colour.
- **Social**: Leaderboards, FB/IG share.

## Art Style
- 3D low-poly cartoonish/stylised, top-down 3/4 isometric
- Flat-shaded vibrant primary palette, rounded friendly vehicles
- Clean lane-line/parking-bay demarcation

## Music
- Loopable 80-110 BPM cheerful instrumental (ukulele/acoustic guitar/chiptune)
- SFX: tap-pop, bus-rev, clear-chime, fail-descend
- Low default volume, "relaxing" is #1 player reason

## Difficulty Curve
- Lv 1-30: Tutorial, 1 colour, single exit, hand-held
- Lv 30-200: 2-3 colours, more vehicles, self-determine order
- Lv 200-2K: 4-6 colours, complex layouts
- Lv 2K+: Endless content-drop

## Build Target for bus-traffic-fever
- Clone tap-and-clear + colour-matching on a grid parking lot
- Target: 30 handcrafted solvable levels + 3-star rating
- Power-ups: hint, undo, restart (no forced ad walls — more generous free path)
- Art: kawaii-bus stylised, 2D-canvas-friendly, bold flat colours
- Audio: WebAudio synthesizable, cheerful loop + tap/clear SFX
