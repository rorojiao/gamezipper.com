About Kaleidoscope Pattern Puzzle

Kaleidoscope Pattern Puzzle is a beautiful rotation-based brain teaser. Each level presents a grid of colorful tiles that have been scrambled. Your goal is to rotate every tile back to its correct orientation to reveal a stunning 4-fold symmetric kaleidoscope pattern. With 30 levels ranging from simple 4×4 grids to challenging 8×8 expert puzzles, it's perfect for puzzle lovers of all skill levels.

How to Play

1. Click or tap any tile to rotate it 90° clockwise.
2. Correct tiles glow gold — use this feedback!
3. Match all tiles to complete the symmetric pattern.
4. Use hints, undo, and plan ahead for 3-star ratings.
