Welcome to Shakashaka!

Click white cells to place black triangles. Each triangle is a right-angled triangle in one of 4 orientations.

How to Play Shakashaka

Goal: Place triangles in white cells so that:

  • Numbers in black cells match the count of adjacent triangles
  • Remaining empty (white) areas form only rectangles

Controls:

  • Click/tap a white cell to cycle: empty → triangle → ... → empty
  • Each click rotates through 4 triangle orientations

Tips:

  • A "0" cell means no adjacent triangles
  • White areas must be perfect rectangles (squares ok)
  • Use hints if stuck (costs star rating)
