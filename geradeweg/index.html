How to Play Geradeweg
Goal: Draw a single closed loop through the grid.

Rules:
• Tap between two cells to draw a line segment
• Tap again to erase
• The loop must be one continuous closed path
• The loop cannot cross or branch
• Numbers show the straight segment length at that cell
• The loop does NOT need to visit every cell
