### Why Hex Path Spin was selected over higher-scored alternatives
- Rube Goldberg (21/25) and Lemmings (21/25) both score 3/5 on feasibility (complex physics/AI)
- Hex Path Spin (19/25) scores 5/5 on feasibility: pure logic puzzle, deterministic, no physics
- Per pipeline skill: "deterministic (puzzle games, grid games, no complex physics)" → reliable single-file build
- Entanglement board game is an IGF award finalist with millions of players across iOS/Android/Steam
- Hex tile rotation puzzle is visually stunning and mechanically unique in catalog

### Entanglement demand evidence
- Original web game (entanglement-game.com) by Mark Gossard, IGF 2011 finalist
- iOS/Android/Steam ports with millions of downloads
- Spiritual successor to Tsuro (board game, 500K+ copies)
- Hex-tile path-building mechanic has NO browser clone in the 519-game catalog
- Keywords: "hex path puzzle", "tile rotation puzzle", "entanglement game" — low SEO competition
