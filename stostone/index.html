Stostone
Nikoli Stone Gravity Puzzle

Shade connected stones in each region. When they fall, they must fill the bottom half of the grid.

How to Play Stostone

Goal: Shade cells so all 5 rules are satisfied.

1️⃣ Shade cells to form "stones" in each region.

2️⃣ Numbers in regions = exact cells to shade. No number = shade at least 1.

3️⃣ Connected: Shaded cells in each region must be orthogonally connected.

4️⃣ No cross-border: Shaded cells can't touch across region borders.

5️⃣ Gravity: When stones "fall down", they must fill the bottom half completely.

💡 Tip: The gravity rule means each column must have exactly half its cells shaded. Use this to deduce!

🖱️ Controls: Click/tap a cell to shade or unshade it.
