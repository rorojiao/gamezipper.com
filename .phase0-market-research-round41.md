## Selected: WORD STACKS — PeopleFun word puzzle (50M+ downloads) — score 20/25 ✅

- **Slug**: word-stacks
- **Type**: Themed word-formation puzzle with stacked tiles + gravity
- **Source**: PeopleFun "Word Stacks" — 50M+ downloads on Google Play, top-10 word game
  for 5+ years. Branded franchise with strong SEO search volume.
- **Mechanic**: Letter tiles arranged in VERTICAL STACKS (columns). Each level has a
  CATEGORY clue (e.g. "At the Beach", "Types of Music"). Player SWIPES across adjacent
  letters (horizontally/vertically/diagonally) to form words matching the theme. When a
  valid word is formed, those tiles DISAPPEAR and remaining tiles FALL (gravity mechanic)
  to fill the gaps, potentially creating new adjacencies. Find ALL themed words to clear
  the board and advance.

### Why this is mechanically DISTINCT (per Principle 6 — 差异化衍生)

| Existing sibling | Mechanic | Why Word Stacks is different |
|------------------|----------|------------------------------|
| Wordscapes (/wordscapes/) | Letters in a CIRCLE/WHEEL, connect to fill crossword blanks | Word Stacks uses STACKED COLUMNS + GRAVITY + category clue (no crossword blanks) |
| Word Search (/word-search/) | Flat grid, find words listed in sidebar | Word Stacks tiles FALL after clearing (gravity), and you FORM words not FIND listed ones |
| Boggle (/boggle/) | 4x4 dice, trace connected letters, no theme | Word Stacks has CATEGORY-themed word sets + gravity column mechanic |
| Text Twist (/text-twist/) | 6 letters, unscramble all words | Word Stacks is grid-based with spatial adjacency + gravity, not letter-bank anagram |

The "stacked columns + gravity + category clue" combination is ABSENT from all 38 word
games in the catalog. The closest mechanical cousins (Wordscapes, Word Search, Boggle)
each lack at least one of the three defining features.
