Armillary Align

Rotate concentric celestial rings so each aligns with its target number. Each ring's alignment depends on its position and its neighbor's. Solve all 30 levels!

How to Play Armillary Align

Armillary Align is a celestial ring rotation puzzle inspired by ancient armillary spheres. Each level presents a set of concentric rings — you must rotate them so that every ring's alignment marker matches its target number. The twist: each ring's alignment depends on its own setting and its neighbor's, following the formula alignment = (2×own + neighbor) mod 8. This cyclic coupling means you can't solve rings in isolation — every rotation ripples outward.

Controls: Tap (or click) any ring to select it. Then use the ↺ (counter-clockwise) and ↻ (clockwise) buttons to rotate the selected ring one position at a time. Alternatively, after selecting a ring, tap the left half of the canvas to rotate counter-clockwise and the right half to rotate clockwise. Use Hint to reveal the required rotation settings, and Levels to jump between solved puzzles.

Tips & Strategy

  • Work inside-out or outside-in: Since each ring couples with its neighbor, changing a ring shifts the alignment of two. Solve rings in a consistent direction to minimize cascading disruption.
  • Use the Hint button sparingly: The hint reveals the exact rotation needed for each ring from your current state. Try to deduce the solution yourself first — it reveals the modular-arithmetic logic.
  • Match your move count to "Optimal": Three stars require near-optimal moves. If you exceed 1.3× the optimal count, you drop to two stars. Reset and retry for a cleaner solve.
  • Gold rings = correct: When a ring turns gold, its alignment matches the target. Use this visual feedback to know which rings still need work before pressing forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game free to play?

Yes! Armillary Align is completely free to play in your browser with no download or sign-up required.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely. The game is fully optimized for touch controls on phones and tablets — just tap a ring to select, then tap the rotate buttons.

How many levels are there?

There are 30 levels across 5 tiers: Novice (6 levels, 3 rings), Apprentice (6 levels, 4 rings), Adept (6 levels, 5 rings), Expert (6 levels, 5 rings), and Master (6 levels, 6 rings). Up to 90 collectible stars (3 per level).

Does my progress save?

Yes — stars and current level are saved to your browser's local storage, so you can close the tab and resume anytime on the same device.
