Tips & Strategies

1. Look for empty tubes first. Always keep at least one tube empty — that's your "scratch space" for reorganizing colors.

2. Work from the bottom. Try to clear colors from the bottom of the tube. The fewer layers above a color, the easier it is to relocate.

3. Don't rush. Ball sort puzzles reward patience. Take your time to scan all tubes before making your first move.

4. Use undo freely. If you paint yourself into a corner, restart or undo. There's no penalty for exploring different solutions.
