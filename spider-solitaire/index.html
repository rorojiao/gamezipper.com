How to Play Spider Solitaire

Goal

Remove all cards by building complete sequences from King to Ace of the same suit.

Rules

  • Build cards in descending order (K, Q, J, 10... A) regardless of suit
  • Only move groups of same-suit consecutive cards as a unit
  • Move cards to empty columns freely
  • Complete a K-A same-suit sequence to remove it

Dealing

Click Deal to distribute 10 cards (one per column). All columns must have at least one card before dealing.

Difficulty

  • 1 Suit: All Spades - Easiest
  • 2 Suits: Spades + Hearts - Medium
  • 4 Suits: All 4 suits - Hardest

Tips

  • Try to empty columns quickly for more flexibility
  • Keep sequences sorted by suit when possible
  • Use the Hint button if stuck
  • Plan ahead before dealing from stock
