How to Play Herugolf

1
Select a ball
Tap a ball to select it. It glows when active.
2
Draw the first shot
Tap adjacent cells to extend the path. First segment: any length.
3
Turn and shrink
Tap perpendicular to turn. Each following segment is capped one cell shorter.
4
Sink the ball
End on a hole. Press Check to verify all balls.

Paths cannot cross walls or share cells with other balls.
