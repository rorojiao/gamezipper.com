How to play Herugolf

Sink every ball into a hole. Each shot after the first must be exactly one cell shorter than the last, and every turn is a right angle.

1
Tap a ball to select it — it glows when active.
2
Tap an adjacent cell to start the path. Keep tapping the same way to extend the first shot to any length.
3
Tap perpendicular to turn. The next segment is automatically capped one cell shorter. Cells you may tap next are gently highlighted.
4
End on a hole to sink the ball, then press Check to verify the whole board.

Tip: paths cannot cross walls or share cells with other balls. Use Undo, Reset and Hint any time.
