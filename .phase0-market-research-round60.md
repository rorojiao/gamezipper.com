## Selected: Shape Fold (22/25)

**Mechanic**: Hinged-panel folding puzzle. Multiple panels connected by hinges are laid flat. Player folds panels along hinge lines (up/down) to match a target silhouette/shape. Unlike Paper Fold (single sheet folding) or Tangram (free placement), Shape Fold uses rigid hinged panels that fold in specific directions.

**Why selected**:
- Zero overlap with 570-game catalog
- Unique verb: "fold" with hinged panels (not paper, not tangram)
- Proven on Poki with strong engagement
- Excellent SEO: "shape fold", "fold puzzle", "folding puzzle game"
- Feasible Canvas build: hinged panel math, drop-shadow rendering
- Market: casual puzzle audience, relaxation + logic blend

**Game design**:
- 30 levels across 5 tiers (Beginner → Master)
- Each level: a set of hinged panels laid flat → fold to match target shape
- Star rating based on moves (fewer folds = more stars)
- Hint system, undo, progress save
- Procedural Canvas art (no external assets needed)
- Web Audio BGM + SFX

**Score breakdown**:
- Market (4/5): Folding puzzles proven on mobile, steady engagement, not top-10 but consistent
- SEO (5/5): "shape fold" is a clean keyword with low competition, zero browser clones
- Monetization (4/5): Interstitial between levels, banner ads, rewarded hints
- Feasibility (5/5): Canvas hinge math is straightforward, deterministic, no physics engine needed
- Uniqueness (4/5): Hinged folding is distinct from paper fold (continuous) and tangram (free placement)
