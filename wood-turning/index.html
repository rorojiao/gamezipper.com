How to Play 🪵
1
Study the target. The dashed cyan outline shows the shape you need to carve. The wood block starts as a full cylinder.
2
Carve by dragging. Drag your finger or mouse across the wood surface to remove material. You can only remove wood, never add it.
3
Watch the match meter. The green meter shows how close your carving is to the target. Aim for the highest percentage!
4
Check to finish. Tap Check when satisfied. Earn 1-3 stars based on your match: 70%=★, 85%=★★, 95%=★★★. Use Undo if you make a mistake.
