How to Play

1. The grid is divided into tanks (cages) shown by thick borders.

2. Numbers on the left tell you how many water cells each row must have.

3. Inside each tank the water is flat: if a cell holds water, every cell of that tank below it is also water.

4. Tap a cell to fill/empty it. Long-press (or right-click) to mark a cell with X (definitely empty).

5. Match every row's clue to win.

💧 Welcome to Aquarium

A calm but deep logic puzzle. Here is the one rule that matters most:

  1. Tanks are the regions bounded by thick borders.
  2. Fill cells with water so every row has the count shown on its left clue.
  3. Inside a tank, water must be flat — lower cells fill first, and the surface is level across the tank.
  4. That single rule chains into surprisingly rich deductions. Take your time.
