What Is Duck Merge?

Duck Merge is a delightful merge-style puzzle game where cute, colorful ducks fall onto a board and you combine two of the same kind into a bigger, better duck. The goal: keep the board from filling up while racking up as many merges as possible.

It borrows the best mechanics from classic match-3 games (think Candy Crush or Suika Game) and wraps them in a charming duck theme. The result is a game that's instantly accessible but offers real strategic depth the deeper you go.

How to Play Duck Merge

Duck Merge is one of the easiest games to pick up on the web. Here's the full loop:

  1. Ducks appear on the board. New ducks drop in one at a time.
  2. Find matching ducks. Two ducks of the same type can merge.
  3. Tap or click to merge. Two matching ducks become a bigger duck and score points.
  4. Keep the board clear. The game ends when the board fills up with no possible merges.
  5. Chain merges. Bigger merges can create room for more merges — chain bonuses add up fast.
  6. Beat your high score. Each session is short enough for one more run.

Tips to Score Higher in Duck Merge

  1. Plan your merges from the bottom up. Don't let the board fill up at the top.
  2. Don't panic-merge. Sometimes waiting for a better chain is worth more.
  3. Watch the upcoming duck. The "next" indicator lets you plan ahead.
  4. Corner stack. Use the corners to keep ducks organized by type.
  5. Prioritize clearing space. An empty space is more valuable than a low merge.
  6. Practice the rhythm. The fastest players merge as soon as ducks touch, no hesitation.
