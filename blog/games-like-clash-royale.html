Tips for Winning Browser Card Battles

  1. Track what's been played - In Crazy Eights and Spades, memory wins games
  2. Save your special cards - Don't burn that Skip or Reverse too early
  3. Watch the discards - Opponents' discards tell you what they can't play
  4. Position before attacking - Tower Defense wins come from lane control, not brute force
  5. Practice one game at a time - Master Crazy Eights, then move to Spades, then Rummy
