What Is Kitty's Café?

Kitty's Café is a relaxing merge-and-serve game. You start with a small café and merge pairs of identical cats to discover new, rarer breeds. Each cat has a personality — some are sleepy, some are energetic — and they all help you serve customers who wander in looking for coffee and treats.

The core loop is satisfying: merge → earn coins → upgrade your café → attract fancier cats → merge more. It's the kind of game you can pick up for five minutes or lose an hour in without noticing.

How to Play Kitty's Café

The game is simple to learn but rewards long-term play:

  1. Merge cats — Drag one cat onto an identical cat to merge them into a rarer breed. Higher-tier cats earn you more coins per customer.
  2. Serve customers — Tap on cat customers as they arrive to fulfill their drink orders before their patience runs out.
  3. Earn coins — Successful orders earn coins that you spend on café upgrades: better seats, fancier drinks, new decorations.
  4. Expand your café — More upgrades attract higher-paying customers with more exotic cat breeds.
  5. Unlock all cat breeds — There are dozens of cat varieties to discover through merging. Each new breed is a small dopamine hit.
