Color Sort Tips & Strategy

  1. Work backwards - Think about what the final state should look like, then plan moves.
  2. Keep tubes open - Always maintain at least one empty tube as buffer space.
  3. Don't scatter colors - Try to consolidate same colors rather than spreading them.
  4. Undo is your friend - If a move doesn't work out, undo and try another path.
  5. Take your time - There's no timer in most color sort games. Think carefully!
