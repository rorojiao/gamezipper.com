5 Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

  1. Learn touch typing. Use all ten fingers without looking at the keyboard. This is the single biggest speed booster.
  2. Focus on accuracy first. Speed comes naturally when you make fewer mistakes. Aim for 95%+ accuracy before pushing speed.
  3. Practice daily. Even 10 minutes a day with our typing game builds muscle memory fast.
  4. Sit up straight. Good posture reduces fatigue and helps you type longer without discomfort.
  5. Use the right keyboard. Mechanical keyboards with tactile feedback can improve both speed and accuracy.
