How to Play Offline on a Plane

Follow these steps before your flight departs:

  1. While online: Visit gamezipper.com and open 2-3 games you want to play.
  2. Let them load: Wait a few seconds for each game to fully load in your browser.
  3. Keep the tab open: Don't close the browser or the tabs.
