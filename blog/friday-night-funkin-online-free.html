What Is Friday Night Funkin?

Friday Night Funkin is an open-source rhythm game created by ninjamuffin99. You play as Boyfriend, who must rap-battle against a colorful cast of characters to impress Girlfriend's dad (and survive her family). The gameplay is simple but addictive: arrow keys fly up the screen and you need to press them in time with the music.

Since its release, FNF has become a massive internet phenomenon with thousands of mods, fan songs, and a dedicated community. The original game features multiple "weeks" (levels), each with unique opponents and tracks.

How to Play Friday Night Funkin Online

Playing FNF online is simple:

  1. Visit the game page — no download needed
  2. Use arrow keys (or WASD) to hit notes as they scroll up
  3. Match the rhythm — hit the right direction at the right time

Controls: Arrow keys or WASD for notes. Enter to confirm, Escape to pause.
