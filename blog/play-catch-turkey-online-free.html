What Is Catch Turkey?

Catch Turkey is a free online reaction game that runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups, no Flash required — just click (or tap on mobile) and start playing. The game challenges your hand-eye coordination as turkeys pop up across the screen and you need to click them before they disappear.

How to Play Catch Turkey

  • Desktop: Click on each turkey as it appears on screen.
  • Mobile: Tap the turkeys with your finger.
  • Turkeys appear faster as you progress — stay sharp!
  • Missing too many turkeys ends the round. Aim for the highest score.

Catch Turkey Tips & Strategy

  • Stay near the center of the screen — turkeys can spawn in any direction and the center gives you the best reach.
  • Don't chase missed turkeys — if one escapes, focus on the next spawn instead of looking back.
  • Use your peripheral vision — watch the whole screen, not just one spot.
  • Warm up first — your first few rounds are practice. Real scores come after you're in the zone.
