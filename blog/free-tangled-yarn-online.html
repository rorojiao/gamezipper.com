What Is Tangled Yarn (Twisted Tangle)?

Tangled yarn — sometimes called Twisted Tangle or yarn untangle — is a calm logic puzzle where you sort and untangle colored threads tangled across pegs. Every thread crosses several others, and the only thing you can do is pull one end at a time. The goal: arrange each thread so it runs cleanly from its starting peg to its matching colored peg, without crossing any other thread.

The concept became wildly popular in mobile puzzle games around 2023 and exploded in 2025-2026 as a search term — especially for tangled yarn online free, untangle puzzle unblocked, and twisted tangle no download. It's the perfect game for people who like Sudoku, color sort, or nonogram but want something more visual and tactile.

How to Play Tangled Yarn

The rules are simple to learn, surprisingly tricky to master. Here's how it works:

  1. Look at the colored pegs. Each thread has a start peg and an end peg of the same color.
  2. Drag a thread end. Pull the free end of a colored thread toward its matching peg.
  3. Don't cross other threads. If two threads would overlap, you have to plan a different route.
  4. Work one color at a time. Most players finish all blue threads, then red, then yellow — it reduces visual chaos.
  5. Start with corner pegs. Threads anchored at the corners usually have the shortest path.
  6. Use undo. If you paint yourself into a corner, undo and try a different order.

Tips for Solving Tangled Yarn Faster

  1. Count crossings. Each thread must cross exactly N other threads. Find the ones with the fewest crossings first.
  2. Start from corners. Threads pinned at the corners typically need to be routed along the border — least interference.
  3. Finish one color at a time. Don't try to solve blue and red simultaneously — your brain will melt.
  4. Look for loops. If a thread forms a closed loop with itself, it's usually a sign you need to back up two moves.
  5. Use the undo button aggressively. Most beginner players solve 80% of a level, get stuck, and restart. Pros use undo to back out of one move at a time.
  6. Don't be afraid to restart. Some levels have multiple valid solutions, and a fresh perspective sometimes solves in seconds what an hour of trying didn't.
