How to Play Bubble Shooter

The core mechanics are easy to learn:

  • Aim your bubble at the cluster of bubbles at the top of the screen.
  • Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop.
  • Use wall bounces — bounce shots off the walls to reach tricky spots.
  • Clear all bubbles to advance to the next level.
  • Don't let bubbles reach the bottom — or it's game over!

Bubble Shooter Tips & Tricks

  1. Aim for the top — clearing bubbles near the top can cause chain reactions that clear large sections.
  2. Use wall bounces — bouncing off walls lets you reach bubbles you can't hit directly.
  3. Plan ahead — look at your next bubble (usually shown in the launcher) and plan two moves ahead.
  4. Create drops — if you pop bubbles that are holding up other bubbles, everything below drops for bonus points.
  5. Don't rush — most bubble shooters don't have a timer. Take your time to find the best shot.
