Block puzzle games are one of the most satisfying puzzle genres ever created. The concept is simple: place shaped blocks on a grid, complete rows and columns to clear them, and try not to run out of space. Here's your guide to the best free block puzzle games online.

How to Play Block Puzzle

The rules take seconds to learn:

  • You get 3 blocks at a time in different shapes (like Tetris pieces).
  • Drag each block onto the 10×10 grid to place it.
  • Complete a full row or column to clear it and score points.
  • The game ends when you can't fit any remaining blocks on the board.
  • Combos — clear multiple lines at once for bonus points!

Block Puzzle Strategy Tips

  1. Keep the board balanced — don't let one side fill up while the other is empty.
  2. Think in threes — you always see 3 pieces ahead. Plan where all three will go before placing the first one.
  3. Avoid isolated gaps — single empty cells surrounded by filled cells are almost impossible to use.
  4. Go for combos — clearing 2+ lines at once gives bonus points and frees more space.
  5. Save space for large pieces — the 3×3 square and long bars need lots of room. Don't fill the center completely.
  6. Edges are your friend — place blocks along edges where they're less likely to create trapped gaps.
