🎮 How to Play Tetris Online Free

No sign-up, no payment, no downloads. Just open your browser, load the game, and start playing. Use your arrow keys (or tap on mobile) to move and rotate the falling tetromino blocks. Fill complete horizontal rows to clear them and score points. The game ends when the blocks stack up to the top of the screen.

🎯 Tetris Rules — Quick Guide

  • Goal: Complete horizontal rows with falling blocks to clear them
  • Controls: Left/Right arrows to move, Up to rotate, Down to soft drop
  • Scoring: More rows cleared at once = higher points ( Tetris = 4 rows at once)
  • Speed: Levels up as you clear more rows — faster falling blocks
  • Game Over: When new blocks can't spawn without overlapping

🕹️ Classic Tetris vs. Modern Variations

While the original Tetris by Alexey Pajitnov set the standard, dozens of variations exist online today. Some add power-ups, others change the physics. But the core appeal remains the same: simple rules, infinite replayability, and that addictive "just one more game" feeling.
