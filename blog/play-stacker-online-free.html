What Is Stacker?

Stacker is a precision timing game where blocks swing back and forth and you must drop them at exactly the right moment. Each block needs to land perfectly on the one below it. Miss the center, and the overhanging part breaks off — making the next layer even smaller.

It's the digital version of the classic Jenga concept: every mistake makes the next move harder. The tension builds with every layer, making Stacker one of the most nerve-wracking casual games you can play.

How to Play

  • Watch the block swing left and right above the stack
  • Click or tap to drop it at the perfect moment
  • Perfect alignment keeps the block full width
  • Imperfect alignment shaves off the overhanging part
  • Stack as high as you can! Game over when your block is too narrow

Stacker Tips for Higher Scores

  • Don't overthink — trust your instincts and tap when it looks right
  • Focus on the edges — align the sides, not the center
  • Stay calm — the higher you go, the more nerve-wracking it gets
  • Watch the rhythm — the swing is predictable. Find the beat.
  • Accept imperfection — a near-perfect stack is fine. Only perfect stacks keep full width.
