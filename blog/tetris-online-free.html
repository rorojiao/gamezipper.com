Tetris Strategy Tips for Higher Scores

  1. Keep the board flat — Avoid creating deep wells or gaps that are hard to fill.
  2. Save the I-piece — The long straight piece is perfect for clearing 4 lines at once. Save it for maximum points.
  3. Think ahead — Don't just place the current piece. Look at the next one too and plan your layout.
  4. Use both rotation directions — Learn to rotate both clockwise and counter-clockwise for faster placement.
  5. Practice T-spins — Advanced techniques like T-spinning can dramatically increase your score.
