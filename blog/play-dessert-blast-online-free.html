What Is Dessert Blast?

Dessert Blast is a classic match-3 puzzle game where you swap adjacent desserts on a grid to create matches of 3 or more identical items. Matched desserts explode, new ones fall from above, and chain reactions can create massive combos and bonus points.

Each level has specific goals — reach a target score, clear certain tiles, or collect specific desserts. As you advance, levels get harder with more obstacle tiles, limited moves, and complex targets.

How to Play Dessert Blast

  1. Swap adjacent desserts by clicking or tapping two adjacent items
  2. Match 3 or more identical desserts in a row or column
  3. Create special desserts by matching 4+ items (line clears, bomb desserts, etc.)
  4. Complete level goals within the move limit to advance
  5. Use combos and cascades to maximize your score and clear tricky boards

Best Dessert Blast Tips & Tricks

  • Plan your swaps ahead — look at the whole board before moving
  • Create special desserts — matching 4 in a line creates a row-clearing dessert; matching 5 in a T or L shape creates a bomb
  • Focus on cascades — one good move can trigger multiple chain reactions
  • Prioritize level goals — don't just go for high scores, target the specific goals first
