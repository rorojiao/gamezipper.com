Why Are These Games So Addictive?

All of these games share key design elements that trigger our brain's reward systems:

  • Short feedback loops: Every action gives immediate visual/audio feedback. Click, clear, boom — satisfaction instant.
  • Easy to learn, hard to master: You understand the core mechanic in seconds but can spend lifetimes perfecting it.
  • Procedural variation: Each round/game is different. You never quite know what's coming next.
  • High score pressure: Personal bests create self-competition. "Can I beat my score?" is the ultimate infinite loop.
  • Zero commitment: Each session is self-contained. You can stop anytime — but you won't.
