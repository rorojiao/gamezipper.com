What Are Clicker Games?

Clicker games (also called idle games or incremental games) are a genre where you tap or click to earn points, coins, or resources. As you progress, you unlock upgrades that automate clicking or multiply your earnings. The satisfying loop of "click → earn → upgrade → earn more" keeps millions of players hooked.
