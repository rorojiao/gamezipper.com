Sudoku is the gold standard for logic-based math puzzles. Fill in the 9x9 grid so every row, column, and 3x3 box contains the digits 1 through 9. No arithmetic required — pure logical deduction. Great for building systematic thinking and patience. Multiple difficulty levels from beginner to expert.
