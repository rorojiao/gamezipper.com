Tips for a High Score

  • Start slow: Don't rush at the beginning. Learn the board layout.
  • Corner control: Keep your snake in safe zones, avoid getting trapped.
  • Don't chase the food: Let it come to you when you're in a safe position.
  • Use the edges: Walls are useful — they can't hurt you if you hug them.
  • Practice consistently: High scores come from playing often.
