🧘 Why Games Help With Stress

Research in cyberpsychology shows that casual gaming for just 10-15 minutes can significantly reduce cortisol (the stress hormone). The key is choosing games that are:

  • Low stakes — no competitive pressure or time limits
  • Satisfying — visual and audio feedback that feels rewarding
  • Simple — easy to understand so your brain can rest
  • Repetitive — rhythmic actions that promote a flow state

All the games below fit these criteria perfectly.
