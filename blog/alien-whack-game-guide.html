👾 Alien Whack — Free Space Reflex Game Guide

Learn how to play, score high, and master the combo system in Alien Whack

What Is Alien Whack?

Alien Whack is a fast-paced space-themed reflex arcade game where aliens pop up from craters across your screen and you have to tap (or click) them before they disappear. You have exactly 60 seconds per round to score as many points as possible. It's the space version of the classic Whack-a-Mole game — but with alien characters, combo multipliers, and a deep space atmosphere.

Quick Facts: 60-second rounds · 3 alien types · Combo system · Works on desktop & mobile · 100% free

🎯 How to Play Alien Whack

Objective

Your goal is simple: whack as many aliens as possible during the 60-second round. The more aliens you hit, the higher your score. But it's not just about speed — you also need to be strategic about which aliens you target.

Alien Types & Scoring

  • 🟢 Green Aliens — Worth 1 point. These are the most common. Hit them to keep your combo going.
  • 🟡 Gold Aliens — Worth 3 points. These are rarer and should be your priority whenever they appear.
  • 🔴 Red Bombs — Cost you 2 points and break your combo. Avoid tapping these at all costs.

The Combo System

Every time you hit an alien (green or gold) without missing or hitting a bomb, your combo bar fills up. When the combo bar is full, your next hits earn a combo multiplier — giving you bonus points on top of the base score. This is the key to getting a really high score. A single combo streak can double or triple your points!

Controls

On desktop: Click the aliens with your mouse. On mobile: Tap the aliens with your finger. That's it — no complicated controls, just fast reflexes.

🏆 Tips & Strategies for High Scores

1. Prioritize Gold Aliens

Gold aliens are worth 3x more than green ones. When a gold alien appears, always go for it first. Don't waste time on a green alien if a gold one is nearby.

2. Ignore Bombs Completely

Bombs not only cost you 2 points, but they also reset your combo bar. Even if a gold alien is next to a bomb, tap the gold one and let the bomb be. The -2 penalty and combo break will cost you more than the gold alien was worth.

3. Keep Your Eyes Moving

Don't fixate on one area. Scan the entire play field. Aliens pop up randomly across all 9 craters. Peripheral vision helps — keep your gaze in the center and react to whatever pops up on the sides.

4. Use Two Thumbs on Mobile

If you're playing on a phone, use both thumbs. This gives you maximum coverage and faster reach to aliens on opposite sides of the screen.

5. Stay Calm as Speed Increases

Some versions gradually speed up as your score increases. Don't panic — focus on accuracy over speed. One missed alien is better than one bomb hit.

6. Aim for the Center

Position your cursor (or keep your dominant hand near the center of the screen). This minimizes travel time to aliens on all sides.
