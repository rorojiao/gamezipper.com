How to Play Snake

The controls are simple:

  • Arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to change direction
  • Eat the food to grow longer and earn points
  • Don't hit the walls or your own tail!
  • The game gets harder as your snake grows longer

Snake Game Tips for High Scores

  1. Stay near the center — you have more escape routes when the snake gets long.
  2. Plan ahead — always know where you'll go 3-4 moves from now.
  3. Use the zigzag pattern — fill the screen in a back-and-forth pattern for maximum length.
  4. Don't chase food — let food come to you when possible to avoid risky paths.
