12 Best Slither.io Alternatives — Free Online Multiplayer Snake Games

April 5, 2026 · 6 min read

Slither.io is one of the most popular browser games ever — millions of people play it daily. But sometimes you want something different, or Slither.io is blocked on your network, or you're just curious what else is out there.

This guide covers 12 of the best free Slither.io alternatives — games that capture that same addictive multiplayer snake gameplay, plus some that take the concept in interesting new directions.

🎮 The Closest Slither.io Alternatives

1. Wormax.io

MultiplayerSnakeMost Similar

Similarity: 95%

Wormax.io is arguably the closest thing to Slither.io. Same core mechanic — grow your worm by eating pellets, avoid other worms, try to make them crash into you. Wormax has slightly different graphics, power-ups, and a few unique game modes. A solid choice when Slither.io is unavailable.

Platform: Browser (HTML5)
Multiplayer: Yes, real players
Download: Not needed

2. Wormate.io

MultiplayerSnakeGreat Graphics

Similarity: 90%

Wormate.io is a well-designed multiplayer snake game with beautiful graphics and smooth gameplay. It has multiple arenas, different game modes (including a Battle Royale style), and regular updates. A bit more polished than Slither.io in some ways.

Platform: Browser (HTML5)
Multiplayer: Yes, real players
Download: Not needed

3. Deeeep.io

MultiplayerOcean ThemeEvolution

Similarity: 75%

Deeeep.io takes the .io formula and puts you in the ocean. You start as a small fish and evolve by eating other creatures. The food chain mechanic is deeper than Slither.io — bigger fish eat smaller fish, but you can team up. Ocean-themed, beautiful, and surprisingly strategic.

Platform: Browser (HTML5)
Multiplayer: Yes, real players
Download: Not needed

4. Mope.io

MultiplayerAnimal EvolutionClassic

Similarity: 70%

Mope.io is one of the original .io games and still going strong. Start as a tiny mouse and evolve up the food chain to become a dragon. Like Slither.io but in a complete ecosystem where everything eats something and gets eaten by something else.

Platform: Browser (HTML5)
Multiplayer: Yes, real players
Download: Not needed

🌟 Other .io Games Worth Trying

8. Agar.io

.io GameMultiplayerOriginal

Similarity: 65%

The original .io game that started everything. You control a cell in a petri dish, eat smaller cells to grow, avoid bigger ones. Different theme than Slither.io (microscopic instead of snake) but same core "grow big, avoid death" loop. A classic for a reason.

9. Diep.io

.io GameTank BattleMultiplayer

Similarity: 50%

Diep.io replaces the snake with a tank. You drive around, shoot shapes to destroy them, level up, and upgrade your tank's abilities. If you want the Slither.io "grow and compete" loop but with shooting mechanics instead of collision-based gameplay, this is for you.

10. Surviv.io

Battle Royale.io GameMultiplayer

Similarity: 40%

Surviv.io is a top-down 2D battle royale. 50 players drop onto an island, find weapons, and fight to be the last one standing. Each round is 5-10 minutes. If you want the competition and survival tension of an .io game but with shooting combat instead of snake mechanics, this is great.

11. Paper.io

.io GameTerritoryStrategy

Similarity: 60%

Paper.io flips the script — instead of eating to grow, you claim territory by drawing shapes. Claim the most area without getting cut off to win. Sounds simple but requires serious strategic thinking. A unique twist on the .io formula that deserves more attention.

12. Limbo.io

.io GameAtmosphericMultiplayer

Similarity: 45%

Limbo.io is a dark, atmospheric multiplayer game where you control a shadow creature in a shadowy world. More atmospheric and less action-focused than Slither.io, but deeply engaging if you want something different. The art direction is genuinely impressive for a browser game.

Wormax.io and Wormate.io are the closest clones. Wormax.io especially is nearly identical to Slither.io but with slightly different graphics and power-ups.
