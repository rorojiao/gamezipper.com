🎮 Brick Breaker Basics

The concept is simple: move your paddle to bounce a ball and destroy all the bricks on screen. But mastering it requires understanding angles, speed control, and power-up management.

Controls

  • Desktop: Move your mouse left and right to control the paddle
  • Mobile: Touch and drag to move the paddle
  • Tip: The ball launches when you click or tap

💡 Essential Tips for Beginners

1. Watch the Ball, Not the Paddle

This is the #1 mistake new players make. Your eyes should follow the ball at all times. Your hand will naturally adjust the paddle position without you staring at it.

2. Learn the Angle System

Where the ball hits your paddle determines its bounce direction:

  • Center hit: Ball goes straight up
  • Left edge: Ball bounces to the left at a sharp angle
  • Right edge: Ball bounces to the right at a sharp angle

Use edge hits to reach bricks hiding in corners and along the sides.

3. Clear Top Bricks First

When possible, aim for the upper rows of bricks. As you clear bricks from the top, the ball has more room to bounce around and hit multiple bricks in a single pass. This creates a cascading effect that clears levels faster.

🏆 Advanced Strategies

The Wall Bounce Technique

Bouncing the ball off the side walls lets you reach bricks that are difficult to hit from below. Aim for the wall at a shallow angle to send the ball skimming across the top row of bricks.

Power-Up Priority

Not all power-ups are created equal. Here's the priority order:

  1. Multi-ball: Three balls means triple the destruction. Always prioritize this.
  2. Fireball: Destroys bricks without bouncing, great for clearing tough rows.
  3. Wider Paddle: Makes it easier to catch the ball, reducing the risk of losing a life.
  4. Extra Life: Always grab these - they're your safety net.

Stay Calm Under Pressure

When the ball is moving fast and bricks are piling up, resist the urge to make frantic movements. Small, controlled paddle adjustments are more effective than big sweeps.

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Chasing power-ups blindly: Don't risk losing a ball just to catch a power-up. Keep the ball in play first.
  • Ignoring the edges: Side bricks often get left behind. Use wall bounces to clear them early.
  • Playing too fast: There's no time limit. Take your time to line up good angles.
  • Not using the whole paddle: The edges are your best weapons for angled shots.

📈 How to Get a High Score

High scores in Brick Breaker come from consistency, not luck:

  1. Never miss the ball - each life is precious
  2. Maximize multi-ball time - with 3 balls active, your score triples
  3. Clear every brick - bonus points for completing levels
  4. Catch every extra life - longer games = higher scores
