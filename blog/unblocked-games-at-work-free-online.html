❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can my employer see what games I'm playing?

If you're playing games on a work device using a work network, your IT department may monitor activity. To minimize risk, use short sessions during legitimate breaks and avoid games with large data transfers. These games run entirely in-browser with minimal network footprint.

Do these games require downloads or installs?

No. All games listed here are pure HTML5 browser games — no downloads, no installs, no plugins like Flash needed. They load and run instantly.

Will these games get me in trouble at work?

That depends on your workplace policies. We recommend playing only during scheduled breaks and keeping sessions short (5-10 minutes). Always respect your company's IT policies.

What games work best on a work computer?
