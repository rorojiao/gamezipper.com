📊 Average Reaction Times by Age

  • Teens (13-19): ~180-200ms
  • Young adults (20-30): ~200-220ms
  • Adults (30-40): ~220-250ms
  • Adults (40-50): ~250-280ms
  • Adults (60+): ~280-350ms

Note: These are averages for simple visual reaction time. With gaming practice, you can perform well above your age group's average!

🧠 How to Improve Your Reaction Time

  • Play fast-paced games daily — even 10 minutes helps
  • Stay hydrated — dehydration slows reaction time by up to 25%
  • Get enough sleep — poor sleep adds 50-100ms to your reaction time
  • Practice specific games — Whack-a-Mole and Snake are great starters
  • Warm up your hands — stretching improves blood flow and speed
