Best Games to Play on a Plane ✈️ (No WiFi Needed)

Updated April 6, 2026 · 4 min read

Eight hours at 35,000 feet with a dead phone battery and no in-flight entertainment? Every traveler's nightmare. The solution: browser games that work completely offline after the first load. Load your favorite game before boarding, and you'll have entertainment for the entire flight — no WiFi, no data plan, no problem.

🎮 Why Browser Games Are Perfect for Flights

Once loaded, modern HTML5 games run entirely in your browser without an internet connection. The game logic, graphics, and audio are all processed locally on your device. After the initial page load on airport WiFi, you can put your phone in airplane mode and play for hours.

💡 Tips for Playing Games on Planes

  • Load before boarding — Open the game on airport WiFi before you board so it's cached
  • Use airplane mode — Save battery and avoid distractions
  • Download audio packs — Some games let you preload sounds for offline use
  • Bring a battery bank — Long flights drain batteries fast, especially with screen time
  • Use headphones — Keep sound on without disturbing neighbors

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do these games work in airplane mode?

Yes. After loading the page once with internet, games run fully offline in airplane mode. Just open the game before boarding while connected to WiFi.

Do I need to download anything?

No downloads required. All games are pure HTML5 that run directly in your browser.

Will playing games drain my battery fast?

Browser games are generally battery-efficient. Put your phone in airplane mode to save battery while playing. A fully charged phone should last 6-8 hours of casual gaming.
