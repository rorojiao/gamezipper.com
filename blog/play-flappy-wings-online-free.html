What Is Flappy Wings?

Flappy Wings is a free browser game inspired by the viral hit Flappy Bird. You control a tiny bird flying through an endless series of pipe gaps. One tap = one flap. Hit a pipe and it's game over. Simple to learn, nearly impossible to master.

The original Flappy Bird was taken down from app stores in 2014, but the craving for that exact gameplay never went away. Flappy Wings recreates the experience — and you don't even need to download anything.

How to Play

  • Click, tap, or press Space to make the bird flap upward
  • Navigate through pipe gaps — each gap you pass = 1 point
  • Don't hit the pipes or the ground — one collision ends the game
  • Gravity pulls you down — keep tapping to stay airborne
  • Beat your high score — that's the whole point!

Why Flappy Wings Is So Addictive

  • Instant restart — died? One click and you're flying again
  • "One more try" loop — you always feel like the next run will be your best
  • Pure skill-based — no luck, no power-ups, just your timing
  • Quick sessions — each run lasts seconds, perfect for short breaks

Tips to Get a Higher Score

  • Stay in the middle — keep the bird at roughly pipe-gap height between gaps
  • Use short, quick taps — don't hold, just quick rhythmic taps
  • Focus on the gap, not the bird — look ahead at where you need to be
  • Don't overcorrect — small adjustments beat wild movements
  • Warm up — your first few runs are warm-ups. The real game starts after
