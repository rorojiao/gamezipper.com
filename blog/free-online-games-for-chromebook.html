How to Get the Best Gaming Experience on Chromebook

Use Chrome browser (obviously) — Games run best in Chrome, which is the default browser on Chrome OS. No setup needed.

Enable hardware acceleration — Go to chrome://settings and search for "hardware acceleration" to make sure it's enabled for smoother game performance.

Bookmark your favorites — Press Ctrl+D to bookmark games for instant access anytime. You can even pin tabs for games you play daily.

Use fullscreen mode — Press F11 to go fullscreen for a more immersive gaming experience on your Chromebook.
