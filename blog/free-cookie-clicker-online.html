What Is Cookie Clicker?

Cookie Clicker is the game that defined the entire "incremental" or "idle" genre. Released by French programmer Orteil in 2013, it started as a one-joke browser experiment and turned into a cultural phenomenon — the template for thousands of idle games that followed.

The premise is dead simple: click a giant cookie to bake more cookies. Then spend those cookies on buildings — cursors, grandmas, farms, factories, banks — that automatically bake cookies for you. Before you know it, you're producing billions of cookies per second and the entire universe is being converted into cookie dough.

How to Play Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is the rare game that genuinely plays itself after the first 5 minutes. Here's the loop:

  1. Click the big cookie — every click bakes one (or more) cookies.
  2. Buy a Cursor — your first auto-clicker.
  3. Buy a Grandma — the iconic Cookie Clicker upgrade.
  4. Buy more buildings — farms, factories, banks, temples, wizard towers.
  5. Unlock upgrades — each tier has special upgrades that multiply production.
  6. Click golden cookies — these rare spawns give massive temporary boosts.
  7. Prestige when ready — ascend to a new dimension with permanent bonuses.
