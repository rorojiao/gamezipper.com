What is Cloud & Sheep?

Cloud & Sheep is a casual simulation game where you play as a shepherd caring for an adorable flock of sheep. The gameplay revolves around the weather — literally! Drag fluffy clouds across the sky to create rain over patches of dirt, turning them into lush green grass for your sheep to graze on.

It's the perfect game for anyone who enjoys cozy, low-stress gameplay — similar in vibe to Kitty Cafe or idle clicker games, but with a focus on nurturing and care.
