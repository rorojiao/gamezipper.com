What Is Color Sort Puzzle?

Color Sort is a sorting puzzle where you have several tubes filled with colored balls (or water, depending on the variant), and your job is to sort them so each tube contains only one color. The catch: you can only move the top ball of any tube, and you can only pour it onto a same-color ball or into an empty tube.

It sounds trivially simple, but the levels ramp up fast. By level 10, you're juggling 8 tubes and 6 colors with maybe 2 empty tubes to work with. By level 30, you're planning 5–6 moves ahead, undoing mistakes, and trying not to fill the last empty tube with mixed colors (a classic Color Sort mistake).

How to Play Color Sort

The rules fit in two sentences. Mastering them takes longer than you'd think:

  1. Tap a tube — The top colored ball will lift out, ready to pour.
  2. Tap another tube — The ball pours in. You can only pour onto a same-color ball, or into an empty tube.
  3. Complete a tube — Fill a tube with 4 balls of the same color to clear it (or one full set in variants with more colors per tube).
  4. Win the level — When every tube contains only one color, you win.
  5. Use undo if you get stuck — GameZipper's Color Sort has an unlimited undo button, so there's no penalty for experimentation.
