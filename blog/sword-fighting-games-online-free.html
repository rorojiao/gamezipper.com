Sword Fighting Game Tips

Want to dominate in sword fighting games? Keep these strategies in mind:

  1. Learn enemy patterns — Every enemy has tells before they attack. Watch for them.
  2. Don't button mash — Timed strikes do more damage than rapid clicking
  3. Use combos — Chain attacks together for massive bonus damage
  4. Keep moving — A stationary target is a dead target
  5. Learn to dodge — Sometimes the best offense is not getting hit
