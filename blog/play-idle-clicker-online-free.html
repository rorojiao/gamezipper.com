What Is Idle Clicker?

Idle Clicker is a browser-based incremental game where you start by clicking to earn points. Use those points to buy upgrades that earn points automatically. Then upgrade those upgrades. Before you know it, you're earning millions per second — and it feels amazing.

Idle games (also called clicker games) became a phenomenon with Cookie Clicker in 2013. The formula is simple but incredibly satisfying: click → earn → upgrade → earn more → repeat.

How to Play

  • Click the button to earn points
  • Buy upgrades with your points to increase click value
  • Purchase auto-clickers that earn points without clicking
  • Upgrade everything — each upgrade multiplies your earnings
  • Watch the numbers grow — that's the whole game, and it's perfect

Why Idle Games Are So Popular

  • Satisfying progression — constant visible progress, no matter what
  • Low commitment — play for 2 minutes or 2 hours
  • Strategic upgrades — deciding what to buy next is a mini puzzle
  • Background play — set up auto-clickers and check back later
  • Stress relief — simple, repetitive, meditative clicking

Idle Clicker Strategy Tips

  • Buy auto-clickers early — passive income beats active clicking long-term
  • Focus on multipliers — a 2x upgrade is usually better than a flat bonus
  • Don't hoard points — spent points earn more points. Invest early!
  • Come back later — idle games earn while you're away
  • Reset wisely — if there's a prestige system, use it when returns slow down
