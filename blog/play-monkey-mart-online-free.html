What Is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is a charming idle management game where you play a monkey who owns and operates a busy supermarket. You plant banana trees, harvest the fruit, stock the shelves, and expand your store aisle by aisle. As your supermarket grows, you hire other monkeys to help run it — and the bananas start flying off the shelves on their own.

It's the perfect blend of two genres that took over the casual gaming world: idle clickers and tycoon management. You start by manually tapping every banana, and within minutes you're overseeing a fully-automated banana empire that earns coins even while you're away.

How to Play Monkey Mart

Monkey Mart is one of the most intuitive idle games on the web. Here's the full loop:

  1. Plant banana trees — Start with one tree and watch it grow.
  2. Harvest bananas — Tap the tree when the bananas are ripe.
  3. Stock the shelves — Walk over to your supermarket and restock the displays.
  4. Serve customers — Monkey shoppers will walk in and buy your bananas.
  5. Earn coins — Every sale adds to your balance.
  6. Hire monkeys — Once you can afford it, monkeys will auto-run each station.
  7. Unlock new aisles — Expand into corn, strawberries, and more exotic produce.
  8. Upgrade everything — Speed, capacity, and unlock special items.
