Racing Game Tips for High Scores

  1. Warm up first - Play 2-3 easy rounds before going for a record
  2. Stay relaxed - Tension slows your reactions. Keep hands loose.
  3. Focus on rhythm - Most speed games have predictable patterns. Find the beat.
  4. Take breaks - Reaction time drops after 20 minutes. Rest and return.
  5. Use keyboard shortcuts - On desktop, keyboard is faster than mouse for most games.
