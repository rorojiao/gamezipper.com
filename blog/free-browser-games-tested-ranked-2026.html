Our Testing Methodology

Every game was played for at least 30 minutes on desktop (Chrome, 2026 MacBook) and 15 minutes on mobile (iPhone 14, Chrome iOS). We measured cold-load time with a 100Mbps connection. Replay value was assessed by asking: "Would I open this again tomorrow without being reminded?" Games were tested between March 28 – April 8, 2026. Individual scores were averaged across two testers.

What Didn't Make the List

We tested 80+ games total. About 30 were cut for: excessive ads (mid-roll video that can't be skipped), required sign-up to play, pay-to-win mechanics, or simply not fun enough to justify 5 minutes. If a game isn't on this list, we tried it and decided you should spend your time elsewhere.
