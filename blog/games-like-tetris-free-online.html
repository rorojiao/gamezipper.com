What Makes a Great Tetris Alternative?

Not every puzzle game captures the Tetris magic. Here's what to look for:

  • Spatial reasoning — you need to think about shapes and how they fit together
  • Increasing difficulty — the game should get harder as you improve
  • Clear feedback — satisfying visuals and sounds when you do well
  • Quick sessions — games should work in 2-5 minute bursts
  • "One more try" factor — you should always want to play again
