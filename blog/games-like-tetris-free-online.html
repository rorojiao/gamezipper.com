Why Browser Games Like Tetris Are So Addictive

Tetris-style games tap into something psychologists call the "Zeigarnik Effect" — our brain's desire to complete unfinished tasks. Every time you clear a line, place a block perfectly, or solve a puzzle, your brain gets a small dopamine hit. That's why "just one more game" turns into an hour.
