What Are Endless Runner Games?

Endless runner games are a genre where your character automatically moves forward and you must dodge obstacles, collect items, and survive as long as possible. The game gets progressively harder, testing your reflexes and concentration. There's no finish line - the goal is to beat your own high score!

Tips for High Scores in Endless Runner Games

  1. Stay calm - Panic leads to mistimed jumps. Focus on rhythm.
  2. Look ahead - Don't stare at your character. Watch what's coming next.
  3. Short sessions - Take breaks between attempts. Fatigue kills high scores.
  4. Learn patterns - Most runners have recurring obstacle patterns you can memorize.
  5. Use both hands - On mobile, find a comfortable grip that lets you tap quickly.
