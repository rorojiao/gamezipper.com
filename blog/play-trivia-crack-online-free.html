What Is Trivia Crack?

Trivia Crack (also known as "Preguntados" in Spanish-speaking countries) is one of the most popular quiz games of the last decade. Originally released in 2013, it became a global phenomenon because of one simple idea: test your knowledge across six universal categories, and let your friends do the same.

The categories are the six "Wheels of Knowledge": Geography, Entertainment, History, Art & Literature, Science & Technology, and Sports. Each round, you spin the wheel and answer a multiple-choice question. Get it right, earn a crown. Collect all six crowns to win.

How to Play Trivia Crack

Trivia Crack is one of the most accessible trivia games on the web. Here's the full loop:

  1. Spin the wheel — the wheel lands on one of six categories.
  2. Read the question — multiple choice, four possible answers.
  3. Pick your answer — you have a time limit (usually 30 seconds).
  4. Earn a crown — correct answers earn a crown for that category.
  5. Defend your categories — friends can challenge you in your weakest categories.
  6. Collect all 6 crowns — first to do so wins the round.
