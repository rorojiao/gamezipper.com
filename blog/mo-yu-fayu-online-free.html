How to Play 摸鱼发雨

Why This Typing Game Is Different

Most typing games feel like homework. 摸鱼发雨 feels like a reward. The rain effect gives you immediate visual feedback that makes fast typing genuinely satisfying. It's oddly meditative — the clicking of keys, the falling characters, the growing sense of accomplishment.

It's also a genuinely useful skill builder. Regular practice improves your Chinese typing speed, character recognition, and familiarity with common words. It's the rare game that's actually productive.

Typing Tips for Beginners

  1. Don't look at the keyboard — Train your muscle memory by keeping your eyes on the screen
  2. Start slow, accuracy first — Speed follows naturally once you're comfortable
  3. Use the correct finger positions — Each finger should cover specific keys to maximize efficiency
  4. Practice daily — Even 5 minutes a day makes a big difference over time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 摸鱼发雨 really free?

100% free, forever. No hidden costs, no energy systems, no ads interrupting your rain.

What's a good typing speed?

For English, 40-60 WPM is considered good. For Chinese typing, 40-60 characters per minute (CPM) is a solid intermediate level. But honestly, just have fun!

Does this help me learn Chinese?

Yes! Seeing characters repeatedly while typing them is one of the best ways to memorize them. It's like flashcards but more engaging.
