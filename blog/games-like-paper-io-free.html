What Makes Paper.io So Fun?

Paper.io combines simple controls with deep strategy. You move your character to draw lines, and when you complete a loop back to your territory, the enclosed area becomes yours. The risk-reward balance — venture further to claim more, but risk getting eliminated — creates incredibly tense moments.
