What Is Whack-a-Mole?

Whack-a-Mole is one of the most iconic arcade games ever created. Moles pop up randomly from holes, and your job is to tap or click them as fast as you can. The faster you react, the higher your score. It's a game of pure reflexes and reaction time.

The original arcade machine was invented in Japan in 1975 and has been a carnival and arcade staple ever since. Now you can play it online for free on any device — phone, tablet, or computer.

How to Play Whack-a-Mole Online

Playing is simple:

  • Click or tap the moles as they pop up from their holes
  • Be quick — moles disappear after a short time
  • Score points for every successful whack
  • Beat your high score — the game gets faster as you progress

The game tracks your reaction time and score, so you can always try to beat your personal best.

Why Play Whack-a-Mole?

Beyond pure fun, Whack-a-Mole is great for:

Tips for a Higher Score

  • Keep your mouse or finger centered over the play area
  • Watch for patterns — moles often appear near the last one
