How to Play Snake Game

Snake is incredibly simple to learn but challenging to master:

  1. Control the snake using arrow keys (desktop) or swipe/D-pad (mobile)
  2. Eat the food (red dots) to grow longer and earn points
  3. Avoid collisions — don't hit the walls or your own tail
  4. Beat your high score! The longer you survive, the higher your score

Snake Game Tips & Strategies

Want to score higher? Here are pro tips from experienced Snake players:

  • Stay near the center — it gives you more escape routes when the snake gets long
  • Follow a pattern — many top players use a zigzag or spiral pattern to cover the board systematically
  • Plan ahead — always think 2-3 moves in advance, especially once your snake is over 10 segments long
  • Don't rush — there's no time limit. Take your time and make deliberate moves
  • Use the edges wisely — hug the walls early when the snake is short, then move inward as it grows

Snake Game History

The Snake game concept dates back to the 1976 arcade game Blockade. However, it was Nokia's 1998 inclusion of Snake on the Nokia 6110 that made it a global phenomenon. Over 400 million people have played Snake on Nokia phones alone.

Today, Snake lives on as a browser game — faster, smoother, and available on every device. The core appeal hasn't changed: simple rules, addictive gameplay, and the eternal quest to beat your high score.
