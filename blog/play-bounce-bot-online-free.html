What Is Bounce Bot?

Bounce Bot is a browser-based platformer where you control a small robot that constantly bounces. Your job is to steer it left and right to land on platforms, avoid obstacles, and reach the goal. The constant bouncing adds a unique twist to classic platformer mechanics.

Unlike most platformers where you choose when to jump, Bounce Bot's robot bounces automatically. This creates a rhythm-based gameplay where timing your horizontal movement is the key skill.

How to Play

  • The bot bounces automatically — you control horizontal movement
  • Left/Right arrows or A/D keys to move
  • Land on platforms — missing means falling into the void
  • Avoid hazards — spikes, moving obstacles, and gaps
  • Reach the exit of each level to progress

Tips for New Players

  • Don't fight the bounce — work with the rhythm, not against it
  • Look ahead — plan your landing spots before you reach them
  • Small movements — gentle taps work better than holding keys
  • Learn the patterns — obstacle positions are consistent, so memorize them
