Typing Speed Tips

  1. Home row position - Keep your fingers on ASDF JKL; at all times.
  2. Don't look down - Train yourself to type without looking at the keyboard.
  3. Accuracy first - Speed comes naturally once accuracy is solid.
  4. Practice daily - Even 5-10 minutes daily beats one long weekly session.
  5. Relax your hands - Tension slows you down. Stay loose.
