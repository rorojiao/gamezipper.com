What Is Flappy Bird?

Flappy Bird was a mobile game created by Vietnamese developer Dong Nguyen in 2013. The concept was brilliantly simple: tap the screen to make a little bird flap its wings and fly through gaps between green pipes. One collision and it's game over. The game became a viral sensation in early 2014, topping app store charts worldwide before Nguyen famously removed it.
