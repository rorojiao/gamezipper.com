What Is Sushi Stack?

Sushi Stack is a food-themed puzzle game where you stack different types of sushi pieces — nigiri, maki, sashimi — to build the highest tower possible. Each piece has different properties, and matching the right pieces together earns bonus points.

Think of it as Tetris meets a sushi restaurant. The colorful food graphics make it visually appealing, and the stacking mechanics keep you coming back for "just one more round."

How to Play

  • Drag and drop sushi pieces onto the stack
  • Match similar types for bonus points
  • Keep the tower balanced — don't let it topple
  • Build as high as possible for the highest score
  • Unlock new sushi types as you progress

Why Food Games Are So Popular

  • Visual appeal — colorful food graphics are universally attractive

Tips for Higher Scores

  • Start with a wide base — stability matters more than height early on
  • Match before stacking — look for combos before placing
  • Keep it centered — balanced towers go higher
  • Use special pieces wisely — save power-up sushi for tricky spots
