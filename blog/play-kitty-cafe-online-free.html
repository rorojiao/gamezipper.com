What Is Kitty Cafe?

Kitty Cafe is a cafe simulation game where you're the barista serving a variety of cats. Each cat arrives with an order — you prepare the items, serve them, and earn tips. The cats react to your service: happy cats leave better tips, impatient cats leave if you take too long, and regulars become loyal customers over time.

It's one of those games that's easy to play but hard to master. On the surface, it's just clicking and serving. But as more cats arrive and orders get more complex, you'll need to prioritize and manage your time to keep everyone happy.

How to Play Kitty Cafe

  1. A cat arrives at your counter with an order (shown in a speech bubble)
  2. Prepare the order — click the right items from your menu (coffee, cakes, cookies, etc.)
  3. Serve the cat by placing the order in front of them
  4. Earn coins and tips based on speed and accuracy
  5. Upgrade your cafe with new drinks, pastries, and decorations as you earn
