Tips for Stealth Office Gaming

  • Use Alt+Tab — keep a spreadsheet open in another window for instant switching
  • Mute first — all games above work perfectly with sound off
  • Resize the window — games work in small windows, so you don't need fullscreen
  • Bookmarks bar — drag your favorite game link there for one-click access
  • No downloads needed — nothing shows up in your Downloads folder or IT logs
