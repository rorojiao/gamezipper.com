🔥 Slope Strategy: How to Beat Your High Score

1. Master the Gentle S-Curve

Don't make sharp movements. Gentle left-right corrections keep you centered and responsive. Sharp turns often lead to overshooting the edge.

2. Watch the Color of Obstacles

Obstacles appear as red barriers and yellow boost pads. Red stops your run — avoid them. Yellow can actually help you skip sections.

3. Use the Walls Strategically

The side walls are not just obstacles — skilled players use wall contact to slow down in tight sections before correcting direction.

4. Play in Short Bursts

Fatigue kills performance. Take a 10-second break between runs to reset your reaction time. This simple habit can double your average score.

5. Focus on the Middle Third

Most beginners hug one side. Pros play the middle third, giving maximum reaction room in both directions.
