How to Play Cookie Clicker

Step 1: Click the big cookie in the center of the screen. Each click earns cookies.

Step 2: Spend cookies on buildings — structures that automatically generate cookies per second (CPS).

Step 3: Unlock upgrades that make your buildings more efficient or give you special abilities.

Step 4: Chase achievements — milestones that unlock permanent bonuses.

The goal is to produce as many cookies per second as possible. There is no real "win" condition — just ever-escalating numbers until your browser can't handle them.

Golden Cookie Strategy

Golden cookies are the most powerful mechanic in Cookie Clicker. They appear randomly on screen and grant temporary bonuses:

  • Frenzy — 7x CPS for 77 seconds
  • Lucky — Instant cookies equal to 13% of your bank
  • Click Frenzy — 777x click power for 13 seconds
  • Elder Frenzy — 6x CPS for 6 seconds + you lose all golden cookies

Pro tip: Wait for a Frenzy, then pop a golden cookie during it for maximum effect. The combo of Frenzy + Lucky during a high-bank moment can jump you ahead by hours of production.

Best Upgrades to Prioritize
