📚 15 Fun Games to Play When Bored at School (Unblocked 2026)

Updated April 2026 • Unblocked • Works on Chromebooks

We've all been there — sitting in study hall or computer lab with nothing to do. Here are 15 free browser games that work on school computers, Chromebooks, and any device with a web browser. No downloads or signups needed.

🎮 Quick Games (5 Minutes)

1

Snake Game

The classic that works everywhere. Use arrow keys to eat and grow. Easy to pick up, hard to put down. Runs perfectly on any school computer.

Keyboard Classic
2

Whack-a-Mole

Click the moles as fast as you can! Pure reflexes, no thinking required. Perfect for a quick brain break between classes.

Mouse/Touch Quick
3

Flappy Wings

Tap or click to fly through pipes. You'll crash. A lot. But that's what makes it fun — trying to beat your (or your friend's) high score.

One Button Addictive
4

Stacker

Stack blocks as high as you can. Simple but requires precise timing. Great for competing with the person sitting next to you.

Timing High Score
5

Bounce Bot

Guide a bouncing robot through obstacles. Quick rounds, satisfying physics, and you look like you're doing homework from a distance.

Platformer Looks Subtle

🧠 Brain Games (Look Smart)

6

2048

Slide number tiles to combine them. Reach 2048 to win! Looks like math homework, so teachers won't suspect a thing.

Numbers Looks Like Work
7

Word Puzzle

Unscramble words and solve puzzles. Actually educational! Your English teacher would approve... probably.

Words Educational
8

Typing Speed Test

How fast can you type? This one is literally school-appropriate — it's typing practice! But also secretly fun and competitive.

Typing 100% School-OK
9

Color Sort

Sort colored liquids into matching containers. Calming and logical. Perfect when you need a stress break from that essay.

Logic Relaxing
10

Memory Match

Flip cards and find matching pairs. Improves memory (scientifically!) and kills time beautifully. Play multiple rounds to beat your record.

Memory All Ages

⚔️ Longer Games (Study Hall)

11

Brick Breaker

Break all the bricks with a bouncing ball. Levels get harder as you go. Perfect for filling a whole study hall period.

Arcade Levels
12

Idle Clicker

Click to earn, buy upgrades, earn more. You can even leave it running in a background tab while you do actual work.

Idle Background-Friendly
13

Glyph Quest

Rotate hexagonal runes to solve puzzles across 50 levels. Looks nerdy enough that nobody will question what you're doing.

Puzzle Deep
14

Wood Block Puzzle

Fit blocks into a grid, complete rows to clear them. Like Tetris but calmer. Great for long periods of free time.

Blocks Zen
15

Phantom Blade

Ninja action game with sword combos. For when you've finished all your work and just need to slash something. Fast-paced and exciting.

Action Combat

💡 Pro Tips

Ctrl+W — Instant tab close if a teacher walks by. Practice this reflex.

Alt+Tab — Switch to your homework tab instantly. Keep a Google Doc open as your "cover."

Volume down — All these games work without sound, but if you want audio, keep it low or use earbuds.

Bookmarks — Save your favorites for quick access between classes.
