What Is Wood Block Puzzle?

Wood Block Puzzle (also known as 1010 Block Puzzle or Qblock) is a relaxing yet challenging puzzle game inspired by Tetris but with a unique twist. Instead of falling blocks, you choose where to place each piece on a 10×10 grid. Fill an entire row or column to clear it and score points.

The game has become one of the most popular mobile puzzle games worldwide, with millions of players enjoying its simple but addictive gameplay. Now you can play it right in your browser — no download needed.

How to Play

  • Drag and drop wooden blocks onto the 10×10 grid
  • Fill a complete row or column to clear it and earn points
  • Plan ahead — you get 3 pieces at a time and must place all of them
  • Game over when you can't fit any remaining pieces on the board

Tips & Strategy for Higher Scores

  • Keep the center clear — it gives you the most placement options
  • Think in rows and columns — focus on completing lines rather than just fitting pieces
  • Don't leave isolated gaps — single empty cells are hard to fill later
  • Save space for large pieces — the 3×3 square and long bars need room
  • Combo clears — clearing multiple lines at once earns bonus points

Why People Love Wood Block Puzzle

  • No time pressure — play at your own pace, no ticking clock
  • Relaxing — the wooden aesthetic and calm gameplay reduce stress
  • Brain training — improves spatial reasoning and planning skills
  • Quick sessions — each game takes 2-5 minutes
