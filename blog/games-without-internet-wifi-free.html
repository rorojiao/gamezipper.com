💡 How to Play Browser Games Without Internet

Browser games (HTML5 games) are different from app store games. Here's why they work offline:

  1. Load the page while connected — The game downloads to your browser in seconds
  2. Game runs in JavaScript — No server needed for gameplay
  3. Keep the tab open — Don't close the browser tab
  4. Play as long as you want — Works until you close the tab

Pro tip: Before a flight or trip, open all the games you want to play in separate browser tabs while you have WiFi. They'll keep working offline!
