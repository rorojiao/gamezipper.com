Quick Arcade Games (1-3 minutes per round)

🐍 Snake Game

The classic that never gets old. Guide your snake, eat food, grow longer, and try not to crash. Simple, addictive, and perfect when you have a few minutes to kill. Works on desktop and mobile.

🐦 Flappy Wings

Tap to fly through pipes in this Flappy Bird-style game. Frustratingly fun and impossible to put down once you start. Great for quick stress relief during a boring afternoon.

🧱 Brick Breaker

Classic breakout-style game with power-ups. Smash all the bricks to advance through levels. The satisfying physics and power-up combos keep you coming back.

🔨 Whack-a-Mole

Test your reflexes by whacking moles as they pop up. Fast-paced and silly — perfect for a quick laugh when you're bored.

📦 Stacker

Stack blocks as precisely as you can. Each level gets faster and harder. A great test of timing and concentration.

Brain Teasers & Puzzles (5-15 minutes)

🔢 2048

Slide numbered tiles to merge them into 2048. A deceptively simple puzzle that has captivated millions. Easy to learn, hard to master.

🎨 Color Sort

Sort colored balls into tubes until each tube has only one color. Satisfying and calming — a great boredom killer that also exercises your brain.

🪵 Wood Block Puzzle

1010-style wooden block puzzle. Place blocks on the grid, fill rows and columns to clear them. Strategic and relaxing at the same time.

📝 Word Puzzle

Wordle-style word guessing game with unlimited plays. Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries. Perfect for word lovers and vocabulary builders.

🃏 Memory Match

Classic card-matching memory game. Flip cards and find matching pairs. Great for sharpening your memory and focus.

Casual & Relaxing Games (play as long as you want)

👆 Idle Clicker

Click to earn, buy upgrades, and watch your empire grow. The ultimate background game — you can even leave it running and check back later.

🐱 Kitty Cafe

Run your own cat cafe! Serve customers and collect adorable cats. Relaxing and wholesome — perfect when you need a mood boost.

🎨 Paint Splash

Create beautiful art by mixing and splashing colors. No skill needed — just tap and enjoy the satisfying visuals.

🍣 Sushi Stack

Stack sushi pieces in this delicious puzzle game. Cute, fun, and surprisingly strategic.

⌨️ Typing Speed Test

Test your typing speed and improve your WPM. Practical and fun — turns boredom into productivity.
