What Is Paint Splash?

Paint Splash is a free browser-based puzzle game that puts you in charge of filling a canvas with color. Each level presents a grid where you slide rows or columns of colored blocks until every cell is filled. Think of it as a sliding puzzle meets paint-by-numbers — except you're the one who creates the pattern.

Unlike traditional sliding puzzles that only have one solution, Paint Splash offers multiple approaches. You can prioritize filling rows, work column by column, or focus on getting the right colors in the right places. The game rewards creative thinking and planning ahead.

How to Play Paint Splash

  • Slide the blocks — Click or tap a row or column to shift all blocks in that direction
  • Fill every cell — Your goal is to cover the entire grid with colored paint
  • Watch the colors — Different colored blocks create patterns as they move
  • Plan ahead — Think a few moves ahead to avoid getting stuck
  • Complete the level — Once every cell is painted, you advance

Paint Splash Tips & Tricks

1. Start with the edges

Begin filling cells at the edges of the grid. It's easier to work inward from the boundaries than to try filling the center first and getting stuck.

2. Think in patterns, not moves

Don't just think "I'll slide this row left." Think about where the colored blocks will end up after 2-3 moves and how that helps you complete the puzzle.

3. Use the colors strategically

Some levels require specific colors to end up in specific places. When you see a challenging pattern, work backward from your goal.

4. Restart is not failure

If you get stuck, restart the level. Each restart teaches you something about how the blocks move. Many players solve levels on their second or third attempt after learning from a failed run.
