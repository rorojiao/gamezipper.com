Pac-Man Tips and Strategies

Whether you're playing Pac-Man or any maze chase game, these strategies will help you score higher:

  1. Learn the ghost patterns — Each ghost has unique behavior. Understanding their movement helps you predict and avoid them.
  2. Save power pellets — Don't grab power-ups immediately. Wait until ghosts are nearby for maximum points.
  3. Stay near tunnel exits — Tunnels are safe zones where ghosts slow down. Use them to escape tight situations.
  4. Clear corners first — Eat dots in the corners early when it's safer, then focus on the center paths.
  5. Watch the timer — In timed modes, plan a route that covers the most ground efficiently.
