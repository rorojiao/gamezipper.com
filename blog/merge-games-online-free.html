What Are Merge Games?

Merge games (also called combination games or synthesis games) are a subgenre of puzzle and idle games where you drag and drop matching items together to create higher-tier versions. Start with basic elements and work your way up through increasingly rare and valuable creations.

The genre exploded in popularity with mobile apps like 2048 and Merge Dragons, but you don't need an app store to enjoy them. These browser-based merge games work on any device — phone, tablet, or computer.
