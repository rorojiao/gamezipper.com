Tips for Night Gaming Without Ruining Your Sleep

  1. Set a timer — 30-45 minutes is the sweet spot before sleep. The "one more game" trap is real.
  2. Avoid competitive games before bed — PvP and ranked modes spike adrenaline. Stick to puzzle and casual games.
  3. Dim the screen — Use night mode or reduce brightness. Blue light makes it harder to fall asleep afterward.
  4. Choose idle/clicker games — They're engaging enough to beat boredom but low-key enough for winding down.
