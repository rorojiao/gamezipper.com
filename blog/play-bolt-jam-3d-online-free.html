What Is Bolt Jam 3D?

Bolt Jam 3D is a 3D screw puzzle game where metal plates are held together by bolts and screws. Your job is to figure out the right order to remove each bolt so that plates can separate and rings can be untangled. It sounds easy at first, but as plates stack up and bolts cross multiple layers, the puzzles become genuinely brain-bending.

The game belongs to the popular "screw puzzle" genre that has exploded on mobile app stores, but GameZipper lets you play it in your browser on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop.

How to Play Bolt Jam 3D

  • Tap a bolt to unscrew it from the plate it's holding.
  • Once all bolts are removed from a plate, it falls away.
  • Some bolts hold multiple plates together — unscrew strategically.
  • Clear all plates to free the rings and complete the level.
  • On desktop, click the bolts. On mobile, just tap them.

Bolt Jam 3D Tips & Strategy

New to screw puzzle games? Here are some strategies to help you solve levels faster:

  • Look for single-bolt plates first. If a plate only has one bolt holding it, removing that bolt immediately clears space.
  • Identify which bolts cross multiple layers. These are usually the key to solving the puzzle — removing them early can free several plates at once.
  • Work from the outside in. Plates on the edges are often easier to remove and create more working space.
  • Don't rush. Unlike action games, Bolt Jam 3D rewards careful observation. Take a moment to study the bolt layout before you start tapping.
