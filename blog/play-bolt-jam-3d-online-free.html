How to Play Bolt Jam 3D

Basic Controls

  • Click or tap a bolt to unscrew it
  • Bolts can only be removed when nothing is blocking them
  • Free all rings from the board to complete each level
  • Think carefully — the order matters!

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the outermost bolts first — they usually have fewer dependencies
  • Look for bolts that only hold one ring — those are safe to remove early
  • If you get stuck, try working backwards: which ring needs to come off last?
