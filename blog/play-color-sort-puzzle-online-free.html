How to Play Color Sort

  1. Tap a tube — Select a tube with colored balls on top. The top ball will highlight
  2. Tap another tube — Pour the ball into another tube. You can only pour onto the same color or into an empty tube
  3. Complete a tube — Fill a tube with 4 balls of the same color to clear it
  4. Clear all colors — Sort every color into its own tube to win the level
  5. Undo if stuck — Use the undo button to take back moves

Color Sort Tips and Strategy

  • Plan ahead — Always think 2-3 moves before pouring a ball
  • Keep empty tubes available — Never fill your last empty tube with mixed colors
  • Clear tubes strategically — Once a tube is nearly full of one color, prioritize completing it
  • Use undo freely — There's no penalty for undoing in Color Sort
  • Start with primary colors — Sort easy colors first to open up space
