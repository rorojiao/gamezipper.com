How to Play Boggle

1. Letters appear on a grid of dice. Each letter can only be used once per word.
2. Tap and drag across adjacent letters (including diagonals!) to trace a path and form a word.
3. Words must be 3+ letters long and in the dictionary.
4. Scoring: 3-4 letters = 1pt, 5 = 2pt, 6 = 3pt, 7 = 5pt, 8+ = 11pt.
5. Reach the target score before time runs out to win!
Qu counts as two letters on one die. Long words score big!
