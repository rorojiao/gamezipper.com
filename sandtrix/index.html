About Sandtrix — Sand Physics Tetris

Sandtrix is a unique twist on the classic Tetris formula. Instead of blocks locking into place, they crumble into colorful sand particles when they land! The sand follows realistic physics, flowing into gaps and settling naturally. Fill complete horizontal rows to clear them and score points.

The sand simulation creates emergent gameplay not found in regular Tetris. Blocks settle into unexpected patterns, and strategic placement can trigger satisfying chain reactions. Each level speeds up the falling blocks, testing your reflexes and planning skills.

Game Features

  • Real-time sand physics simulation
  • 7 classic tetromino shapes with unique colors
  • Ghost piece preview shows landing position
  • Next piece preview
  • Increasing speed with each level
  • High score saved locally
  • Touch controls for mobile play
  • Ambient background music

Controls

  • Desktop: Arrow keys to move, Up to rotate, Space to hard drop, P to pause
  • Mobile: Swipe to move, tap to rotate, swipe down for fast drop

Tips & Strategy

  • Sand fills gaps automatically — use this to your advantage
  • Don't leave thin columns — sand can't bridge wide gaps
  • The I-piece (cyan) is great for clearing full rows quickly
  • Watch the next piece preview to plan ahead
  • Hard drop (Space) gives bonus points but less control
