How to Play FreeCell

Goal

Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, building from Ace to King by suit.

Free Cells

The four cells in the top-left store one card each. Use them to temporarily hold cards during maneuvers.

Foundations

Build from Ace to King by suit in the top-right. Four piles, one per suit.

Tableau

  • 8 columns below, all cards face-up from start
  • First 4 columns: 7 cards. Last 4 columns: 6 cards
  • Build cards DOWN in alternating colors (red on black, black on red)
  • Move single cards or stacks using the supermove formula

Supermove Formula

Move up to (N+1) x 2^M cards at once, where N = empty free cells and M = empty tableau columns.

Auto-Move

Safe cards (when both opposite-color lower ranks are already on foundations) are auto-moved when you click Auto-Move.

Tips

  • Use free cells sparingly - they enable big supermoves
  • Empty tableau columns are powerful - plan to create them
  • Click Auto-Move often to clear the tableau faster
  • Use Hint if stuck, and Undo freely
