Blob Pop is a free online Puyo Puyo style chain-reaction puzzle game. Drop pairs of colored blobs into the play field, connect four or more blobs of the same color to pop them, and watch as gravity pulls remaining blobs down to trigger massive chain combos. With 30 handcrafted levels across six difficulty tiers, an endless survival mode, and a two-minute time attack, Blob Pop offers endless replay value. Play directly in your browser — no download, no sign-up, works on desktop, tablet, and mobile including school Chromebooks.
