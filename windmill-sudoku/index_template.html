How to Play Windmill Sudoku

Windmill Sudoku features 5 overlapping 9×9 Sudoku grids arranged in a pinwheel/X pattern:

  • Center grid in the middle, with 4 diagonal grids (NW, NE, SW, SE)
  • Each diagonal grid shares one corner 3×3 box with the center (highlighted)
  • Standard Sudoku rules apply to each grid independently
  • Shared cells belong to two grids — entering a digit updates both

Controls

  • Tap a cell to select, then tap a digit (1-9) to fill
  • Notes mode: enter pencil marks (small candidates)
  • Erase: clear a cell or its notes
  • Hint: reveals one correct digit (limited use)
  • Undo: reverts your last move
  • Check: highlights incorrect entries in red

Difference from Samurai Sudoku

Samurai Sudoku grids share edge-center boxes forming a + shape. Windmill shares corner boxes forming an X/pinwheel shape.
