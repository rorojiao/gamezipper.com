Triple Tile Match - Free 3D Tile Puzzle

Play Triple Tile Match online for free. Match 3 identical tiles to clear them from the board. Stack, sort, and clear layered tiles before the tray fills up. Perfect for fans of tile matching games, match-3 puzzles, and mahjong-style games.

How to Play Triple Tile

  • Tap or click a tile to send it to the tray at the bottom.
  • When 3 identical tiles are in the tray, they automatically match and clear.
  • Clear all tiles from the board to win the level.
  • If the tray fills up with 7 unmatched tiles, it's game over.
  • Some tiles are stacked or hidden — clear top layers to reveal what's underneath.

Tips & Strategy

  • Before placing a tile, check if there are 2 more of the same visible on the board.
  • Don't fill your tray with tiles you can't match soon.
  • Clear top layers first to reveal hidden tiles below.
  • Keep at least 2-3 tray slots open for flexibility.
  • Press R to restart if you get stuck.
