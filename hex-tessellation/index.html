How to Play 🧩

Goal: Fill the hexagonal silhouette completely with polyhex pieces.

Drag pieces from the tray onto the board.
Tap a piece to rotate it 60°.
Right-click or double-tap to flip.
Pieces must fit with no gaps and no overlaps.

Use 💡 hints sparingly — you get 3 per level!
