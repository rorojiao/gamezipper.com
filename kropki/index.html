How to Play

Fill the grid so every row and every column contains each digit exactly once.

Black dot — adjacent cells are doubles (e.g. 2 & 4, 3 & 6).

White dot — adjacent cells are consecutive (differ by 1, e.g. 4 & 5).

🚫 No dot — neither rule applies.

Tap a cell, then tap a digit. Toggle Pencil to keep candidate notes. Earn 3 stars by solving fast with few hints.
