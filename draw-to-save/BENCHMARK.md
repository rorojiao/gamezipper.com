## Core Mechanic (our game)
Player draws a **single continuous freehand stroke** that becomes a solid
barrier. When they release, threats (falling spikes, bee swarm, rising lava,
cannonballs, rolling boulders) activate and move toward the character. If no
threat reaches the character before the survival timer expires, the level is won.
