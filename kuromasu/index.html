How to Play Kuromasu

1
Read the Numbers

Each number tells you how many black cells are visible from that cell, looking up, down, left, and right to the grid edge.

2
Shade Cells Black

Click or tap a cell to shade it black. The number counts all black cells it can "see" in straight lines.

3
Keep Whites Connected

All white (unshaded) cells must form one connected group. You cannot isolate white cells with black cells.

4
No 2x2 Black Blocks

You cannot have a 2x2 square that is entirely black. At least one cell in every 2x2 area must be white.

5
Tips

Use hints (3 per puzzle) when stuck. Right-click or long-press to mark cells as definitely white (dot). Use undo/redo freely.
