ABOUT MAGIC TILES

Magic Tiles is a free online piano rhythm game. Tap black tiles as they fall down 4 lanes — feel the beat, build your combo, and earn 3 stars on every song. No download, no signup, plays right in your browser.

Five hand-tuned chapters take you from easy 80 BPM melodies up to 170 BPM virtuoso patterns. Web Audio synthesizes real piano tones for every tap, so every hit makes music.

How to Play

Rules

  • Black tiles fall down 4 lanes.
  • Tap each tile when it reaches the glowing bar at the bottom.
  • Tap an empty lane — Miss.
  • Let a tile pass — Miss.
  • 3 Misses ends the song.

Scoring

  • Perfect (center of zone): +150 & times combo multiplier.
  • Good (close): +120 & times combo multiplier.
  • OK (edge): +100 & times combo multiplier.
  • Combo multiplier grows up to 5×.
  • Three stars require 95%+ accuracy. Two stars: 80%+. One star: just finish.

Tips

  • Use one finger per lane on mobile for fast songs.
  • Watch the glowing hit zone — don't stare at the top.
  • Listen to the beat. The music tells you when to tap.
  • Higher chapters need faster fingers — earn stars to unlock them.
