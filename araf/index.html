How to Play Araf

Goal: Divide the grid into regions using borders.
Rule 1: Each region must contain exactly two numbered cells.
Rule 2: Region area must be greater than the larger number.
Rule 3: Region area must be less than the sum of both numbers.
Controls: Click/tap the edges between cells to toggle borders. Click a cell edge to draw or remove a wall.

Each puzzle has a unique solution. Use Check to verify your progress!
