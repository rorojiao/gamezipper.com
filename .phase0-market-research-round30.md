### Selected: Qwirkle (slug: `qwirkle`)

**Mechanic**: Color+Shape tile placement game. Place hex/square tiles in lines
where all tiles in a line share either the same color OR the same shape (but not
both identical). 6 colors × 6 shapes = 36 unique tile types, 3 of each = 108
tiles total. Score points for each tile in valid lines; 6-point bonus for a
"Qwirkle" (completing a line of all 6 of one attribute). Player vs AI opponent.

**Gap Score (25-pt rubric)**:
- Market gap (≥5M downloads, no browser clone): 9/10 — Spiel des Jahres 2011
  winner, 3M+ physical copies sold worldwide, #1 family strategy game.
  No quality browser version exists. Massive recognition.
- Search volume: 4/5 — "qwirkle" has steady branded search, recognized name
  in board game community, App Store/Play Store app drives search.
- Monetization potential: 4/5 — Long sessions (20+ min), perfect for
  interstitials between rounds + banner. High replay via AI difficulty levels.
- Technical feasibility: 3/3 — Pure DOM, tile drag-drop + line validation.
  No physics. AI is greedy/heuristic (place highest-scoring valid move).
- Uniqueness: 2/2 — Mechanic is distinct from:
  - Triple Town (merge 3, not line placement)
  - Scrabble/Crossword (letters/words, not color+shape)
  - Dominosa (partition grid, not build lines)
  - Mastermind (deduction, not placement)
  - Mahjong (matching pairs, not line building)

**Total: 22/25** ✅ EXCEEDS 18/25 threshold

### Rejection log
- **Sagrada**: Good game but niche recognition. Dice-draft adds RNG complexity.
  18/25 borderline — defer unless Qwirkle fails.
- **Patchwork**: Polyomino overlap (Pack Master, Pentomino, Tetra Fit). 16/25.
- **Tantrix**: Hex tile path-loop overlaps hex-path-spin, prism-path, glyph-quest.
- **Tsuro**: Path-laying overlaps path cluster (20+ games with "track"/"route").
- **Azul**: Pattern drafting overlaps tile-connect, tile-dynasty concepts.

### Decision
**Build Qwirkle** as game #528. Single-file HTML5 with:
- 108 tiles (6 colors × 6 shapes × 3 each)
- Player vs AI (3 difficulty: Easy=greedy, Medium=greedy+block, Hard=minimax-2)
- Hand of 6 tiles, draw from bag
- Line placement validation (same color all different shapes, or same shape
  all different colors)
- Qwirkle bonus (6-tile line = +6 points)
- Swap tiles option (exchange hand tiles for bag tiles)
- Game ends when bag empty + one player uses all tiles
- 30 levels across 5 tiers (increasing AI difficulty + board complexity)
- Star ratings based on score vs par
