How to Play Red

Red is a creative puzzle game where your goal is simple: turn the entire screen red. But each of the 30 levels uses a completely different trick — there's no single rule to learn! You'll tap circles rapidly, drag sliders, spin wheels, spell words, memorize patterns, solve math puzzles, guide balls through mazes, and much more.

The key is experimentation: tap, drag, rub, shake, and look for clues. If you get stuck, tap the hint button (💡) for a text clue specific to that level. Levels are organized into 6 difficulty tiers from Beginner to Master, so the challenge ramps up steadily.

Tips & Strategy

  • Read the hint first: Every level has a unique hint. Tap 💡 before you start tapping randomly — it tells you the core mechanic.
  • Timing matters: Several levels (tap-fast, rhythm, tower-building) require precise timing. The hint will tell you if speed or rhythm is involved.
  • Try different gestures: Not all levels use clicks. Some need dragging (sliders, sorting, guiding), circular motions (spinning), or rapid back-and-forth (shaking).
  • Math levels: Later tiers include number puzzles — perfect squares, binary conversion, descending order. Have a calculator handy for the Expert/Master tiers.
  • Memory levels: Echo and sequence levels get progressively longer. Focus on one segment at a time rather than the whole pattern.
  • Use the level select: Stuck on a hard level? Skip it and come back. Tap ☰ to access the level menu.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Red free to play?

Yes! Red is completely free to play in your browser with no download or sign-up required.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely. Red is fully optimized for touch controls — drag, tap, and swipe all work naturally on phones and tablets.

How many levels are there?

Red has 30 levels across 6 difficulty tiers: Beginner (1-5), Easy (6-10), Normal (11-15), Hard (16-20), Expert (21-25), and Master (26-30). Each level introduces a unique puzzle mechanic.

Is my progress saved?

Yes — your current level is automatically saved to your browser. When you return, you'll resume from where you left off.

What if I get stuck?

Tap the hint button (💡) for a clue specific to the current level. You can also skip to any level via the menu (☰).
