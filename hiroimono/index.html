How to play Hiroimono

Hiroimono (Goishi Hiroi — "picking up Go stones") is a sequential path puzzle. Pick up every stone on the board, one at a time, in legal pickup order.

  • Start by picking any stone on the board.
  • Each next stone must lie on the same row or column as the stone you just picked.
  • Your path may not skip any stone that is still on the board — but you may travel over empty squares where stones have already been removed.
  • You may go straight or turn 90°. You may not reverse directly back toward the immediately previous stone.
  • You win when every stone has been picked up.
  • Use H for a hint, Z to undo, R to restart, Space/Enter to check.
