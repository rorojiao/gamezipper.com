Pipe Connect — Free Online Pipe Puzzle Game

Play Pipe Connect free online. Rotate pipe pieces to build a connected path from the water source to the drain. A logic puzzle game with 50+ levels across 4 difficulty tiers.

How to Play

  • Tap any pipe to rotate it 90 degrees clockwise.
  • Connect pipes from the green source (S) to the red drain (D).
  • Water flows automatically when all pipes are connected.
  • Earn 3 stars by solving in fewer moves (close to par).
  • Use hints wisely — 3 per level, earn more with 3-star completions.

Game Features

  • 50+ procedurally generated levels
  • 4 difficulty tiers: 5x5, 6x6, 7x7, 8x8 grids
  • 6 pipe types: straight, corner, T-junction, cross, source, drain
  • Star rating system (1-3 stars based on moves)
  • Hint system with BFS-based solution finder
  • Score system with move penalty and time bonus
  • Smooth rotation and water flow animations
  • Sound effects via Web Audio API
  • Progress saved to localStorage

Tips & Tricks

  • Start from the source and work outward — connect what you can see first.
  • Corner pipes only connect in 2 directions — use that to narrow possibilities.
  • Cross pieces connect all 4 directions — they can go anywhere on the path.
  • Use hints when stuck — they highlight the next pipe to rotate.
  • Watch the par score — fewer moves means more stars and bonus hints.
