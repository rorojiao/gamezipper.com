Pottery Master is a free online clay-shaping puzzle game where you spin the potter's wheel and sculpt clay vessels to match target silhouettes. Drag along the spinning clay to push material in and out, crafting bowls, vases, goblets, and bottles across 30 handcrafted levels. Test your precision and artistry in this relaxing brain teaser — no download required!

📋 How to Play Pottery Master
  1. Study the target: The dashed outline shows the vessel shape you need to create.
  2. Drag to shape: Touch and drag along the clay. Drag away from center to widen, toward center to narrow. Changes blend smoothly into nearby areas.
  3. Watch the match meter: It shows how close your profile is to the target. Reach the green zone to win!
  4. Use tools wisely: Undo reverses your last change, Reset starts over, and Hint reveals the target shape briefly.
  5. Earn stars: Match closely or use fewer moves for 3-star ratings across all 30 levels.
