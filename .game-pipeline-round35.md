## Gameplay Design
- Top-down suitcase view (rectangular container)
- Items spawn (clothes, electronics, toiletries, shoes) with different shapes/weights
- Player drag-and-drop items into suitcase
- Cannot overlap items, must stay within bounds
- Level complete: all items fit
- Difficulty curve: more items, awkward shapes, fragile items, weight distribution limits, bonus for optimal packing
- Star ratings: time-based (how fast you pack) + unused space bonus

## Technical Specs
