How to Play

  • Click (desktop) or tap (mobile) to make your bird flap.
  • Navigate through the gaps in the pipes to score.
  • Each pipe successfully passed = +1 point.
  • Don't touch the pipes or the ground — instant game over!

Game Features

Tips & Tricks to Beat Your High Score

  • Tap at a steady rhythm — don't over-tap or you'll shoot up too high. Consistent small taps work best.
  • Focus on the gap, not the bird — train your eyes on the opening between pipes and let your reflexes handle the rest.
  • Stay in the middle — keep your bird at mid-screen height so you have room to adjust in both directions.
  • Don't panic after a near-miss — relax and reset your rhythm. Panic tapping is the #1 cause of crashing.
  • Short sessions work best — play for 2-3 minutes, take a break, come back fresh. Your focus sharpens after rests.

How to Play

  • Click, tap, or press Space to make the bird flap upward
  • The bird constantly falls due to gravity
  • Navigate through the gaps between green pipes
  • Each pipe cleared scores one point

  • Small, controlled flaps — do not over-flap; gentle taps work better
