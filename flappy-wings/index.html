Flappy Wings — Free Endless Flapping Game

Play Flappy Wings free online — the addictive one-tap arcade game. Tap to flap, dodge the pipes, and beat your high score. Endless fun!

How to Play

  • Click (desktop) or tap (mobile) to make your bird flap.
  • Navigate through the gaps in the pipes to score.
  • Each pipe successfully passed = +1 point.
  • Don't touch the pipes or the ground — instant game over!

Game Features

  • 🐦 One-tap simple controls
  • 🚀 Instant restart — play again in one tap

Tips & Tricks

  • Tap at a steady rhythm — don't over-tap.
  • Focus on the gap, not the bird.
  • Relax — panic causes erratic tapping.
