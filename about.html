GameZipper is a free browser game platform founded in 2024. We believe quick games should be — open and play. No downloads, no sign-ups, no friction. From a 5-minute break at school to unwinding at home, our HTML5 games run in any browser on any device.

🎯 Our Mission

Make quality browser games accessible to everyone, everywhere. Whether you're on a school Chromebook, a work laptop, or a phone — GameZipper games load fast, run fair, and never ask for your credit card.
