How to Play Mastermind
1
The computer creates a secret code of 4 colored pegs. Your goal is to guess the exact colors and their positions.
2
Select colors from the palette below the board, then tap a peg slot to place the color. Fill all 4 slots and tap Submit Guess.
3
After each guess you receive feedback pegs on the right side of the row:
● Black peg = correct color, correct position
● White peg = correct color, wrong position
4
Feedback pegs are shuffled so you can't tell which slot they correspond to. Use logic and deduction!
5
Crack the code (4 black pegs) before running out of guesses to win. Fewer guesses = higher score!
