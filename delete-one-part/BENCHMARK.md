# Competitive Benchmark — Delete One Part (Round 46)

## Top competitors (mobile, 100M+ download tier)
1. **DOP: Delete One Part** (SayGames Ltd.) — 100M+ downloads. THE category leader.
   - Each level shows a hand-drawn picture with a "problem"
   - Player drags a finger to ERASE part of the picture to solve a riddle
   - Riddles: "erase the water so the fish swims", "erase the wall to find the key",
     "erase the extra wheel", "erase the sadness (rain cloud)"
   - "Aha!" moment per level drives binge replay (5-15s sessions)
   - Hint button (reveals target area), skip button (rewarded ad)
   - Hundreds of levels, escalating absurdity
2. **DOP 2: Delete One Part** (SayGames) — sequel, same core + new riddles. 50M+.
3. **DOP 3 / DOP 4** — further sequels. SayGames milks the franchise hard = proven revenue.
4. **Delete Puzzle: Erase One Part** / **Erase Master** / **Brain Out: Delete** — clones,
   each 10M+ downloads. Genre is validated and heavily cloned = high demand.

## Core mechanics to match
- **Drag-to-erase**: finger or mouse drag acts as an eraser brush on the picture
- **Erase brush**: circular region, follows pointer, removes pixels (alpha to 0)
- **Riddle framing**: each level is a mini brain-teaser ("erase the X to do Y")
- **Target zone**: the correct area to erase; erasing it solves the level
- **Wrong erase penalty**: erasing the wrong area wastes "ink/eraser" or lowers star rating
- **Auto-solve detection**: when enough of the target is erased, level completes instantly
- **Reveal**: erasing often reveals something hidden underneath (the "solution")

## Level archetypes (the 5 riddle families)
1. **Erase the obstacle** — wall/lock/cloud blocking something; erase it to open/reveal
2. **Erase to reveal** — fog/paint/snow covering something; erase to uncover
3. **Erase the wrong part** — scene has an error (extra wheel, wrong number); erase the mistake
4. **Erase to free/connect** — cage/net/barrier trapping something; erase to release
5. **Trick riddles** — abstract ("erase the sadness" = erase a rain cloud → face smiles)

## Monetization
- Interstitial between levels (Monetag, wired site-wide)
- Rewarded "show solution" hint + "skip level" (high-converting in DOP genre)
- Banner slot
