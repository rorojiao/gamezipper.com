Chocolate Bean Storm — Free Online Bubble Shooter

Chocolate Bean Storm is a sweet twist on the classic bubble shooter. Aim, match, and pop colorful chocolate beans to clear levels and rack up points. With vibrant visuals and satisfying chain reactions, it's the perfect casual puzzle for quick breaks!

How to Play Chocolate Bean Storm

  • Aim by moving your mouse or finger to set the shooting direction.
  • Click or tap to launch a chocolate bean toward the cluster.
  • Match 3 or more beans of the same color to pop them and clear them off the board.
  • Popping beans can cause chain reactions — dropping unconnected beans above for bonus points.
  • Clear the target number of beans or the whole board to advance to the next level!

Tips & Strategy

  • Aim for anchor beans at the top of clusters — popping them drops everything below.
  • Use wall bounces to reach beans tucked in corners.
  • Plan ahead — check which bean color is coming next and match strategically.
  • Big clusters = big points. Try to set up 5+ bean matches for maximum score.
  • Clear the board before beans reach the bottom to keep the game going!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chocolate Bean Storm free to play?

Yes! It's completely free to play online in your browser. No download, no signup — just open and play.

Can I play on my phone?

Absolutely — touch controls make it easy to aim and shoot on any mobile device or tablet.

How do I score more points?

Create large matches and chain reactions. Popping an anchor bean that drops a whole cluster gives the biggest score boost!

What happens if beans reach the bottom?

The game ends. Clear beans quickly and efficiently to keep the board under control.
