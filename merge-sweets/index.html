Merge Sweets

Tap bakery stations to generate ingredients. Merge matching treats to fulfill customer orders!

How to Play

  1. Tap stations at the top to generate random base ingredients.
  2. Drag one item onto an identical matching item to merge them.
  3. Merging two items creates the next level treat in that chain.
  4. Check the order panel for customer requests.
  5. Drag required items onto order slots to fulfill orders.
  6. Complete all orders before time runs out to win!
