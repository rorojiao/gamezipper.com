Merge Sweets

Drag and merge matching bakery treats to create delicious cakes!

How to Play

  1. Drag any item onto an adjacent matching item to merge them.
  2. Two identical items combine into the next tier treat.
  3. Earn points for each merge — bigger treats score more!
  4. Reach the target score before running out of moves.
  5. Chain merges (3+ in one drag) give a 1.5x bonus!
  6. New items appear at the top row when you run low.
