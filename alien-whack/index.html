About Alien Whack

Alien Whack is a fast-paced reflex arcade game set in outer space. Whack the aliens before they escape — test your reaction speed and hand-eye coordination in this addictive browser game. Simple to play, hard to master.

Works on desktop and mobile. Click or tap the aliens as fast as you can to score points. Miss too many and the game ends. How high can you score?

How to Play Alien Whack

  1. Start the game — Click or tap the START GAME button. A 3×3 grid of holes appears against a starry space backdrop.
  2. Whack green aliens — They pop up randomly. Tap them fast for 1 point each. They disappear quickly, so stay alert!
  3. Catch gold aliens for 3× — Rare golden aliens flash briefly. Hit them for 3 points — they're worth the extra attention.
  4. Avoid bombs — Bombs cost you 2 points AND reset your combo streak. Learn their pattern and skip them.
  5. Build combos — Consecutive hits fill the combo bar. When it's full, your next hit earns a 2× multiplier!
  6. Beat the clock — You have 60 seconds. Aliens speed up in the final 15 seconds. Stay sharp!

Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Prioritize gold aliens — At 3 points each, one gold alien equals three green ones. Always go for gold when it appears.
  • Don't chase bombs — The -2 penalty plus combo reset makes bombs devastating. If you're unsure, wait for the next alien.
  • Watch the edges — Aliens often appear at corners and edges of the grid. Keep your eyes scanning the whole board.
  • Maintain your combo — A full combo bar gives 2× multiplier. Consistent accuracy beats frantic tapping.
  • Speed up in final seconds — The last 15 seconds are the biggest scoring window. Aliens appear faster, so stay focused.
