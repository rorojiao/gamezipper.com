Welcome to Hyper Sudoku!

It follows all standard Sudoku rules — each row, column, and 3×3 box gets digits 1–9.

But there are four extra regions, shown with colored borders.

The Four Extra Regions

The four colored 3×3 regions are positioned in a windmill pattern between the standard boxes.

Each colored region must also contain all digits 1–9 with no repeats — just like a standard box.

How to Play

① Tap a cell, then tap a digit on the pad.
