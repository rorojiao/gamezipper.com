How to Play Blackjack

Goal

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over (busting).

Card Values

  • Number cards: face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10
  • Ace: 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

Actions

  • Hit — Take another card
  • Stand — Keep your hand
  • Double Down — Double your bet, get exactly one more card
  • Split — If you have two matching cards, split into two hands
  • Surrender — Give up half your bet and fold (first two cards only)
  • Insurance — Side bet when dealer shows Ace (pays 2:1 if dealer has Blackjack)
