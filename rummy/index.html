Rummy

How to Play Rummy

Objective: Be the first to meld all your cards. Form sets (3+ cards of the same rank) and runs (3+ consecutive cards of the same suit).

Your Turn: Draw a card from the stock pile (face-down) or the discard pile (face-up). Then either meld cards or lay off on existing melds. Finally, discard one card.

Classic Mode: Meld cards whenever you have valid combinations. Lay off cards on your opponent's melds. Win by melding all cards.

Gin Rummy Mode: Keep melds hidden. Go out with Gin (zero deadwood) or knock (10 or fewer deadwood points). Your opponent can lay off deadwood on your melds when you knock.

Scoring: Ace = 1, 2-9 = face value, 10/J/Q/K = 10 points. Winner scores the opponent's deadwood total.

Controls: Click cards to select. Click stock/discard to draw. Click Meld to lay down selected cards. Click Sort to arrange your hand.
