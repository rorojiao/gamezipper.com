## Technical Benchmark

### Physics Requirements
- Bullet trajectory: line segment + reflection off walls
- Reflection formula: angle in = angle out (specular reflection)
- Collision detection: point-in-polygon / line-segment intersection
- No external physics engine needed — custom raycasting is simpler and lighter

### Level Design Patterns
- Tier 1 (Beginner): 1-2 enemies, 1-2 walls, 1 bullet → direct hit or simple bounce
- Tier 2 (Easy): 2-3 enemies, 2-3 walls, 2 bullets
- Tier 3 (Medium): 3-4 enemies, explosive barrels, 2-3 bullets
- Tier 4 (Hard): 4-5 enemies, hostages (don't shoot), limited bullets
- Tier 5 (Expert): Complex layouts, multiple ricochets required, tight angles

### Visual Style
- Dark neon theme (consistent with GameZipper catalog)

- Wall obstacles in contrasting color
- Particle effects on hit

## Game Specification

### Mechanics
1. **Aim**: Touch/mouse drag from shooter position. Show dotted trajectory preview.
2. **Shoot**: Release to fire. Bullet travels in straight line, reflects off walls up to N bounces.
3. **Win**: Hit all enemies. Bullet is consumed on enemy hit or after max bounces.
4. **Lose**: Run out of bullets with enemies remaining.
5. **Hazards**: Explosive barrels (chain reaction), hostages (shooting = instant fail).

### Level System
- 30 handcrafted levels across 5 tiers
- 3-star rating: 1 star = complete, 2 = efficient bullets, 3 = perfect (minimum bullets)
- localStorage progress save

### UI
- Title screen with play button
- Level select grid (locked/unlocked)
- HUD: bullets remaining, enemies remaining, star display
- Win/Lose overlay with retry/next buttons
- Settings: sound toggle, music toggle
