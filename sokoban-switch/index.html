How to Play

You are a warehouse worker. Your job is to push every crate onto a goal tile.

New twist: some walls are colored gates. To pass through, find the matching pressure plate and step on it.

Boxes can sit on plates too — that opens gates! Different plate colors open different gate colors. You have 4 colors: RED · BLUE · GREEN · YELLOW.

Keys: Arrow keys / WASD to move · U to undo · R to restart · Z to redo.

Tip: You can walk on gates freely when they are open. Step OFF a plate to close its gate. Boxes never open or close gates themselves.
