Stacker — Free Precision Block Stacking Game

Play Stacker free online! Stack moving blocks precisely to build the highest tower. A skill-based arcade game that tests your timing.

How to Play

  • Press Space / tap to drop the moving block onto the stack.
  • The block snaps over the previous one — only the overlapping part stays.
  • The narrower your stack gets, the harder it becomes.
  • Reach the top to win the level!

Game Features

  • ⬜ Pure timing-based gameplay
  • 📐 Precision challenge increases with each layer
  • 🚀 Get into the zone with rhythmic play
  • 📱 One-tap mobile compatible

Tips & Tricks to Stack Higher

  • Watch the rhythm of the block's movement, not just its position — time your tap to the beat of its swing.
  • Tap slightly early — account for your reaction time delay. If you wait until it looks perfect, you'll be too late.
  • Stay calm and breathe — nervousness kills accuracy. Each layer is just one tap.
  • Perfect alignment matters more early on — even a tiny misalignment in the first 5 layers compounds into a very narrow stack later.
  • Focus on the edges — watch where the block's sides line up with the stack below rather than the center.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Stacker?

A row of blocks moves back and forth across the screen. Tap or press spacebar to drop the row onto the stack below. The overlapping portion stays; anything that hangs off falls away. Stack as high as possible with perfectly aligned blocks to reach the top and win!
