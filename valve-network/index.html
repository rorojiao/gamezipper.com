❓ How to Play

Goal: Route colored fluids from sources (▶) to matching targets (⬓). Toggle valves to control flow!

How: Tap any valve to open or close it. Fluid flows only through open valves. Close valves to avoid dead-ends (dashed circles). Tier 1-2: Basic Routing Single source, one or two targets. Tier 3: Color Matching Multiple colored fluids — each target needs its matching color. Tier 4: Pressure Valves (⚙) Gear-marked valves need upstream pressure. Tier 5: No Contamination Targets must not receive wrong colors. Tier 6: Loop Networks Complex pipe graphs with loops.

Stars: ⭐⭐⭐ for par valve count. Use Undo (↶), Reset (↻), and Hints (💡)!
