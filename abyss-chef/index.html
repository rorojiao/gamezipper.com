Abyss Chef - Free Online Cooking Puzzle Game

Play Abyss Chef — a free cooking game online where you help Ollie the octopus chef fish deep-sea ingredients, cook amazing dishes, and run an underwater restaurant. Perfect for fans of kitchen puzzle and restaurant game genres. No download needed!

How to Play

  • Fish for deep-sea ingredients by casting your line at the right moment.
  • Cook dishes by combining caught fish with recipes in your underwater kitchen.
  • Serve customers quickly to earn tips and keep your restaurant rating high.
  • Manage 25 fish species across 30 days of service to become the best deep-sea chef.
