Watermelon Merge — Free Online Suika Fruit Merge Puzzle

Play Watermelon Merge (Suika Game) free online! Drop and merge matching fruits to create larger ones, all the way to the ultimate watermelon. Physics-based puzzle with 11 fruit levels. No download required.

How to Play

  • Move the dropper left and right at the top of the container (mouse or touch).
  • Click or tap to drop the current fruit into the container.
  • When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next larger fruit.
  • The merge chain goes: Cherry → Strawberry → Grape → ... → Watermelon.
  • Don't let fruits stack above the top line — game over!
  • Create the watermelon (the largest fruit) for maximum points.

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep large fruits in the center: Big fruits in the middle create a stable base and leave room for smaller fruits to merge on the sides.
  • Plan your drops: Don't just drop wherever — think about where identical fruits will end up after physics settles.
  • Chain merges: Dropping a fruit that triggers multiple merges in sequence is hugely efficient — set up cascading combinations.
  • Watch the fill line: The top of the container is your failure condition. Never let fruits pile above it.
  • Use the walls: Drop fruits near walls to control where they settle and set up future merges.
