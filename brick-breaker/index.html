Brick Breaker — Classic Breakout Arcade Game

Play Brick Breaker free online! Launch the ball, break all the bricks, collect power-ups, and clear every level. A timeless arcade classic.

How to Play

  • Move your mouse or use touch to control the paddle.
  • Bounce the ball to break bricks and clear the level.
  • Collect power-ups that fall from broken bricks.
  • Don't let the ball fall off the bottom — you lose a life!

Game Features

  • 🎮 Responsive paddle controls
  • ⚡ Multiple power-ups: wide paddle, multi-ball, laser
  • 🏆 Progressive level difficulty

Tips & Tricks

  • Aim for the top corners to create chain reactions.
  • Prioritize power-up bricks for game-changing boosts.
  • Use the edges of the paddle to change ball angle.
