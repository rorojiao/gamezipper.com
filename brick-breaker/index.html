How to Play

  • Move your mouse or use touch to control the paddle.

Tips & Tricks for High Scores

  • Aim for the top corners to create chain reactions — bricks near the top are worth more points when comboed.
  • Prioritize power-up bricks for game-changing boosts like wide paddle and multi-ball.
  • Use paddle edges to change ball angle — hitting the left edge sends the ball left, right edge sends it right.
  • Stay centered — keep your paddle near the middle of the screen so you can react to unexpected bounces.
  • Multi-ball strategy: when you get multi-ball, focus on keeping at least one ball alive rather than chasing all of them.
  • Clear from the edges inward — removing side bricks first gives you more wall-bounce angles for tricky shots.
