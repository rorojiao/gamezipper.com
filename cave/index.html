Welcome to Cave!

Cave (also known as Corral) is a Nikoli logic puzzle. Find the hidden cave by shading cells.

  • Numbered cells are always part of the cave (unshaded).
  • A number = how many cave cells are visible from it in 4 directions (including itself), stopping at the first shaded cell.
  • Left-click an empty cell to shade it (wall). Click again to mark unknown (dot). Click again to clear.
  • Right-click (or long-press) cycles the opposite way.
  • All cave (unshaded) cells must form one connected region.
  • All shaded cells must connect to the grid edge (no enclosed walls).

Press H for a hint, C to check your progress, U to undo.
