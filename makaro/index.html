How to Play Makaro

  • The grid is divided into rooms separated by bold borders. Each room has 2–5 cells.
  • A room with N cells must contain each number 1 through N exactly once.
  • An arrow on a cell points to the neighbor (among its 4 orthogonal neighbors) with the largest number.
  • Two edge-adjacent cells in different rooms cannot contain the same number.
  • Click a cell to select it, then type a number (1–5) or tap the number pad.
  • Use Check to highlight errors, Hint to reveal a correct cell, and Undo/Redo to navigate.
