How to Play

1Patients arrive at the entrance door with a colored department tag and a shrinking patience ring.
2Tap a patient in the queue to select them, then tap an empty bed of the matching color to assign them.
3The doctor auto-walks to occupied beds and fills the treatment bar. Faster doctors = more tips.
4When treatment completes, the patient auto-discharges and pays cash. Quick discharges build a combo multiplier.
5If patience hits 0, the patient leaves angry and you lose the potential cash.
6Between days, spend cash on upgrades: more beds, faster doctor, extra doctor, new departments.
7Reach the day's cash target before time runs out to earn stars and unlock the next day.
