Geneva Drive

Rotate the driver crank to index star wheels to target angles. Chains couple wheels — fewer moves, more stars.

How to Play

  • Goal: Rotate all star wheels to their target angles.
  • Controls: Tap CW / CCW buttons to rotate each chain by one step.
  • Chains: Wheels linked by a dashed chain rotate together.
  • Counter-rotation: Some wheels in a chain step in opposite directions.
  • Stars: Solve in fewer moves for higher star ratings.
  • Keyboard: Arrow Left/Right for chain 1, number keys 1-3 to select chain.
