How to Play Twiddle

Sort the scrambled numbers by rotating 2x2 blocks
1

The grid holds the numbers 1 to N² scrambled up. Your goal: restore them to ascending order — 1 top-left, N² bottom-right.

2

Tap any cell to rotate the 2x2 block anchored at that cell 90° clockwise. The four numbers cycle around. Use the ↺ CCW button to switch to counter-clockwise.

3

Every scramble is solvable. Use Hint for a nudge, Undo to backtrack, and solve the whole board to win stars! ⭐
