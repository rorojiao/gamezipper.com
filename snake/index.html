How to Play Snake

  • Use arrow keys or WASD on desktop to control the snake.

Simple Snake Strategy

  • Use the outer edges early so the center stays open for recovery moves.
  • Do not chase food if it traps you in a tight loop with no exit.
  • Once your snake gets long, think in rectangles and lanes instead of single turns.
