About Sequence Memory Puzzle

Sequence Memory Puzzle is a brain training memory game inspired by Simon Says. Watch the sequence of zones light up with audio cues, then repeat the pattern in the correct order. Master 30 levels across 5 difficulty tiers — from beginner to expert. Choose between 4 pattern types: Classic colors, Shapes, Arrows, or Numbers. LocalStorage saves your progress so you can continue your training anytime.
