How to Play

Sugar falls from the dispenser at the top.

Draw lines by clicking and dragging. Sugar particles will slide and bounce along your drawn lines.

Guide enough sugar into each cup to fill it. Fill all cups to complete the level!

In later levels, colored sugar must go into matching cups.
