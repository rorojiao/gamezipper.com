# Phase 0 Market Research — Round 65 (2026-07-06)

## Research Summary
Catalog: 569 live games. Scanned for gaps in puzzle categories.

## Candidates Scored (≥18/25 = viable)

### 1. Pizza Maker Puzzle — 23/25 ⭐ WINNER
- Slug: `pizza-maker`
- Mechanic: Assemble pizzas to match customer orders — place crust, sauce, cheese, toppings on a circular grid
- Market: Good Pizza Great Pizza: 50M+ downloads. Cooking puzzle is proven massive category
- SEO: "pizza game", "cooking game", "pizza maker", "puzzle cooking", all high volume
- Monetization: Interstitial between every 5 orders, banner on menu
- Feasibility: Very High — grid placement + order matching, no complex physics
- Uniqueness: ZERO overlap — no pizza/cooking/baking game in 569-game catalog

### 2. Tilt Maze (Ball Roll) — 22/25
- Slug: `tilt-maze`
- Mechanic: Tilt board to roll ball through maze
- Market: 10M+ downloads across marble/labyrinth games
- Feasibility: Very High
- Uniqueness: No tilt-ball game in catalog (have maze-runner, labyrinth but different mechanic)

### 3. Mining Puzzle — 20/25
- Slug: `mining-puzzle`
- Mechanic: Dig through layered earth, collect gems, avoid hazards
- Market: Diggy's Adventure 50M+
- Feasibility: Very High
- Uniqueness: Near-zero overlap (treasure-dig is pathfinding, this is grid digging)

## Decision: Pizza Maker Puzzle (23/25)
- Largest market (50M+ downloads cooking puzzle category)
- Zero catalog overlap
- Best SEO keywords
- Highest engagement (order matching + time pressure)
- Proven single-file HTML5 feasibility
