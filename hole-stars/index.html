How to Play

1. Move the hole: Drag your finger or mouse to move the black hole around the level.

2. Swallow objects: Move the hole over objects smaller than it to swallow them.

3. Grow bigger: Each swallowed object makes your hole bigger, letting you eat larger objects.

4. Clear the level: Swallow all target objects to win. Get 3 stars by being fast and efficient!

Watch out: Obstacles like spikes and bombs can shrink your hole! Magnet objects attract nearby items. Stars give bonus points.
