Why People Love Games Like 2048

2048 became a viral sensation because it hits a perfect sweet spot: simple enough to learn in seconds, deep enough to master over months. The core mechanic — sliding tiles to merge matching numbers — creates a satisfying feedback loop that keeps players coming back.
