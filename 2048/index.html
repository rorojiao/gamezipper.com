2048 Game Online — Free Classic Number Puzzle

Play 2048 free online. Slide numbered tiles to combine them and reach the 2048 tile. A classic number sliding puzzle with smooth animations.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to slide all tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one.
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win — but keep going for a higher score!
  • The game ends when no more moves are possible.
