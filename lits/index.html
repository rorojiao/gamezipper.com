How to Play LITS

Objective: Shade cells to place one tetromino in each region.

Tetromino Shapes: L, I, T, and S (all rotations/reflections allowed).

Rules:

  • Each region must have exactly 4 shaded cells forming an L, I, T, or S shape.
  • Two adjacent regions cannot have the same tetromino shape.
  • All shaded cells must form one connected group (orthogonally).
  • No 2x2 square may be fully shaded.

Controls:

  • Left-click / Tap: Shade or unshade a cell.
  • Right-click / Long-press: Mark cell as empty (X).
  • Use Undo/Redo to review moves.
  • Use Hint to reveal one correct cell.
