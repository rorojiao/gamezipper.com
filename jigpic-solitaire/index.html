How to Play

1. The Goal

Restore the scrambled picture by swapping tiles. Use the reference thumbnail as your guide.

2. Swap Tiles

Tap the first tile to select it (it will glow), then tap a second tile to swap their positions.

3. Correct Tiles

Tiles in the correct position will have a subtle green glow. Use this feedback to track progress.

4. Stars

Solve within par moves for 3 stars. Under 2x par for 2 stars. Any completion earns 1 star.

5. Hints

Use the Hint button to highlight two tiles that should be swapped. Limited to 3 hints per level.

6. Undo

Made a mistake? Use Undo to revert your last move. Unlimited undos, but each undo adds to your move count.
