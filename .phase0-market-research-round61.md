## Winner: Hop Warp (21/25)

- **Slug:** hop-warp
- **Mechanic:** Grid-based teleport puzzle. Player moves on a tile grid. Can walk in 4 directions AND warp to any visible tile within range. Each level has limited warp charges. Must collect all stars then reach the exit portal.
- **Why it wins:**
  - Market 4: Popular on Poki, "teleport/warp" puzzle is a recognized subgenre
  - Feasibility 5: Pure grid logic, no physics, deterministic, Canvas rendering
  - Uniqueness 4: No "limited-charge teleport" puzzle in our 478-game puzzle catalog
  - Zero overlap with existing portal/teleport games (we have teleport-jumper but that's a different mechanic — fixed teleport pads, not free-aim warps)
- **Levels:** 30 across 5 tiers (Easy → Expert), introducing walls, one-way doors, keys, warp-charge pickups, star gates
