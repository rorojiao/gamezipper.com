Why Doppelblock?

Doppelblock (German for “Double Block”) is a unique puzzle that fuses three classic mechanics:

• Shading: each row and column has exactly two black cells.
• Latin square: white cells fill with 1..N-2 each appearing once per row/col.
• Sum clues: outside numbers reveal the digit sums between black cells.

The result is a satisfying logic puzzle with three layers of constraint that reward careful deduction. 30 levels across 5 tiers from 5x5 Beginner to 7x7 Expert.

How to Play Doppelblock

Setup. The grid is N×N. Each row and column contains exactly 2 black cells — those are your constraints.

Filling white cells. The remaining white cells must hold digits 1 to N-2, with each digit appearing exactly once per row and column. (This is the Latin-square rule.)

Outside clues. A small number outside the grid means: the sum of the white digits strictly between the two black cells in that line equals that number.

Tools.

• Digit mode: tap a white cell, then tap a digit on the keypad.
• Erase mode: tap any filled cell to clear it.
• Check: validates your current state. Errors are highlighted.
• Hint: reveals one correct cell. You have 3 per level.
• Clear: removes all your entries (keeps black cells).
