How to Play

🎯 Goal

Collect all the cards to win!

🔄 Taking Turns

  • Click your deck to flip a card onto the center pile
  • If you play a face card (J/Q/K/A), the next player must match it
  • Ace = 4 chances, King = 3, Queen = 2, Jack = 1
  • If they fail, you take the pile!

👋 Slapping

  • Doubles — two same-rank cards in a row
  • Sandwich — same rank with one card between
  • Tens — two cards summing to 10
  • Marriage — King + Queen adjacent
  • 4-in-Row — ascending/descending sequence
  • Top/Bottom — top card matches bottom of pile

⚠️ Slapping on no combo = burn penalty (lose 1 card)!
