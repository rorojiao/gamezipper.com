How to Play

Mahjong Connect (also known as Shisen-Sho or Lianliankan) is a tile-matching puzzle.

1. Click two identical tiles to remove them.
2. A valid path between them has at most 2 right-angle turns.
3. The path only travels through empty cells - and the 1-cell border around the board is always empty.
4. Any 2 Seasons match. Any 2 Flowers match.
5. Clear the board before the timer runs out (Time Attack only).

Power-ups: Hint highlights a matchable pair. Shuffle rearranges the remaining tiles. Undo restores your last match.
