Cloud & Sheep - Free Cute Sheep Care Simulator

Play Cloud & Sheep — a free online sheep game where you care for adorable fluffy sheep by dragging fluffy clouds to create rain and grow grass. This cute animal game lets you feed, clean, and play with your sheep flock. No download needed!

How to Play

  • Drag clouds to water grass and grow food for your sheep.
  • Tap sheep to check their hunger, happiness, energy, and health stats.
  • Merge clouds together to create bigger rain clouds or even storms.
  • Complete 20 challenges and earn stars to buy items from the shop.

Game Features

  • 🐑 Adorable sheep with unique names, moods, and personalities
  • ☁️ Interactive cloud and weather system with rain and storms
  • 🌱 Dynamic grass growth and flower blooming mechanics
  • 🏪 Item shop with toys, fountains, windmills, and fireworks
  • 📋 20 challenges to complete with star rewards

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cloud & Sheep free to play?

Yes! Cloud & Sheep is a free online sheep game that runs directly in your browser. No sign-up or install needed.

How do I make it rain in Cloud & Sheep?

Drag medium or large clouds over the grass area. Bigger clouds produce more rain. You can merge small clouds together to create larger ones.

Can my sheep have babies?

Yes! When two adult sheep are very happy (happiness above 75%), they may produce a baby sheep. Keep your flock happy and well-fed!
