How to Play

1Drag dice from the tray at the bottom onto any empty cell.

2Connect 3 or more dice of the same value to merge them into a higher value.

3Chain merges: the new die may connect with nearby dice of the new value, triggering another merge with bonus points.

4Keep space open: the game ends when you cannot place the current dice.

5Higher dice = more points. Merging 6 dice gives more per-die than merging 3.
