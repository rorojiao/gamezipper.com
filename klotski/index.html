How to Play Klotski

Klotski (华容道, "Huarong Pass") is a classic Chinese sliding-block puzzle. The board is a 4-column × 5-row grid. Your goal is to slide the large 2×2 gold block (Cao Cao) down to the bottom-center exit. Blocks can only slide horizontally or vertically into empty spaces — they cannot jump or rotate. There are four block types: the 2×2 Cao Cao block (gold), 1×2 vertical warriors (blue), 2×1 horizontal warriors (green), and 1×1 soldiers (gray). Drag any block in the direction you want it to move; if the path is clear, it slides one cell. Win by maneuvering Cao Cao to the center of the bottom row.

Tips & Strategy

Work backwards: figure out which soldiers and warriors must move out of Cao Cao's path before you slide it down. Clear space near the bottom exit early — you'll usually need both bottom-corner soldiers relocated. Don't waste moves shifting blocks you'll need again; every move counts toward your star rating. Use Undo to backtrack without resetting, and the Hint button performs an optimal next move via the built-in solver. Lower move counts earn more stars (par varies per level, from 72 to 200 moves).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game free?

Yes! Klotski is completely free to play in your browser with no download required.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes — drag blocks with your finger. The touch controls are fully optimized for phones and tablets.

How many levels?

30 handcrafted levels across 3 tiers of difficulty, each named after a famous Chinese stratagem (e.g. 横刀立马, 四面楚歌, 空城计). Par moves range from 72 to 200.

What is the goal?

Slide the 2×2 Cao Cao block to the bottom-center exit. Only the big block can escape — the smaller blocks are obstacles you must rearrange to clear its path.
