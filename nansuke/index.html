How to Play

Rules:
1. Fill all the white cells with numbers from the bank below the grid.
2. Each number runs either across (left-to-right) or down (top-to-bottom).
3. One digit per cell. Numbers cannot overlap incorrectly — crossing digits must match.
4. Every number is used exactly once.

Controls:
- Click a number in the bank to select it.
- Click a grid cell to place the selected number starting there (try across first; right-click forces down).
- Erase mode: click placed numbers to remove them.
- Keys: E=erase, H=hint, R=clear, Enter=check, Esc=menu.
