How to Play Chinese Checkers

Objective

Be the first player to move all 10 marbles from your starting triangle to the opposite triangle of the board.

Movement

  • Click a marble to select it
  • Move to any highlighted adjacent empty position (step move)
  • Or jump over an adjacent marble to an empty position beyond it (jump move)

Chain Jumps

  • After a jump, if another jump is possible from the landing position, you MUST continue jumping
  • Chain multiple jumps in one turn for strategic advantage
  • Unlike checkers, regular steps are optional - you can choose to move only one step

Winning

The first player to fill all 10 positions of their target (opposite) triangle wins!

Player Configurations

  • 2 players: opposite triangles
  • 3 players: alternating triangles
  • 4 players: two pairs of opposite triangles
  • 6 players: all triangles
