How to Play

Move Left/Right: Arrow keys or A / D

Jump: Space, W, or Up arrow (hold = higher)

Gravity Flip: Run into a wall to flip 90 degrees

Orbs: Collect for bonus score and star ratings

Gaps: Holes in the floor - fall through and die

Boosts: Green arrow tiles speed you up

Crumble: Purple tiles break after you step on them

Stars: Earn 1-3 stars per level based on orb collection

Mobile: tap left/right halves to move, tap center to jump
