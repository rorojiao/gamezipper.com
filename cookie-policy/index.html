Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

GameZipper uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your gaming experience. Here's what we use them for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. They enable core features like game loading, account preferences, and security.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language settings, recently played games, and game customizations.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our games and website through aggregated, anonymous data.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you play games to help us optimize loading times and fix bugs.

Types of Data We Collect

When you play games on GameZipper, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Game play sessions and duration
  • High scores and game progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Pages visited and links clicked
  • Referring website or search engine

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us (such as through a contact form).

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on GameZipper are set by third-party services we use, including:

  • Cloudflare: For security and performance optimization ( CDN cookies)
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting and delivery of our website

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies from ad networks, or social media tracking cookies on GameZipper.

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to save:

  • Your recently played games
  • Game settings and preferences
  • High scores and achievements

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you choose to share it.

Managing Your Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or restrict cookies through their settings. Check your browser's help menu for instructions.
  • Browser Extensions: Various privacy extensions can help you manage cookies across websites.
  • Local Storage: You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or settings.

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect how some games function on our site.

Do Not Track

GameZipper respects "Do Not Track" browser signals. When you enable Do Not Track, we will disable analytics and tracking cookies. Essential cookies required for site functionality will still be used.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
