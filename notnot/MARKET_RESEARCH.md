## Selected Candidate: Notnot (20/25)

### Market Data
- **Notnot** (Tushar Lobo): 5M+ downloads
- Core mechanic: Brain direction/Simon-says variant — watch arrow direction and respond accordingly
- Gameplay: Quick reaction, follow arrow cues to navigate or complete patterns
- Difficulty: Speed and accuracy ramp, requires high focus
- Monetization: Free with ads
- Audience: Casual, reflex, brain-training gamers

### Why Selected
- **Genuine gap**: Zero brain direction/Simon-says puzzle on GZ
- **High feasibility**: Arrow cues + tap/press input + timer = achievable in single-file HTML
- **Proven demand**: 5M+ downloads proves solid player interest
- **Endless content**: Procedural arrow patterns = unlimited levels
- **Reflex aesthetic**: Broad appeal for casual reflex games

### Scoring Criteria
- D (Demand): 4 — 5M+ downloads, sustained interest 2026
- S (SEO): 3 — "brain direction", "arrow puzzle", "reflex game" — decent keywords
- R (Retention): 3 — reflex sessions are short; replay via speed challenges
- F (Feasibility): 5 — arrow rendering, tap/click, timer, no complex physics
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — grep confirmed no brain direction/Simon-says variant
