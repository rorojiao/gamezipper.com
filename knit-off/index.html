How to Play

Objective

Fill all knitting bobbins with yarn of the matching color to complete each level!

How It Works

  • Tap a yarn knot at the TOP to move it to the waiting tray
  • Tap a yarn in the TRAY to send it to the matching color bobbin at the BOTTOM
  • Each bobbin needs a specific number of yarn pieces

Game Over

The game ends if the waiting tray fills up (7/7) with no matching bobbins available. Choose your yarn wisely!

Special Elements

  • Locked knots need an adjacent match to unlock (Tier 3+)
  • Frozen bobbins need 2 extra yarn to thaw (Tier 4+)

Boosters

  • Hint: Highlights the optimal next move
  • Undo: Reverses the last move
  • Shuffle: Randomizes yarn positions

Star Rating

Earn stars based on how efficiently you complete each level. 3 stars = perfect!
