How to Play

  1. Select a clue — Click any Across or Down clue to highlight that word in the grid.
  2. Type your answer — Letters fill in automatically from the selected cell.
  3. Navigate between words — Click another clue or use arrow keys to move.
  4. Check your progress — Correctly filled words highlight in green.
  5. Complete the puzzle — Fill every cell correctly to win!

💡 Tips for Solving Crosswords Faster

  • Start with the easiest clues — Fill in what you know first. These letters help you solve crossing words.
  • Look for short words — 3-4 letter words have fewer possibilities and are easier to guess.
  • Use crossing letters — If you know one word, the intersecting letters narrow down the crossing word significantly.
  • Think about word patterns — Common endings like -ING, -TION, -LY often appear in crossword puzzles.
  • Take breaks — Stepping away and coming back often helps you see answers you missed.

❓ Why Play Crossword Puzzles?

Crossword puzzles are one of the best brain training exercises. Research shows regular crossword solving improves vocabulary, memory, and cognitive function. Our free online crossword requires no download — play on your phone during commutes, on your tablet during breaks, or on your desktop at home.

Unlike newspaper crosswords, our digital version gives instant feedback — correctly completed words light up green so you always know you're on the right track. Perfect for both crossword beginners and experienced solvers.
