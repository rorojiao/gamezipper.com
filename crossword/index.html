About Crossword Puzzle

Crossword Puzzle is a classic word game you can play right in your browser — no download, no signup needed. Fill in the grid by solving clues for across and down words. New puzzles available every day, with multiple difficulty levels from easy to challenging.

Perfect for daily brain training, commutes, or breaks. Click or tap a clue to highlight that word, then type your answer. The game validates as you go and highlights correctly filled words.

How to Play

  • Click a clue to select that word (highlights the word in the grid)
  • Type your answer — letters appear in the grid cells
  • Navigate between words by clicking the next clue
  • Correctly filled words will be highlighted in green
  • Complete the entire puzzle to win
