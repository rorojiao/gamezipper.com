Bubble Pop — Free Online Bubble Shooter Puzzle

Bubble Pop is a colorful match-3 bubble shooter where you aim and fire bubbles to create groups of three or more matching colors. Pop clusters, clear the board, and advance through 20+ levels of bubble-bursting fun. Play free online — no download required!

How to Play Bubble Pop

  • Aim by moving your mouse or finger to set the direction. A guide line shows where your bubble will go.
  • Click or tap to fire the bubble toward the cluster above.
  • Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to pop them and clear them from the board.
  • Popping a cluster also removes any bubbles hanging below it — aim for big chain reactions!
  • Don't let the bubbles reach the bottom line or it's game over!

Tips & Strategy

  • Look for bank shots — bounce bubbles off the side walls to reach hard-to-hit spots.
  • Target anchor bubbles at the top — popping them drops everything hanging below for massive points.
  • Check the next bubble preview (usually shown at the bottom) and plan two shots ahead.
  • Don't waste bubbles on small matches. Wait for opportunities to create big clusters.
  • Clear from the top down — this creates the biggest cascading clears.

Yes! Bubble Pop is 100% free to play online in your browser. No download, no signup — just start popping.

Absolutely — the game supports touch controls. Just drag to aim and tap to shoot.
