How to Play

Goal. Get every passenger onto a bus that matches their color. When a bus fills up, it rolls away and you score points.

Tap. Tap a passenger in the line and they walk to the bus of the same color.

Plan. Each bus has limited seats. Think ahead so you never run out of room.

Boosters. Stuck? Use Add Slot to grow a bus, Undo to take back a move, or Sort to reshuffle the queue.

Stars. Finish with the fewest buses used to earn three stars.

Endless. Clear all of tier 3 to unlock the procedural Endless mode.
