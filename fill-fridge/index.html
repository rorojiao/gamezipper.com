Fill The Fridge - Free Online Fridge Packing Puzzle

Fill The Fridge is a satisfying organization puzzle where you drag grocery items into a refrigerator and try to fit everything perfectly. Think of it as Tetris meets real-life fridge packing — every item has a unique shape, and your job is to arrange them all without any overlaps.

How to Play

  • Drag items from the staging area at the bottom of the screen
  • Move them over the fridge grid to see a placement preview
  • Release to snap the item into place on the grid
  • Fit every single item into the fridge to complete the level
  • Use Undo to remove the last item you placed
  • Use Hint to highlight where an item can fit
  • Use Shuffle to rearrange the item order

Game Features

  • 20 handcrafted levels across four difficulty tiers
  • Easy levels with simple rectangles and generous fridge space
  • Expert levels with L-shapes, T-shapes, S-shapes, and tight packing
  • Smooth drag-and-drop with snap-to-grid precision
  • Ghost preview shows valid and invalid placements
  • Star rating system based on packing efficiency
  • Particle effects and celebration animations
  • Undo, Hint, and Shuffle tools to help you solve each puzzle
  • Progress saved automatically in your browser
  • Works on desktop and mobile with touch controls

Tips and Tricks

  • Start with the largest items first — they are hardest to place later
  • Place tall items along the edges of the fridge to maximize center space
  • Use the hint button when stuck — it highlights one valid placement
  • The undo button only removes the last item, so think before you place
  • Shuffle rearranges items but does not change the fridge — try different orders
  • In harder levels, look for natural slots where L-shapes and T-shapes interlock

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fill The Fridge?

Fill The Fridge is a free browser puzzle game where you drag and drop grocery items of different shapes into a fridge grid. The goal is to fit every item without any overlaps.

Can I play Fill The Fridge on my phone?

Yes. Fill The Fridge works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Touch controls let you drag items with your finger on mobile devices.

How do I get more hints?

You earn bonus hints by completing levels with a 3-star rating. You start with 9 hints and gain one each time you earn three stars on a level.

What happens when I shuffle?

Shuffle removes all placed items from the fridge and randomizes their order in the staging area. This helps you try different packing strategies.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, your level progress and star ratings are saved in your browser's local storage automatically. You can pick up where you left off anytime.

Strategy Guide

Fill The Fridge rewards spatial thinking. Like a real Tetris game, the key is visualizing where each shape fits before you commit. Start by scanning all items and identifying the largest or most awkward shapes. Place those first, then fill in the gaps with smaller items. The grid-based snapping means you never have to worry about pixel-perfect placement — just focus on which cells each shape occupies.

Who Is This Game For?

Fill The Fridge is perfect for fans of organization games, spatial puzzles, and Tetris-style packing challenges. If you enjoy satisfying visuals, clean UI, and the feeling of perfectly fitting everything into place, this game is for you. It's also great for short sessions — most levels take under two minutes.
