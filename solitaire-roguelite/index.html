How to Play

Goal

Defeat all 18 monsters by playing Klondike solitaire. Each card placed on a foundation deals 1 damage to the enemy.

Damage

  • Move card to foundation: 1 damage to enemy
  • Recycle stock pile: 1 damage to you
  • Complete the deal: bonus damage
  • Run out of recycles (3): take remaining enemy HP as damage

Items

After each victory, choose 1 of 3 items. Items modify solitaire rules - flip extra cards, place wild cards, undo moves, and more.

Upgrades

Spend gold between runs on permanent upgrades: more HP, starting items, and bigger stock piles.

Winning

Defeat all 18 monsters across 3 biomes to complete the dungeon.
