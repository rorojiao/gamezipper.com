How to Play Hidato

Goal: Fill every empty cell with a number from 1 to N such that consecutive numbers (1→2→3...→N) form a single connected chain. Each number must be in a cell that touches the previous number — orthogonal (up/down/left/right) or diagonal.

  1. Find the endpoints. The numbers 1 (smallest) and N (largest) are always pre-filled. These are your chain's start and finish.
  2. Tap an empty cell to open the number picker. Choose the number that extends the chain from the last placed number.
  3. 8-direction adjacency: cells touch if they share a side or a corner — so you can move diagonally to escape tight spots.
  4. Use hints when stuck. You start each level with 3 hints. The hint fills one cell with the correct number.
  5. Win condition: When all cells are filled and the chain 1→N is continuous (no broken links), you win! Earn up to 3 stars based on time and hint usage.

Tip: Count the cells between the largest placed number and N. That tells you the minimum chain length you still need to lay down.
