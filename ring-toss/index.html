How to Play

1. Aim: Touch or click near the ring launcher at the bottom and drag backward to set your aim.

2. Power: The further you pull back, the harder the throw. A dotted line shows your trajectory.

3. Throw: Release to launch the ring. It arcs with gravity and may be affected by wind.

4. Score: Land rings on pegs to earn points. Reach the target score to win!
