## USP (Unique Selling Point)
Physics-based ring toss puzzle with:
1. Realistic ballistic trajectory (gravity + arc prediction)
2. Pegs with point values and ring-stacking mechanics
3. 30 handcrafted levels across 5 tiers
4. Obstacles: walls, moving pegs, wind, multi-ring levels

## Core Mechanics
- **Throw**: Drag backward to aim (slingshot style), release to throw
- **Trajectory**: Parabolic arc with gravity, dotted prediction line
- **Pegs**: Rings land on pegs. Each peg has a point value (1-50pts)
- **Objective**: Reach target score by landing rings on pegs
- **Stacking**: Multiple rings can stack on one peg (bonus points)
- **Stars**: 3★ (target×1.5), 2★ (target×1.2), 1★ (target met)

## Tier Progression
- T1 (L1-6): Simple pegs, 3-5 rings, static field
- T2 (L7-12): Point-multiplier pegs, 5-7 rings
- T3 (L13-18): Obstacles (walls), wind effects
- T4 (L19-24): Moving pegs, limited ring count
- T5 (L25-30): Multi-ring levels, high-value targets, complex layouts

## Visual Design
- Dark carnival theme: navy background (#0a0e27) with neon accents
- Rings: glowing cyan/magenta/green circles
- Pegs: wooden posts with glowing tips
- Particle effects on successful ring landing
- Trajectory preview: dotted cyan line
