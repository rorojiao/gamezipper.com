## Tier 1 — Round 4 New Gaps (≥18/25 required)

### 1. One Line Puzzle (Connect the Dots) — Score: 23/25 ✅ COMPLETED
- **市场需求 (5/5)**: Rising trend 2026, "Line Puzzle: Connect Dots 2026" on App Store + TapTap, 1Line 50K+ downloads, classic universally understood mechanic
- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "one line puzzle", "connect dots puzzle", "draw one line", "dot puzzle game" — zero GZ coverage, very high search volume
- **留存潜力 (4/5)**: Hundreds of levels possible, satisfying "connect everything" mechanic, quick sessions
- **实现可行性 (4/5)**: Grid-based dot placement, line drawing, no-crossing constraint — well-defined algorithm, very doable in Canvas
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: Flow Connect = connect matching color pairs with pipes. One Line = draw a single continuous line through ALL dots on the grid. Completely different mechanic
**Slug**: `one-line-puzzle`
**Target**: 50+ levels, single continuous line through all dots, no crossing, hint system, star rating

- **留存潜力 (3/5)**: Creative puzzles, multiple solutions, but may be niche
- **实现可行性 (3/5)**: Physics engine for drawn shapes, collision detection — complex but possible in Canvas
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: No drawing-based physics puzzle in GZ at all
**Slug**: `physics-draw-puzzle`
