## Selected Candidate: Arrow Block Escape (24/25)

### Market Data
- **Tap Away 3D** (Popcore): Part of series with 250M+ combined installs
- **Arrow Cube Escape** (GOODROID, Inc.): Released 2025, currently trending in Radar #33; "directional block removal with color-sorting mechanics"
- **Block Out - Tap Away**: 1000+ levels, very popular 2025-2026
- **Tap Block Away 3D**: Top Master Edition, 2026 hit
- Genre: 3D directional-block tap puzzle — rotate the cube, find unblocked arrows, tap to slide cubes out, clear the board within move limits

### Core Mechanic
- A 3D cube composed of small unit cubes (e.g. 4×4×4 or 5×5×5)
- Each unit cube has an arrow on top pointing one of 4 directions (N/S/E/W)
- Tap a cube → it slides (slides repeatedly) in its arrow direction until it leaves the stack or hits another cube
- A cube can only be tapped if there is open space immediately in its arrow direction
- Goal: clear all cubes within move limit / before overflow
- Modern variant (Arrow Cube Escape): cubes have COLORS, must also match target color sorting for bonus

### Competitor Benchmarks
- **Arrow Cube Escape** (GOODROID, 2026) — directional block removal + color sort
- **Tap Away 3D** (Popcore) — genre-defining classic
- **Block Out - Tap Away** (2026) — 1000+ levels, color matching

### Scoring
- D (Demand): 5 — Popcore 250M+, multiple 2026 hit clones, dominant genre
- S (SEO): 5 — "tap away 3d", "arrow cube escape", "block puzzle 3d" all zero GZ coverage
- R (Retention): 5 — thousands of levels possible, satisfying tactile feedback, daily challenge potential
- F (Feasibility): 4 — Isometric/2.5D Canvas achievable; arrow direction + slide logic is more complex than flat puzzle but doable in single-file HTML
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — strict grep confirms zero tap-away / arrow-block / cube-escape games in GZ (Block Out is 3D Tetris, different)

### Implementation Plan
- Slug: `/arrow-block-escape/`
- 30 levels across 5 tiers (3×3×3 → 5×5×5 → mixed shapes)
- Isometric Canvas rendering (2.5D for performance)
- Per-cube arrow direction + color
- Tap to slide: cube animates out in arrow direction
- Move counter + star rating
- Power-ups: Hint (highlight tappable cube), Bomb (remove any cube), Rotate (free camera rotate)
- localStorage v1 save (save_progress)
- Procedural BGM via Web Audio + 8 SFX (tap, slide, clear, complete, fail, hint, level-up, victory)
- Cleanup, responsive mobile+desktop, touch-action:none, full S-grade systems
