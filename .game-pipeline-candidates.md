## Candidates

### 1. Lights Out (Puzzle Logic)
**Mechanic:** Click tiles to toggle themselves and adjacent tiles; turn all lights off
**Competitor:** "Lights Out Puzzle" (1M+ downloads on Play Store)
**Score Breakdown:**
- Market demand: 4/5 (classic logic puzzle, steady downloads)
- Uniqueness: 5/5 (not on GameZipper)
- Browser viability: 5/5 (simple grid, Canvas, pure logic)
- Development complexity: 5/5 (straightforward, 5 tiers × 5 levels = 25 levels)
- Monetization potential: 4/5 (interstitial ads, hint power-ups)

**TOTAL SCORE: 23/25 ✅ PASS**

**Implementation:**
- Grid sizes: 3×3 (Beginner), 4×4 (Easy), 5×5 (Medium), 6×6 (Hard), 7×7 (Expert), 8×8 (Master)
- Procedural generation: Guaranteed solvable via Gaussian elimination on GF(2)
- Difficulty: Increase grid size + minimum moves required
- Features: Hint (show next move), Undo, Level select, 3-star rating

**Competitor:** "Minesweeper Classic" (5M+ downloads, 50+ years history)
**Score Breakdown:**
- Market demand: 5/5 (iconic, massive global audience)
- Uniqueness: 5/5 (not on GameZipper)
- Browser viability: 5/5 (grid-based, simple state)
- Development complexity: 4/5 (logic simple, but requires flood-fill algorithm)
- Monetization potential: 4/5 (high playthrough rate, ad breaks)

**TOTAL SCORE: 23/25 ✅ PASS**

**Implementation:**
