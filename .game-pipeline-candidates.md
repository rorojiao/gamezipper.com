## Selected Candidate: Rope Physics / Rescue (24/25)

### Market Data
- **Developer**: Supersonic Studios
- **Downloads**: 90M+ on Google Play
- **Rating**: 4.5 stars, 500K+ reviews
- **Core mechanic**: Manipulate/tie ropes to rescue targets (different from cutting). Players drag rope ends to tie knots, stretch ropes to reach targets, and manage rope tension with weight physics. Rope can be anchored to pegs, wrapped around obstacles, and adjusted to guide the rescue object to safety.
- **Why Selected**:
  - 90M+ downloads — massive proven demand
  - ZERO overlap — Rope Rescue mechanic is DISTINCT from Cut the Rope (tie/manipulate vs cut)
  - Perfect Canvas fit — physics + rope mechanics already proven in GZ ecosystem
  - Highly addictive — rope physics creates satisfying "aha!" moments
  - Strong SEO potential ("rope game", "rope rescue puzzle", "rope physics game")
  - Code reuse — Cut the Rope Verlet physics can be adapted

### Core Mechanic Difference
- **Cut the Rope**: CUT ropes to let objects fall through gravity physics
- **Rope Rescue**: TIE/MANIPULATE ropes to rescue targets, rope stretching, weight distribution, knot tying

### Competitor Benchmarks
- **Rope Rescue** (Supersonic Studios, 90M+, 4.5★, Top 15 Puzzle)
- **Cut the Rope** (ZeptoLab, 1B+, 4.7★, #1 Puzzle) — Similar physics but different mechanic
