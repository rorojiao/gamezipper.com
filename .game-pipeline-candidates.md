## Candidate Details

### 1. Grass Master (21/25) — SELECTED
- **Mobile data**: SayGames, #17 free Google Play US (June 2026)
- **Mechanic**: Swipe to cut grass from a grid field. The field has patches of grass in different heights/colors. Player swipes in straight lines to mow the grass, revealing hidden items beneath. ASMR-style satisfying cutting animation with grass particles.
- **Why**: Completely unique mechanic (zero overlap — no grass cutting game on GZ), highly satisfying gameplay loop, simple Canvas implementation (grid + particles), proven SayGames demand.
- **Comparable GZ games**: Sand Balls (physics dig — similar satisfaction), Slice Master (cutting — different mechanic)
- **Core loop**: Swipe to cut → clear grass → reveal item → next field. Simple, addictive, "one more level" feel.
- **SEO keywords**: grass cutting game, lawn mower puzzle, grass master, grass cutter, lawn mowing game

### 2. Beads Out (20/25)

- **Why**: Voodoo hit, unique sequence mechanic, zero overlap.

### 3. Pocket Sort (20/25)

- **Why**: Two proven mechanics combined, SayGames backing, simple grid.
