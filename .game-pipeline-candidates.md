## Selected Candidate: Hex Haven — Dorfromantik-style Hex Tile Landscape Builder (24/25)

### Market Data
- **Dorfromantik** (Toukana Interactive, 2022): 50K+ Steam reviews, "98% Positive", Gamescom 2022 award, BAFTA nominee
- **Islanders** (GrizzlyGames, 2019): 30K+ Steam reviews, "97% Positive"
- **Pan'orama, Tiledom** (clones): millions of mobile downloads combined
- **2026 status**: Dorfromantik launched on PS5/Xbox in 2025-2026, STILL being reviewed as a top cozy puzzler ("quiet, confident yes" — spawningpoint.com 2026 review)
- Core mechanic: Place hex tiles on a hex grid; tile edges must match adjacent tiles (forest/field/water/town/train); complete quest objectives for bonus tiles and score
- Monetization: paid game ($15) but mobile clones are free with ads — perfect fit for GZ free model
- Audience: cozy/zen/mindfulness gamers — exactly GZ's core

### Why Selected
- **Massive genuine gap**: zero hex-tile-landscape or tile-placement-scoring puzzle on GZ (hex-block is block-fitting, hexa-sort is liquid-sorting, hex-minesweeper is minesweeper — all different)
- **Highest score (24/25)** of any candidate this round
- **Proven demand**: 4 years on market, still charting, 80K+ reviews across originals
- **Endless procedural content**: hex tile + quest generation = unlimited levels
- **Perfect fit for GZ aesthetic**: zen, relaxing, beautiful procedural landscapes
- **Feasibility**: hex grid + drag-drop + edge-match + sprite tiles = well-understood Canvas pattern

### Scoring Criteria
- D (Demand): 5 — 4-year charting presence, BAFTA/Gamescom, 80K+ reviews
- S (SEO): 5 — "hex puzzle", "tile puzzle", "landscape builder", "cozy puzzle" — all trending, zero GZ coverage
- R (Retention): 5 — endless procedural, daily challenge mode, creative sandbox mode
- F (Feasibility): 4 — hex grid math + edge matching + tile sprites; medium complexity
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — grep confirmed no landscape/tile-builder/edge-matching game
