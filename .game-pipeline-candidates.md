### Rule Set
- **Deck:** Standard 52 cards, shuffled, dealt evenly to 2-4 players
- **Turn:** Players take turns flipping top card onto center pile
- **Face Card Challenge:**
  - Ace → next player gets 4 chances
  - King → 3 chances
  - Queen → 2 chances
  - Jack → 1 chance
  - If challenger fails → face-card player takes the pile
  - If challenger plays a face card → challenge transfers
- **Slap Rules (win pile):**
  - Doubles: two same-rank cards in a row
  - Sandwiches: same rank with exactly one card between
  - Tens: two consecutive cards summing to 10
  - Marriage: King + Queen adjacent
  - Four in a row: ascending or descending sequence
  - Top Bottom: first card of pile matches top card
- **Win:** Collect all cards
- **Burn penalty:** Slap on invalid combo → forfeit one card to bottom of pile
