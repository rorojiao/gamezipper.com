## Round 48 Market Research (2026-06-27) — FRESH
Queue was empty after Portal Bounce (#447); mandatory Phase 0 research run.
Exhaustive gap audit against 448-game catalog: checked 300+ keywords across every
puzzle category (sorting, merge, physics, drawing, logic, word, spatial, Nikoli,
matching, slide/push, color, construction, memory, stealth, time, temperature,
perspective, wire/fuse, catapult, mosaic, textile, goods/shelf, conveyor, domino,
gear, lever, balloon, etc.). Most categories are heavily saturated (10+ games each).

1. **Blind Spot (line-of-sight block-placement puzzle)** — 27/25 ✅ SELECTED
   - Market gap 9: `grep -ic 'line.of.sight\|vision.*block\|stealth.*puzzle\|camouflage\|conceal' games-data.js` = 1 (Thief Puzzle, which is maze/pathfinding NOT block-placement). The vision-blocking/barrier-placement mechanic is COMPLETELY uncovered. Light Beam Puzzle is the OPPOSITE (redirect beams to hit targets). No game hides targets FROM guards.
   - Search volume 4: "blind spot", "line of sight puzzle", "stealth puzzle", "hide and seek" are solid evergreen terms with low competition for "blind spot puzzle free online".
   - Monetization 5: Level-based, ultra-short satisfying sessions (plan→place→solve in 10-30s), "aha!" moment per level, ideal for interstitial between levels + rewarded "show solution" / "skip" ads.
   - Feasibility 4: Grid-based Canvas (7x7 to 10x10), deterministic ray-casting for vision (Bresenham/DDA), block drag-and-drop, 30 hand-designed levels. Pure logic puzzle, no physics needed. Single-file friendly.
   - Uniqueness 5: Vision-blocking/barrier-placement is mechanically distinct from ANY existing GZ game. Not a clone of Light Beam (redirect), Lights Out (toggle), Thief Puzzle (pathfinding), Unblock Me (slide), or any construction/sorting game.
   - Slug: blind-spot · Tier 1 · cat: puzzle · emoji: 👁️
