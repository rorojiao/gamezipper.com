## Selected Candidate: Penalty Shootout — Soccer Penalty Shootout (23/25)

### Market Data
- **Penalty Shooters / Penalty Shooters 2** (Poki): Top-50 most played game on Poki, millions of plays/month. Alternating shooter+goalkeeper format.
- **Soccer Star** (Miniclip): 100M+ downloads on mobile. Penalty minigame is core engagement.
- **Penalty Champs / Euro Cup Penalty**: Consistently trending during tournaments, huge viral spikes during World Cup/Euro.
- **8 Ball Pool** (Miniclip) for context: proves physics-based sports games work on web.
- **Penalty shootout is the #1 uncovered sports category on GZ.**

### Why Selected (23/25 — highest of R40)
- **D (Demand) 5**: Soccer is world's #1 sport. Penalty games have 100M+ downloads collectively. Always-trending during tournaments.
- **S (SEO) 4**: "penalty shootout game", "penalty kick game", "soccer penalty" — high search volume, zero GZ coverage.
- **R (Retention) 4**: Quick 30s rounds, tournament bracket progression, alternating shoot/save roles = deep engagement.
- **F (Feasibility) 5**: Ball trajectory (angle+power+curve), goalkeeper dive animation, simple physics. Very achievable in single-file Canvas.
- **Z (Zero-overlap) 5**: Strict grep: penalty=0, soccer=0, football=0 across 302 games.

### Gameplay Design
- 2D side/perspective view of goal with goalkeeper
- **Shooting phase**: Player drags from ball to aim (direction + power), can add curve/spin
- **Saving phase**: Player controls goalkeeper, taps a corner to dive
- Alternating roles: 5 penalty rounds each, sudden death if tied
- Tournament mode: Pick a national team → group stage → knockout → final
- AI opponent: shoots and saves with adjustable difficulty
- Satisfying feedback: ball physics, net ripple, crowd cheer SFX, goal celebration
