## Selected Candidate: Flood Fill — Color Spread Puzzle (22/25)

### Market Data
- **Flood-It!** (LabPix Games): One of the most popular browser puzzle games of all time, millions of plays
- **Color Spread** (multiple clones): Combined 100M+ downloads on Google Play/App Store
- **Mad Virus** (Namco): Classic grid infection game, still charting
- **Bubble Blast Color** (Voodoo/Tap to Play): Top-rated on mobile charts 2024-2026
- Core mechanic: Start from top-left corner (or center), pick a color, all connected cells of your territory change to that color and absorb adjacent same-color cells. Goal: fill the entire board in minimum moves.
- Monetization: Free with ads — perfect for GZ model
- Audience: casual puzzle fans — exactly GZ's core demographic

### Why Selected
- **Genuine gap**: Zero flood-fill game on GZ (color-cascade is conveyor-shooter, chain-reaction is explosion-based, two-dots is connect-dots)
- **Highest score (22/25)** of any candidate this round
- **Proven demand**: 30+ years of popularity, still charting on app stores
- **Endless procedural content**: Seeded grid generation = unlimited levels
- **Perfect fit for GZ**: Simple mechanics, satisfying animations, zen aesthetic
- **Feasibility**: Trivial Canvas implementation (grid + flood fill algorithm)
- **High retention**: Time attack, daily challenge, endless mode

### Scoring
- D (Demand): 4 — Classic puzzle, consistently charting, 50M+ combined downloads
- S (SEO): 4 — "flood fill", "color spread", "color fill puzzle" — trending search terms, zero GZ coverage
- R (Retention): 4 — Endless procedural, daily challenge, time attack
- F (Feasibility): 5 — Trivial Canvas implementation
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — Grep confirmed no flood/color-spread/territory-expansion game
