## Tier 1 — CRITICAL Gaps (≥18/25 required)

### 1. Checkers (Draughts) — Score: 24/25 ✅ COMPLETED
- **市场需求 (5/5)**: #1 most-played browser game on PlayBrain (123 sessions in 30 days), 100M+ Android downloads, universally known game
- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "checkers", "checkers online", "draughts", "play checkers free" — zero GZ coverage, extremely high search volume
- **留存潜力 (5/5)**: Classic strategy game, infinite replayability, AI difficulty levels, quick sessions perfect for ads
- **实现可行性 (4/5)**: Grid-based game, well-defined rules, AI opponent (minimax), Canvas rendering — very doable
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: No board strategy game in GZ at all. Chess exists but completely different game
**Slug**: `checkers`
**Target**: AI opponent (3 difficulty levels), online-style moves, king pieces, mandatory captures, 3 difficulty tiers

---

### 2. Bubble Shooter — Score: 23/25
- **市场需求 (5/5)**: 500M+ all-time downloads, consistently top-10 puzzle game, classic casual genre
- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "bubble shooter", "bubble pop", "bubble blast" — zero GZ coverage for this specific mechanic (aim + shoot)
- **留存潜力 (5/5)**: Hundreds of levels, satisfying pop mechanics, color matching, very addictive
- **实现可行性 (3/5)**: Physics-based bubble trajectory, grid management, wall bouncing — moderately complex but doable
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: Bubble Pop in GZ is a tap-to-pop game, not aim-and-shoot bubble shooter
**Slug**: `bubble-shooter`
**Target**: Aim-and-shoot mechanic, bubble grid, wall bouncing, color matching, 30+ levels

---

### 3. Brain Out / Tricky Puzzle — Score: 22/25 ✅ COMPLETED
- **市场需求 (5/5)**: 100M+ downloads, viral social media appeal, "think outside the box" genre
- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "brain out", "brain test", "tricky puzzle", "trick questions" — zero GZ coverage
- **留存潜力 (4/5)**: Each puzzle is unique, players share funny solutions, strong word-of-mouth
- **实现可行性 (3/5)**: Each level needs unique creative puzzle design — requires more design effort but single-file doable
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: Impossible Quiz = trivia/knowledge questions. Brain Out = lateral thinking, physics manipulation, creative solutions
**Slug**: `brain-out`
**Target**: 30+ creative "think outside the box" puzzles, shake/rotate/tap creatively, funny answers

### 4. Mahjong Solitaire — Score: 22/25 ✅ COMPLETED
- **市场需求 (5/5)**: One of the largest web game categories ever, featured on CrazyGames, millions of daily players
- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "mahjong", "mahjong solitaire", "free mahjong" — zero GZ coverage, enormous search volume
- **留存潜力 (4/5)**: Relaxing, infinite layouts, daily challenges, very high session length
- **实现可行性 (3/5)**: Tile matching with layout generation, free-tile detection algorithm, timer — complex but established patterns
- **零重叠 (5/5)**: No tile-matching solitaire game in GZ. Tile Dynasty is a different game.
