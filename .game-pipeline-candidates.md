1. **Delete One Part (DOP-style erase-to-solve brain puzzle)** — 22/25 ✅ SELECTED
   - Market gap 9: SayGames' "DOP: Delete One Part" (and the wider DOP franchise) is a 100M+
     download hyper-casual brain-puzzle phenomenon — erase part of a picture to solve a riddle
     ("erase the water so the fish can swim", "erase the wall to find the key"). The erase-to-
     reveal mechanic is COMPLETELY uncovered on GZ: `draw-one-part` (draw verb) is a different
     mechanic, and grep for erase/scratch/rub/reveal/dop/delete-one returns ZERO across the
     entire catalog. The closest siblings are uncover-the-picture style but none use the
     drag-to-erase interaction. Genuinely uncovered, high-demand mechanic.
   - Search volume 4: "dop game", "delete one part", "erase puzzle", "brain puzzle", "draw
     one part" — strong steady evergreen terms; the DOP brand alone drives millions of searches.
   - Monetization 5: Level-based, ultra-short satisfying sessions (5-15s each), "aha!" moment
     per level drives binge replay, perfect for interstitial between levels + rewarded "show
     solution" / "skip" ads. Top DOP titles monetize at industry-leading eCPM.
   - Feasibility 2: Canvas scene drawing (procedural primitives — no per-level image assets) +
     erase mask (globalCompositeOperation='destination-out' on an overlay layer) + per-level
     target-zone solve detection. Moderate: the bulk of work is hand-designing 30 distinct
     scenes + target zones, but each scene is simple shapes. Single-file friendly.
   - Uniqueness 2: Erase-to-reveal is mechanically distinct from draw-to-complete (draw-one-
     part), scratch-card (none on GZ), and any existing puzzle. The "delete the WRONG part to
     fix the picture" riddle framing is unique.
   - Slug: delete-one-part · Tier 1 · cat: puzzle · emoji: 🧽
   - Competitor features to match: drag-to-erase interaction (finger/mouse), 30 handcrafted
     riddle levels (6 tiers × 5) escalating from "erase the obstacle" → "erase to reveal" →
     "erase the wrong part" → "precision erase" → "erase to free/connect" → "trick riddles",
     star ratings by erase efficiency (minimal wrong-erases), hints (highlight target zone),
     undo (restore erase layer), level select with progress save, achievements, sound +
     procedural music, save progress (localStorage), mobile-first touch + desktop mouse.
