## Selected Candidate: Seating Puzzle (21/25)

### Market Data
- **Trend**: #2 Best Puzzle Mobile Games 2026 (gaming.net)
- **Inspiration**: "Is This Seat Taken?" (Jaleo), "Perfect Tidy" organization puzzles
- **Genre**: Cozy constraint-satisfaction puzzle (massive 2024-2026 trend)
- **Core mechanic**: Arrange guests around dinner tables based on their preferences/constraints. Each guest has rules: "won't sit next to enemies", "must face the window", "wants to be near the host", etc. Satisfy ALL constraints to complete the level.
- **Why Selected**:
  - Trending top puzzle category 2026
  - ZERO overlap — no constraint-seating game on GZ
  - Perfect Canvas fit — grid/table-based puzzle, drag-and-drop
  - Highly satisfying gameplay — cozy + logic
  - Easy procedural level generation
  - Strong SEO ("seating puzzle", "seat arrangement puzzle")
  - Broad appeal — cozy puzzle genre is dominant on mobile

### Competitor Benchmarks
- **Is This Seat Taken?** (Jaleo) — #2 puzzle mobile 2026
- **A Little to the Left** (Max Infer) — Top 6 puzzle 2026
- **Unpacking** (Witch Beam) — award-winning organization puzzle

## Scoring Criteria
- D (Demand): Downloads/rankings (5=100M+, 3=top ranked)
- S (SEO): Search volume × GZ zero coverage
- R (Retention): Session length / addictiveness
- F (Feasibility): Single-file HTML5 Canvas achievable
- Z (Zero-overlap): No similar core mechanic on GZ (verified against all 329 games)
