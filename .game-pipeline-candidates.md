1. **Magnet Drop (physics magnet-ball-into-goal puzzle)** — 25/25 ✅ SELECTED
   - Market gap 8: Magnet physics puzzles (launch/roll a steel ball through a field
     of attracting + repelling magnets to reach the goal cup) are a proven
     CrazyGames / magnetgames.net trend ("Magnetism" = silver ball to landing cup
     surrounded by magnets). GameZipper's `/magnets/` is a Nikoli +/- logic grid
     puzzle (different genre) and `grow-worm/` only uses magnets as a feature — no
     pure physics magnet-ball course exists. Genuinely uncovered mechanic.
   - Search volume 4: "magnet game", "magnetism", "magnet puzzle", "magnet ball",
     "steel ball magnet" — steady evergreen terms.
   - Monetization 4: Level-based, short satisfying sessions, "reach the goal"
     objective creates replay compulsion, perfect for interstitial between levels
     + rewarded ads (extra balls / hints / skip).
   - Feasibility 4: Canvas + gravity ball + magnet force (inverse-square
     attract/repel) + obstacle course + launch mechanic + 30 levels. Standard 2D
     physics, no per-level art assets (drawn shapes), single-file friendly.
   - Zero-overlap 5: The launch-ball + navigate-magnet-field + reach-goal mechanic
     is mechanically unique. `magnets` = logic grid (no physics); `gravity-drop`,
     `gravity-orbit`, `plinko`, `pin-master` are all gravity/pinball without
     magnet polarity forces. IP-safe (generic "Magnet Drop").
   - Slug: magnet-drop · Tier 1 · cat: puzzle · emoji: 🧲
   - Competitor features to match: drag-to-aim launch of a steel ball (trajectory
     preview), gravity + friction physics, attractor magnets (pull) and repulsor
     magnets (push) with field visualization, fixed obstacles/walls, teleporters/
     boost pads, goal cup (ball must land inside), star ratings by ball count,
     30 handcrafted levels (6 tiers × 5), level select, hints, achievements,
     sound + procedural music, save progress.
