- **SEO 空白 (5/5)**: "flow free", "connect dots puzzle", "pipe puzzle" — zero GameZipper coverage, high search volume
- **留存潜力 (5/5)**: Hundreds of levels, daily puzzles, perfect for short sessions, extremely addictive
