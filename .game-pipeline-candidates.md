## Selected Candidate: Chroma Zen (22/25)

### Market Data
- **I Love Hue** (Zut!): 10M+ downloads, 4.7★ Google Play, Apple Design Award 2017
- **I Love Hue Too**: Sequel, 5M+ downloads, even more polished
- Core mechanic: Arrange scrambled colored tiles into a perfect smooth color gradient
- Gameplay: Tap two tiles to swap them; reconstruct the original gradient
- Difficulty: More scrambled tiles = harder levels; larger grids = harder
- Monetization: Free with ads, premium unlock for unlimited daily levels
- Audience: Casual, zen, mindfulness gamers — very broad appeal

### Why Selected
- **Genuine gap**: Zero color-arrangement/gradient puzzle on GZ (stained-glass is color logic, color-sort is liquid sorting, paint-splash is action)
- **High feasibility**: Grid + HSL color interpolation + swap mechanic = very achievable in single-file Canvas
- **Proven demand**: 10M+ downloads proves massive audience
- **Endless content**: Procedural gradient generation = unlimited levels
- **Zen aesthetic**: Perfect for GZ's casual puzzle audience

### Scoring Criteria
- D (Demand): 4 — 10M+ downloads, Apple Design Award, still charting 2026
- S (SEO): 4 — "color puzzle", "hue puzzle", "gradient game" — trending, zero GZ coverage
- R (Retention): 4 — endless procedural levels, daily challenges, zen mode
- F (Feasibility): 5 — grid of tiles, HSL interpolation, tap-to-swap, Canvas rendering. Very straightforward.
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — grep confirmed no gradient/color-arrangement game
