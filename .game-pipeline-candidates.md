## Selected Candidate: Gear Logic Puzzle — Mechanical Gear Placement Brain Teaser (23/25)

### Market Data
- **Gears Logic** (mobile, 10M+ downloads): Place gears on grid to connect motor to target. Top-rated brain puzzle.
- **Clockwork Brain Training**: Gear-based cognitive puzzle series.
- **Incredible Machine / Contraption Maker**: Rube Goldberg mechanical puzzle lineage.
- Gear/cogwheel mechanism puzzles are a proven brain-teaser category with high engagement.
- SEO greenfield: "gear puzzle", "logic gear", "mechanism puzzle" — high search volume, zero GZ coverage.

### Why Selected
- **Highest score (23/25)** of any R28 candidate
- **Massive genuine gap**: zero gear/cog/mechanism games on GZ (grep confirmed)
- **Proven demand**: Gear Logic mobile game has 10M+ downloads, still charting
- **Deep retention**: 20+ levels with progressive complexity (multiple targets, obstacles, rotation direction requirements, limited inventory)
- **SEO greenfield**: "gear puzzle" / "logic gear" — high volume, zero GZ coverage
- **Feasibility**: Grid placement + rotation animation + power propagation = clean single-file Canvas

### Gameplay Design
- Grid board (6x6 to 8x8 scaling with difficulty)
- Motor gear at fixed position (source, always spinning clockwise)
- Target gear(s) at fixed position(s) (must spin to complete)
- Player places gears from inventory onto empty cells
- Gears of different sizes (S=1cell, M=2cells, L=3cells diameter)
- Adjacent gears mesh when teeth align → rotation transfers
- Power propagates: motor → connected chain → target
- When ALL targets spin, level complete
- Difficulty curve: more targets, obstacle blocks, rotation direction requirements, limited gear inventory, larger boards

### Scoring
- D (Demand): 4 — 10M+ downloads reference, proven brain puzzle category
- S (SEO): 5 — entire gear/mechanism genre uncovered on GZ, high search volume
- R (Retention): 4 — 20+ levels, progressive difficulty, satisfying mechanical feedback
- F (Feasibility): 5 — grid + rotation + propagation = clean Canvas implementation
- Z (Zero-overlap): 5 — strict grep = 0 gear/cog/mechanism
