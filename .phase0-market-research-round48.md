## Selected Candidate: SLIDE CAT (Ice Slide Puzzle)

### Why Slide Cat?
1. **Proven market demand**: Ice sliding puzzles are beloved from Pokémon games, Neko Sliding on CrazyGames
2. **Genuine mechanical novelty**: "slide until hitting obstacle" grid mechanic — NO similar game in catalog
3. **High score**: 23/25 (Market=4, Novelty=5, SEO=4, Browser=5, Gap=5)
4. **Perfect browser feasibility**: Pure grid logic, no physics engine needed
5. **Confirmed gap**: Not in 557-game catalog, no ice/slide-until-stop mechanic exists

### Mechanic Details
- Player (a cat) starts at a position on a grid
- Player chooses a direction (swipe/arrow)
- Cat slides in that direction until hitting a wall or obstacle
- Goal: reach the exit/target tile in minimum moves
- Tile types: ice (slide), walls (block), buttons (toggle bridges), holes (fall), stars (collect)

### Differentiation from existing sliding-puzzle
- sliding-puzzle: 15-tile rearrangement puzzle (swap tiles to form a picture)
- slide-cat: directional slide puzzle (slide character across ice until hitting obstacle)

### Runner-up: Sandtris (22/25)

Deferred — higher complexity (cellular automaton) vs Slide Cat's pure grid logic.
