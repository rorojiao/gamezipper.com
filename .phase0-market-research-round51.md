## SCORING DETAIL (Top 3)

### 1. HOLE STARS — 22/25 ⭐
- Market: 5/5 — #4 on App Store Puzzle top free, trending in 2026
- SEO: 4/5 — "hole stars puzzle", "black hole puzzle game" strong search volume
- Monetization: 5/5 — casual 5-min sessions, perfect for interstitial ads
- Feasibility: 4/5 — needs simple physics simulation (gravity, collision), Canvas 2D doable
- Uniqueness: 4/5 — black-hole exists but is action-based; this is puzzle (order matters, sizes matter)
- Mechanic: Player controls a small black hole. Objects of different sizes need to be swallowed in a specific order to clear the level. Larger objects require the hole to grow first. Strategic depth: what order to eat things to fit through gaps.
