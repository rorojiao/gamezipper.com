Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

GameZipper is a free browser-games site. We keep data collection to an absolute minimum:

  • Analytics data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, session duration, device type, country). This data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Game scores: High scores are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. They are never sent to our servers.
  • No personal information: We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details. No account or sign-up is required.

2. Cookies

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics sets cookies (_ga, _gid) to distinguish users and measure traffic. These are first-party cookies.
  • Ad-related cookies: Our third-party advertising partners may set cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your browser settings or Your Ad Choices.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: Traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Advertising partners: We work with ad networks to keep the site free. These partners may collect anonymous browsing data per their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

All gameplay happens in your browser. We do not store personal data on our servers. Game progress and settings are saved locally on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all locally stored game data by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking using browser extensions or privacy settings.
  • Request information about any data we may hold by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach us at: privacy@gamezipper.com
