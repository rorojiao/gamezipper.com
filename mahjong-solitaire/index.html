Mahjongg Solitaire vs Mahjong Solitaire - Spelling Variants

Mahjongg Solitaire and Mahjong Solitaire are two spellings of the same classic tile-matching puzzle game. The double-g spelling "Mahjongg" is widely used by online game portals and is the most common search term, while the single-g spelling "Mahjong" follows the original transliteration of the traditional Chinese tile game. This free online Mahjongg Solitaire game works the same way regardless of how you spell it: click two free matching tiles to remove them, clear all 144 tiles to win.
