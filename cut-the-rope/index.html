About Cut the Rope Physics Puzzle

Cut the Rope is a physics-based puzzle game where you swipe to cut ropes and guide a candy ball into Om Nom's mouth. With realistic Verlet integration rope physics, every cut creates dynamic swinging and falling motion. Collect golden stars along the way for a perfect 3-star rating!

How to Play

  • Swipe or drag your mouse across ropes to cut them
  • The candy swings on remaining ropes with real physics
  • Collect up to 3 golden stars per level
  • Guide the candy into Om Nom's mouth to complete each level
  • Use bumpers to bounce the candy in new directions
  • Catch bubbles to float the candy upward, then tap to pop

Game Features

  • 25 handcrafted levels across 5 difficulty tiers
  • Realistic rope physics using Verlet integration
  • Cute Om Nom character with expressive animations
  • Bumpers, bubbles, and multi-rope puzzles
  • Star collection and level progression
  • Progress saved automatically to your browser
  • Works on desktop and mobile devices

Tips & Strategies

  • Timing is key: Cut ropes when the candy is swinging in the right direction
  • Think about momentum: The candy keeps its velocity when a rope is cut
  • Use bumpers wisely: They bounce the candy at the angle of impact
  • Bubble control: Tap bubbles at the perfect moment to drop candy onto stars
  • Watch Om Nom: His eyes follow the candy — use this to judge distance
