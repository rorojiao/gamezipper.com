How to Play

Move everyone: Arrow keys, WASD, or swipe. Every clone moves one tile in that direction.

Walls split them: A clone blocked by a wall stays put while others keep going.

Match the goal: Land each clone on its color-matched ring to lock it in.

Hazards: Holes kill, ice slides you until blocked, portals teleport, switches open doors.

Stars: ≤ par = 3★, par+5 = 2★, completed = 1★.
