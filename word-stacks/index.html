How to Play

📖
Read the category at the top (e.g. ANIMALS). Your goal is to find all words matching that theme.
👆
Drag across adjacent letter tiles to spell a word. Letters connect horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
⬇️
When you form a valid themed word, those tiles disappear and tiles above fall down with gravity.
⭐
Find all themed words to clear the board. Earn 3 stars by finishing fast without hints!
💡
Use Hints (3 per level) if stuck. Shuffle rearranges tiles. Bonus words give extra coins!
⌨️
Keyboard: Arrow keys to move, Enter to submit, Backspace to clear.
