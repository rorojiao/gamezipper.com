Tiny Fishing — Free Online Idle Fishing Game

Play Tiny Fishing free online! Cast your hook, catch 30 unique fish species, upgrade your gear, and explore the ocean depths in this addictive idle fishing game. No download required.

How to Play

  • Click or tap to cast your hook into the water.
  • The hook sinks down — move it left and right to navigate past fish.
  • Reel in by clicking/tapping again — your hook catches fish on the way up.
  • Sell caught fish for coins and use them to upgrade your gear.
  • Upgrades let you dive deeper and catch rarer fish.
  • Discover all 30 fish species across different ocean depths.

Tips & Tricks

  • Upgrade line length first: A longer line lets you reach deeper waters where rare, valuable fish live.
  • Dodge on the way down: Your hook is empty on the way down — avoid small fish to reach the big ones below.
  • Catch on the way up: Hook as many fish as possible while reeling up for maximum profit per cast.
  • Invest in hook capacity: Upgrading your hook lets you catch more fish per cast, compounding your earnings.
  • Target rare fish: Deep-water fish are worth far more. Focus upgrades on depth to unlock them.
