Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Word Search?

Look at the word list, then find each word hidden in the letter grid. Click or tap the first letter and drag to the last letter of the word.

Is Word Search free to play?

Yes, Word Search on GameZipper is completely free. No download or registration required.

How many categories are available?

Does Word Search work on mobile?
