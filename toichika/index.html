How to Play Toichika

Toichika (遠い誓い, “distant promise”) is a Nikoli logic puzzle by Gesaku. The grid is divided into outlined regions.

  • Draw exactly one arrow in each region — pointing up, down, left, or right.
  • Every arrow must form a facing pair: it points directly at exactly one other arrow along the same row or column.
  • Two paired regions may not share a border (touch orthogonally).
  • No other arrow may lie on the line strictly between a facing pair.

Controls: Tap a cell to place / rotate an arrow (empty → → → ↓ → ← → ↑ → erase). Keys: H hint, R restart, Enter check, Esc menu.
