## CONFIRMED GAPS — Genuine new mechanics we don't have

### 1. Blockins — Dual-Block Simultaneous Control (21/25)
- Source: Poki.com/en/puzzle (featured puzzle game)
- Mechanic: Control TWO blocks simultaneously on a grid. Each block has different properties (color, movement). Navigate both to their respective exit tiles. Blocks can interact — one may need to stand on a switch while the other passes through a gate.
- Why popular: Novel co-op puzzle for single player; brain-teasing spatial reasoning
- Gap status: ✅ No match — verified by 5 keyword searches (blockins, dual-block, twin-block, two-block, control-two all = 0 hits)
- H5 feasibility: HIGH — Canvas grid, no physics, swipe/keyboard controls
- Mobile: PERFECT — swipe moves both blocks simultaneously

### 2. Longcat — Growing-Body Maze Navigation (20/25)
- Source: Poki.com/en/puzzle
- Mechanic: Control a cat whose body grows longer each time it eats food. Navigate grid maze, plan path so growing body doesn't trap you. Like snake but with designed puzzle levels.
- Gap status: ✅ No match (longcat, long-cat, stretch-cat all = 0)
- Overlap risk: LOW — grow-worm is action, this is puzzle-level design

### 3. Draw to Smash — Draw-to-Destroy Physics (20/25)
- Source: Poki.com/en/puzzle
- Mechanic: Draw shapes that fall via gravity to crush/smash targets below.
- Gap status: ⚠️ ADJACENT — we have draw-to-save (protect) and physics-draw-puzzle, but NOT draw-to-destroy

### 4. Rail in the Air — Draw Rails for Trains (20/25)
- Source: Poki.com/en/puzzle
- Mechanic: Draw train tracks in mid-air to connect stations, trains follow your drawn path.
- Gap status: ⚠️ ADJACENT — train-tracks exists but this is draw-based, not pre-laid track

### 5. Cats Drop — Animal Suika Merge (18/25)
- Source: Poki.com/en/puzzle (kawaii-fruits-3d adjacent)
- Mechanic: Drop cute animals into container, same animals merge into larger ones (Suika game but animal-themed)
- Gap status: ⚠️ ADJACENT — watermelon-merge is fruit suika, this is animal-themed with different feedback loop
