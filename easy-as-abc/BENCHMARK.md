# Easy as ABC — Competitive Benchmark

## Competitors Analyzed

### 1. Puzzle Headquarters (puzzle-bridges.com)
- **Grid sizes:** 5x5, 6x6
- **Letters:** A, B, C, D
- **Features:** Daily puzzle, timer, auto-check, pencil marks
- **Monetization:** Free with ads
- **Weakness:** Clunky mobile interface, no star ratings, limited difficulty curve

### 2. Croco-Puzzle (croco-puzzle.com)
- **Grid sizes:** 4x4 to 7x7
- **Letters:** Variable (A-C to A-E)
- **Features:** Competition mode, rankings, manual entry
- **Monetization:** Subscription
- **Weakness:** Desktop-first, no mobile optimization, intimidating for beginners

### 3. Nikoli Official (nikoli.com)
- **Grid sizes:** 4x4, 5x5
- **Letters:** A, B, C
- **Features:** Premium puzzles, handcrafted quality
- **Monetization:** Paid subscription
- **Weakness:** Paywalled, limited free content

### 4. BrainBashers (brainbashers.com)
- **Grid sizes:** 4x4
- **Letters:** A, B, C
- **Features:** Daily puzzle, print option
- **Monetization:** Display ads
- **Weakness:** Outdated design, no touch optimization, very easy puzzles only

## Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Mobile-first design** — Touch-optimized with proper viewport handling
2. **30 handcrafted levels** — Progressive difficulty from Beginner to Expert
3. **Star rating system** — Replayability incentive
4. **Procedural generation** — Unlimited practice puzzles
5. **Daily challenge** — Engagement and retention
6. **Free with non-intrusive ads** — No paywall, accessible to everyone
7. **Modern dark UI** — Clean, polished visual design
8. **Undo + hints** — Accessible to all skill levels

## Differentiation Strategy
- Focus on smooth mobile experience (most competitors are desktop-first)
- Progressive difficulty curve (competitors jump from easy to hard)
- Clean visual feedback with color-coded cell states
