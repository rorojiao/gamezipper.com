Basketball Shoot — Free Arcade Game Online

Ready to shoot some hoops? Basketball Shoot is a fast-paced arcade game where you drag, aim, and release to sink baskets in just 60 seconds. Score as many points as possible before time runs out!

How to Play

  • Drag from the basketball to aim your shot — like a slingshot.
  • Release to shoot. The ball follows a realistic arc with gravity.
  • Score 2 points for regular baskets, 3 points for swish shots (nothing but net).
  • Consecutive baskets earn streak bonuses!
  • The hoop moves position after each shot — adapt your aim!

Game Features

  • 🏀 Realistic basketball physics with gravity and air drag
  • ⏱️ 60-second timed rounds
  • 🎯 Random hoop positions after each shot
  • 🔥 Streak bonus system for consecutive baskets
  • 📱 Works on mobile and desktop
  • 🏆 High score saved locally

Tips

  • Practice the drag-back gesture — it controls both power and angle.
  • Swish shots give +3 points — aim for a clean arc over the rim.
  • Consecutive baskets multiply your score — keep the streak alive!
  • Watch the hoop position carefully — it changes after each shot.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Basketball Shoot?

Drag from the basketball to aim your shot — the farther you drag, the stronger the shot. Release to shoot. Score as many baskets as you can in 60 seconds!

Is it free?

Yes, completely free. No download, no sign-up — just open and play in any browser.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! Works on both mobile and desktop. Drag with your finger on mobile or mouse on desktop.
