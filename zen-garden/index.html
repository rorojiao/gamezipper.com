The Three Rules

1
Balance. Every row and column must contain an equal number of black and white stones.
2
No three in a row. Never place three stones of the same color next to each other (horizontally or vertically).
3
Uniqueness. No two rows or two columns may be identical once completed.
·
Tap a cell to cycle: empty → black → white → empty. Locked clue stones cannot be changed.
