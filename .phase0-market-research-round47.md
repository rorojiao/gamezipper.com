## Selected Candidate: HEXA AWAY

### Why Hexa Away?
1. **Proven market demand**: Tap Away 3D / BlockAway = 100M+ downloads globally
2. **Genuine mechanical novelty**: Hex grid = 6 exit directions (vs 4 in tap-away)
3. **Natural evolution**: Our tap-away was R46's last successful build
4. **SEO differentiation**: Unique search terms ("hexa away", "hex block away", "hex away puzzle")
5. **Hex topology**: Creates fundamentally different puzzle logic (diagonal paths, 6-way constraints)
6. **Confirmed gap**: Not in catalog, no hex variants of tap-away exist

### Differentiation from existing tap-away (R46)
- tap-away: 4-direction grid, blocks slide up/down/left/right
- hexa-away: 6-direction hex grid, blocks slide in 6 directions (including diagonals)
- Different path calculation, different puzzle solving strategies
- New visual presentation (hexagonal cells)
