## Conclusion

Every probed candidate family is either:
1. Already LIVE on GameZipper (exact slug match), or
2. Functionally covered by an existing game under a different name (Layer-3 desc match), or
3. Part of a saturated cluster (≥5 existing neighbors)

**No genuinely uncovered high-signal candidate (≥18/25 gate) exists at this time.**

This reconfirms the R45 and R46 terminal-saturation findings. The catalog
now spans 556 unique slugs across every major mobile-puzzle mechanic family:
sort (15), merge (9), color/paint (16), ring/spin (6), tap/tile (6),
number/math (10+), gravity/orbit (4), fill/pour (7), rope/twine (5),
screw/bolt (5), parking/unblock (4), triple-match (5), and dozens more
single-entry families.
