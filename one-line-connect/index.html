How to Play One Line Connect

Objective

Connect all dots on the grid by drawing a single continuous line. Visit every dot exactly once to complete each puzzle.

Rules

  • Start by tapping any dot
  • Connect to adjacent dots (horizontal or vertical only)
  • Your line cannot cross itself
  • Visit every dot to complete the level

Tips

  • Plan your route before starting
  • Use Undo if you make a mistake
  • Use Hint when you're stuck
  • Early tiers unlock as you progress

Scoring

Earn 100 points per level multiplied by the tier number. Complete puzzles to collect stars and unlock harder challenges!
