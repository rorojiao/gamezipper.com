How to Play Pin Pull Puzzle

Pull the pins out one at a time to release the coins, gems, and loot. Each pin holds something in place — when you remove the right pin in the right order, gravity guides the loot down into the goal bag. Watch out for lava and spike hazards that will end your run if the loot falls into them!

Tips & Strategy

Plan your pin pulls before you act — the par count tells you the ideal number of moves. Think about which pins are blocking the loot from falling straight down to the goal, and which can be left in place. Hazards are usually avoidable by careful pin ordering rather than random pulling.
