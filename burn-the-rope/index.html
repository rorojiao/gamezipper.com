Burn the Rope — Free Online Fire Physics Puzzle

Burn the Rope is a unique puzzle game where you ignite ropes and guide fire to burn every segment. Fire only burns upward — so you must rotate the world to keep the flames alive! Collect bonus bugs and clear all 30 levels for the perfect score.

How to Play Burn the Rope

  • Tap or click any rope to ignite it. Fire spreads along the rope automatically.
  • Fire only burns upward (relative to gravity). Use arrow keys or on-screen rotate buttons to spin the world and redirect the flames.
  • At junctions, fire splits and tries all branches — but only continues on paths that go upward.
  • Collect colorful bugs along the ropes for bonus points and extra stars.
  • Burn every rope segment in a level to win!

Tips & Strategy

  • Before igniting, trace the rope layout mentally and plan which way to rotate first.
  • At junctions, rotate so both branches point upward to burn them simultaneously.
  • Time your rotations carefully — fire goes out quickly if the rope turns downward.
  • Collect all bugs in a level to earn the full 3-star rating.
  • Later levels have fireproof chains that block fire — plan around them!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Burn the Rope free to play?

Yes — Burn the Rope is 100% free online with no download or sign-up. It runs in your browser on desktop and mobile.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! Tap ropes to ignite and use the on-screen buttons to rotate the world. Designed for touchscreens.

How many levels are there?

30 handcrafted levels across 6 tiers of increasing difficulty, from simple straight ropes to complex junction graphs.

How do fireproof chains work?

Some rope segments are fireproof and won't burn. You must route your fire around them to reach all the burnable ropes.
