Bowling Master — Free Online Bowling Puzzle Game

Bowling Master is a physics-based bowling puzzle where you aim, set power, and knock down all the pins. Across 30 handcrafted levels you'll face walls, oil slicks, bumpers, and tricky pin layouts. Play free online with no download!

How to Play Bowling Master

  • Aim by dragging left or right to adjust your throw angle.
  • Set power by pulling back and releasing — the further you pull, the harder the ball goes.
  • Release to bowl the ball down the lane toward the pins.
  • Knock down all pins to clear the level. Some levels need creative angles or bank shots!
  • Watch for obstacles: walls block and deflect your ball, oil slicks change its direction, and bumpers bounce it unpredictably.

Tips & Strategy

  • Aim for the "pocket" — the space between the front pin and the one next to it. This maximizes pin scatter.
  • Use walls to your advantage — bank shots can curve around obstacles to reach hidden pins.
  • Don't always go for maximum power. Sometimes a softer, well-aimed throw is more effective.
  • Account for oil slicks — they'll change your ball's direction, so aim slightly off-center to compensate.
  • To earn 3 stars, clear all pins using the par number of shots or fewer.
