How to Play Simon Says

Objective

Watch the colored sequence flash, then repeat it exactly. Each round adds one more step to the pattern.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 1.2s per flash, max 20 rounds
  • Medium: 0.8s per flash, max 30 rounds
  • Hard: 0.5s per flash, no limit
  • Speed Mode: 0.3s per flash, for experts

Scoring

  • Base points: 10 x round number
  • Speed bonus for quick responses
  • Combo multiplier for consecutive correct inputs
  • Perfect bonus for completing without mistakes

Game Over

One wrong color ends the game. Try to beat your high score!
