How to Play

Each level shows a grid of colored tiles forming a gradient.

Some tiles have dots ⬤ - they are in the wrong position.

Tap a dotted tile to select it, then tap another dotted tile to swap their colors.

When all tiles are in their correct positions, the gradient is complete and all dots disappear!

Earn 3 stars by solving in fewer moves. Use hints if you get stuck.
