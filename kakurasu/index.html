How to Play Kakurasu

Goal: Shade cells so every row & column clue is satisfied.

Row clue (right) = sum of column-numbers of shaded cells in that row.

Column clue (bottom) = sum of row-numbers of shaded cells in that column.

Gold cells are locked givens — already correct.

Tap a cell to shade/unshade. Satisfied clues glow cyan.
