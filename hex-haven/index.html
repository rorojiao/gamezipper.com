How to Play Hex Haven

Hex Haven is a relaxing tile placement puzzle. Build beautiful landscapes by matching hex tiles!

🎯 Basic Rules

  • Click or tap on the board to place your tile
  • Each tile has different terrain types on its 6 edges
  • Match edges with adjacent tiles to create connected landscapes
  • Complete quests to earn stars and advance

⭐ Quests & Scoring

  • Each level has 1-3 quests (e.g., "Connect 6 Forest tiles")
  • Matched edges earn points
  • Longer connections and multiple matches score higher
  • Complete all quests for 3 stars

💡 Tips

  • Look at the preview to plan your next move
  • Try to complete multiple quests with one tile
  • Use Undo if you make a mistake
  • Endless mode is perfect for relaxing play
