How to Play Norinori

1. The grid is divided into regions (outlined in cyan). Some regions show a number.
2. Tap a cell to shade it. Tap again to clear. Shaded cells glow magenta.
3. Every shaded cell must have exactly ONE shaded neighbor — this forms dominoes of 2 cells. Dominoes cannot touch orthogonally.
4. Each numbered region must contain exactly its labeled count of shaded cells.
5. Tap Check to validate. When all dominoes form AND every number is satisfied, you win!
