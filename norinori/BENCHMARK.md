## Why Selected (21/25 — Top of Nikoli-gap queue)
- **D (Demand) 4**: Norinori is a top-20 Nikoli puzzle, popular on puzzle sites (Puzzle Team, Simon Tatham, Logic Masters Germany).
- **S (SEO) 4**: "norinori puzzle", "norinori online", "play norinori" — niche but zero GZ competition.
- **R (Retention) 4**: Logic puzzles with 25+ levels = 30-60 min sessions, retry-driven.

- **Z (Zero-overlap) 5**: Grep confirms zero "norinori" matches. Different mechanic from existing yajilin, nurikabe, heyawake.

## Rules (Nikoli Official)
1. Grid is divided into outlined **regions**.
2. Some regions display a **number** = exact count of shaded cells they must contain.
3. Shaded cells form **dominoes**: every connected group of shaded cells must contain **exactly 2 cells** (orthogonally adjacent).
4. Regions without a number may contain any number of shaded cells.
5. **Goal**: shade cells so every numbered region hits its exact count AND all shaded cells form disjoint size-2 dominoes.

## Music Style
- Ambient electronic, mysterious, ethereal, soft synth pads (Puzzle BGM per spec)

## Level Verification Standard
Every level generated by constructive algorithm: place N disjoint dominoes first → derive region numbers from placement → guarantee at least one solution exists. Solver confirms reachability via BFS over all valid domino-tileings.
