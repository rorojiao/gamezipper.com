How to Play

Objective

Guide your robot to the green goal tile. Collect stars along the way for a perfect rating.

Movement

  • Tap or click any tile to set a waypoint
  • The robot will automatically navigate to that tile
  • Collect all 3 stars for a 3-star rating

Rotation

  • Rotate the diorama by swiping or using rotation buttons
  • Some paths are hidden from certain angles
  • Rotate to reveal new routes and the goal

Height

  • The robot can climb one level using stairs/ramps
  • Walls block movement completely
  • Gaps cause the robot to fall (restart)

Switches

  • Step on buttons to toggle bridges or open doors
  • Bridges appear/disappear when activated

Hints

Earn hints by completing levels with 3 stars. Hints show you the optimal path.
