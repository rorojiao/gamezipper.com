How to Play Cribbage

1The Goal

First player to 121 points wins. Score points through pegging during play and counting combinations in your hand.

2The Deal

Each player gets 6 cards. Discard 2 to the crib (4 cards each). Non-dealer cuts the starter card. If it's a Jack, dealer scores 2 points (His Heels).

3Pegging

Alternate playing cards. Running total must not exceed 31. Score: 15 (2pts), pair (2pts), three-of-kind (6pts), four-of-kind (12pts), run (1pt/card). Say "Go" when you can't play.

4The Show

Non-dealer counts hand first (with starter), then dealer counts hand, then dealer's crib. Count 15s, pairs, runs, flushes, and His Nobs (Jack matching starter suit).

5Winning

Skunk: win by 31+ points counts as 2 wins. Double skunk: 61+ points.
