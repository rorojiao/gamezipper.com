How to Play Gravity Flip

Tap the screen or press Space to flip gravity. Your glowing orb falls up or down depending on the current gravity direction. Time your flips to navigate through narrow gaps, avoid red spikes, spinning saw blades, and pulsing laser beams. Collect all three golden stars in each level for a perfect three-star rating, then reach the green exit portal to advance to the next challenge.

Tips & Strategy

Watch the gravity indicator at the edge of the screen, blue means gravity pulls down, red means it pulls up. Green bounce pads reverse your gravity and launch you with extra speed. Moving platforms (marked with arrows) shift horizontally or vertically, plan your flips around their movement. The par flip count tells you the ideal number of gravity flips for a three-star score, fewer flips means better ratings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gravity Flip free to play?

Yes! Gravity Flip is completely free to play in your browser with no download or sign-up required.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely. The game is fully optimized for touch controls, just tap anywhere on the screen to flip gravity.

How many levels are there?

30 handcrafted levels across 5 difficulty tiers, from Beginner to Expert. Each tier introduces new hazards.

Does progress save automatically?

Yes, your highest unlocked level and star ratings are saved locally so you can continue where you left off.
