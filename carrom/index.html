How to Play Carrom

Objective

Pocket all your colored pieces, then pocket the red queen to win. White pieces belong to Player 1, black pieces to Player 2.

Aiming & Shooting

  • Click and drag on the gold striker disc to aim
  • Drag direction is the shot direction
  • Drag distance determines shot power
  • Release to shoot

Queen Rules

  • After pocketing ALL your pieces, you must pocket the queen
  • The queen must be "covered" - pocket another piece in the same or next turn
  • If you pocket the queen without a cover, it returns to center

Fouls

  • If your striker goes in a pocket: -5 points
  • One of your previously pocketed pieces returns to center

Winning

  • First to pocket all their pieces plus the queen wins
  • If neither has pieces left, the player with more points wins
