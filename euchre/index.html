How to Play Euchre

1Trump: A suit chosen by bidding. Trump beats all other suits.
2Bowers: The Jack of trump is the Right Bower (highest). The Jack of the same color is the Left Bower (second highest).
3Ordering Up: If the face-up card is your suit, you can Order It Up to make it trump.
4Going Alone: Call trump alone for a chance at 4 points, but your partner sits out.
5Following Suit: Must follow suit if able. Play highest trump to win, or highest of led suit.
6Scoring: Makers get 1pt (3-4 tricks), 2pts (march=5). Going alone + march = 4pts. Euchre = 2pts for defenders.
