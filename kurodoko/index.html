🎯 Goal

Shade (black out) the correct cells so that each row and column contains the exact number of white cells shown by the clue.

📜 Rules

  • The number next to each row/column = how many white cells it must have.
  • No two black cells may share an edge (diagonal is OK).
  • No 2×2 block of black cells.
  • All white cells must be connected (one group).

🕹️ Controls

  • Tap a cell to toggle between white and black.
  • Undo reverses your last move.
  • Check verifies your solution. Solve fast for ⭐⭐⭐!

⭐ Star Ratings

  • ⭐⭐⭐ — Solve under the target time
  • ⭐⭐ — Solve in moderate time
  • ⭐ — Complete the puzzle

💡 Tip

Start with rows/columns where the clue equals the grid size — those are all white. Then look for rows with small numbers: many cells must be black there.
