How to Play

Core Mechanic

Tap or click to flip your knife. The knife automatically flies forward and slices through wood obstacles. Time your flips to align the knife with gaps!

Obstacles

Wood Plank - Slice through it!
Spinning Plank - Time your flip!
Moving Barrier - Dodge it!
Spike - AVOID! Breaks your knife!

Power-Ups

  • Shield: Blocks one spike hit (3 uses)
  • Slow-Mo: Slows obstacles for 5 seconds (2 uses)
  • Magnet: Auto-aligns knife to gaps for 3 seconds (2 uses)

Scoring

  • Each slice = 10 points
  • Consecutive slices build combo multiplier (up to x3)
  • Fast completion earns speed bonus
  • Earn 3 stars by slicing everything quickly!
