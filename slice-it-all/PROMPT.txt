Build a complete, commercial-quality HTML5 game: Slice It All — a hyper-casual knife-flip slicer. Single file: slice-it-all/index.html (self-contained, no external deps except CDN fonts and 1 analytics script).

## Game Concept
A sharp knife flies forward automatically across a side-scrolling 3D-ish scene. Player taps/clicks to flip the knife 180° in mid-air. The goal is to cleanly slice every obstacle (pencils, ropes, planks, glass, bombs, balls) by passing the knife blade through them. Touching an unsliced obstacle ends the run. Cleanly sliced pieces fly off with physics and particle bursts. Stars are awarded per level based on flips used and clean-sliced count.

## Game Design Spec
- 30 levels across 5 difficulty tiers (Starter Lv 1-6, Classic Lv 7-12, Challenge Lv 13-18, Expert Lv 19-24, Master Lv 25-30)
- Each level has 1-5 obstacles, varying heights, gaps, and bomb hazards at higher tiers
- Knife moves at constant forward speed; obstacle spacing is set per level
- Tap/click = 180° flip. Knife continues forward at all times.
- Obstacle types: pencil (long thin), rope (horizontal line), plank (rectangle), glass (translucent square), bomb (round, fails level on slice without knife pointing up), ball (round, easy slice)
- Star rating: 1 star = finish level, 2 stars = all clean slices, 3 stars = finish with only 1 flip total (perfect)
- Each level has 1-3 flips available; player who uses fewer flips scores higher

## UI / UX
- Home screen: Big "PLAY" button, knife preview (rotating), 6 unlockable knife skins (Default / Gold / Crystal / Flame / Shadow / Galaxy), best level + total stars
- Level select: 5 tier cards expanding to show 6 level buttons each; locked levels grayed
- In-game: Top HUD = level number, stars earned (0/3), flip counter, score. Bottom = back button

## Systems to Implement (all benchmarked)
1. **Level system**: 30 levels, mulberry32 seeded obstacle layouts. Each level data: {obstacles:[{x, y, type, w, h}], maxFlips, parFlips, tier}
2. **Scoring**: base 100 × tier, +50 combo bonus per consecutive clean slice without flipping, perfect bonus 500
3. **Progress save**: localStorage 'sliceItAll_v3' = {bestLevel, totalStars, starsPerLevel:{}, skin:0, skinsUnlocked:[true,false...], powerups, achievements[], soundOn}
4. **Skin unlock**: Gold = 10 stars, Crystal = 25 stars, Flame = 50 stars, Shadow = 80 stars, Galaxy = 120 stars
5. **Power-ups** (3): Magnet (auto-flip when knife within 30px of obstacle), Shield (1 free unsliced touch), Slow-Mo (2x slow 5 sec). Earned via watching rewarded buttons or 1 each at start
6. **Achievements** (12): First Slice, 10 Stars, 30 Stars, 60 Stars, 90 Stars (all 3-star), First Master, Master Tier Clear, No Shield, No Powerups, Combo King (5x combo), Flip Master (3 perfect levels), Skin Collector
7. **Combo system**: Consecutive clean slices without hitting an obstacle = 1.5x/2x/3x/5x score
8. **Sound**: Web Audio API procedural SFX (flip / slice / perfect / fail / level_complete / star_earn / button_click / hover / powerup_collect / combo / knife_land / shield_break) and procedural BGM (driving synth pulse 110 BPM, 16s loop)
9. **Particle system**: Slice burst (8-12 colored particles), flip puff (6 white particles), star earn (gold sparkles)
10. **Screen shake**: 6px on collision, 3px on slice
11. **Camera**: Follows knife horizontally with smooth lerp (slight lookahead)

### Play Screen
- Side view, knife starts at x=20% of canvas, flies right
- Obstacles laid out at x positions from 30% to 95% of level distance
- HUD top: ← Back, Level X, ★ 0/3, Flips: 2, Score: 0
- Knife tip points right; flip rotates it 180° (tip points left temporarily)
- Slicing detection: when knife blade passes through obstacle's vertical range while pointing right and crosses its x position
- Unclean contact = game over
- Reaching right edge = win (calculate stars)

### Level Generation (mulberry32)
- Per level, seeded: obstacle count (1-5 by tier), spacing (300-200px by tier), heights (random within range)
- Type distribution by tier: Tier 1 only pencils+planks; Tier 2 add ropes; Tier 3 add glass; Tier 4 add balls+more ropes; Tier 5 add bombs+complex mixes
- Validate: ensure path exists (at least 1 safe y corridor)
