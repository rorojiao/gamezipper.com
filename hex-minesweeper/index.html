How to Play

Goal: Reveal all safe hexagons without hitting mines.

Reveal: Left-click (or tap) a hexagon to reveal it. Your first click is always safe.

Flag: Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag suspected mines.

Numbers: Each number tells how many of the 6 adjacent hexagons contain mines.

Chord Click: Click a revealed number when the correct flags are placed to auto-reveal remaining neighbors.

Column Totals: Numbers at grid edges show total mines in that column.

Hint: Tap the star button for a logical hint highlighting a deducible cell.

No Guessing: Every puzzle can be solved with pure logic - no luck needed!

Stars: 0 mistakes = 3 stars, 1-2 mistakes = 2 stars, 3+ = 1 star.
