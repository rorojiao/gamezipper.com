How to Play

Goal: Reveal all safe hexagons without hitting mines.

Reveal: Left-click (or tap) a hexagon to reveal it.

Flag: Right-click (or long-press) to mark suspected mines.

Numbers: Each number tells how many of the 6 adjacent hexagons contain mines.

Chord Click: Click a revealed number when correct flags are placed to auto-reveal neighbors.

Column Totals: Numbers at grid edges show total mines in that column.

Hint: Tap the star button for a logical hint.

No Guessing: Every puzzle can be solved with pure logic!
