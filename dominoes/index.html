The Basics

How to Play Dominoes

Objective

Be the first to play all your tiles, or have the fewest pips when the game is blocked.

Game Modes

ModeWhen StuckScoring
DrawDraw from boneyardWinner gets loser's pips
BlockMust passWinner gets loser's pips
All FivesDraw from boneyardScore on multiples of 5

Rules

  • 28 tiles from [0|0] to [6|6], each player draws 7
  • Player with highest double goes first
  • Place tiles matching open ends of the chain
  • Doubles are placed perpendicular to the chain
  • Round ends when someone empties their hand, or both are blocked
  • Blocked: fewest pips wins, scores the difference
  • First to 100 match points wins

All Fives Scoring

After each play, if both open ends sum to a multiple of 5, you score that amount immediately!
