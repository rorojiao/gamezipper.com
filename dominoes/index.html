The Basics

Dominoes is played with tiles showing two numbers. Match one end of your tile to an open end on the table.

How to Play Dominoes

Objective

Be the first to play all your domino tiles. The game continues until one player is out of tiles or the game is blocked.

Game Modes

ModeRuleScoring
DrawDraw from boneyard if stuckWinner takes opponent's pips
BlockPass if you cannot playWinner takes opponent's pips
All FivesScore on multiples of 5Score open end sum / 5

Matching

  • Place a tile so one half matches an open end of the chain
  • The first tile is usually a double - it can be placed sideways
  • Both ends of the chain are open for matching

All Fives Scoring

When the two open ends sum to 5, 10, 15, 20... the player who just played scores that amount immediately!

Winning

  • First to play all tiles wins the round
  • If blocked, player with fewest pips wins
  • First to 100 points wins the match
