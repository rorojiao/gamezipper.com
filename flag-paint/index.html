Welcome to Flag Paint!
Your goal is to paint each flag by selecting colors and filling in the correct regions. Let's learn how to play!
Step 1: Pick a Color
Tap a color swatch at the bottom to select it. The selected color will glow blue.
Step 2: Fill Regions
Now tap a region on the flag. If the color is correct, it fills in! If wrong, you'll get a mistake. Fill all regions to complete the flag.
Earn Stars!
Complete with no mistakes for 3 stars. Use hints sparingly — each hint costs a star. Ready to paint the world?
