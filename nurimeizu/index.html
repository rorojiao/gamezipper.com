How to Play Nurimeizu

Goal: Paint rooms black (walls) or white (paths) to create a maze from S to G.

Rules:

1. Each room (bold-bordered area) must be entirely one color.

2. White cells must be connected with no loops (a tree).

3. No 2x2 block of the same color.

4. All circles must be on the shortest S-to-G path.

5. No triangles on the shortest path.

Controls: Tap a room to paint it. Use White/Black/Erase buttons.
