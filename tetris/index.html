🎮 How to Play

⬅️
Move Left/Right: Arrow keys on desktop, swipe or tap ← → buttons on mobile.
🔄
Rotate: Up arrow key or tap the ↑ button to rotate clockwise.
⬇️
Soft Drop: Down arrow to drop faster. Hard Drop: Space key for instant drop.
🗑️
Clear Lines: Fill a full row to clear it. 1 line=100pts, 2=300pts, 3=500pts, 4=800pts.
⚡
Level Up: Every 10 lines cleared increases your level — blocks fall faster!
👻
Ghost Piece: The transparent preview shows where your block will land.
⏸️
Pause: Press P to pause/resume the game.
