How to Play Yosenabe

Goal: Move all numbered circles into gray pot regions.
Move: Tap a circle to select it. Then swipe up/down/left/right, or tap a region cell to send it there.
Sum: Numbers in each pot must add up to the target number shown on the region.
Rules: Paths cannot cross each other or pass through other circles. Every pot needs at least one circle.
