Path Finder - Free Online Numberlink Puzzle Game

Play Path Finder online for free — connect matching numbers to fill the grid without crossing paths! With 40 hand-crafted Numberlink puzzles from 5x5 to 8x8, it's perfect for fans of path-drawing puzzles, logic games, and connect-the-dots brain teasers. No download required.

How to Play Path Finder

  • The grid contains pairs of numbered endpoints. Your goal is to connect each pair with a path.
  • Drag from one number to its matching partner to draw a path.
  • Paths travel through empty cells — they can't cross each other or overlap.
  • Every cell on the grid must be filled by a path to solve the puzzle.
  • Connect all number pairs correctly to complete the level.
  • Tackle 40 puzzles ranging from 5x5 beginner grids to 8x8 expert challenges.

Tips & Strategy

  • Start with pairs that have only one obvious route — usually those near edges or corners.
  • If two paths must cross, one of them needs to reroute — backtrack and rethink.
  • Since every cell must be filled, look for stranded cells that only one path can reach.
  • Number pairs on the same edge often hug the border — try that first.
  • Don't leave isolated gaps — if a cell can only be reached by one pair, route through it.
  • Larger grids reward planning ahead — sketch mentally before committing to a path.

What is Numberlink?

Numberlink is a classic Japanese logic puzzle where you connect matching numbered pairs with paths that fill the entire grid without crossing.
