How to Play
1
Pick up pieces from the tray at the bottom by tapping or clicking them.
2
Drag the piece over the grid. A green outline means it fits. Red means it doesn't.
3
Release to drop the piece in place. Pick it up again anytime to rearrange.
4
Fill the entire grid with all pieces to complete the level. No overlaps, no gaps!
5
Use Hint if you're stuck — but fewer hints means more stars!
