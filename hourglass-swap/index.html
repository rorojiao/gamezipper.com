How to Play

Goal: Each level gives you 2-4 hourglasses and a target time. You need to reach a moment when any hourglass has exactly the target amount of sand (top or bottom).

Sand Flow: All hourglasses start with sand on top, flowing down at 1 unit per second. The numbers on each glass show the current sand amount in top/bottom.

Flip: Tap any hourglass to instantly flip it. Top becomes bottom and vice versa.

Win: When any glass hits the exact target, you win! Fewer flips = more stars.

Reset: Tap Reset to restart the current level.
