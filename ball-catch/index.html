🎮 How to Play Ball Catch

🎯 Objective

Catch as many falling balls as you can to score points! Miss 3 balls and it's game over. Every catch counts — how long can you survive?

🕹️ Controls

Move your paddle left and right by moving your mouse or dragging your finger on mobile. Stay under the falling balls!

🔥 Pro Tips

  • Watch the ball color — all colors score equally, so focus on timing
  • Balls fall faster as your score increases — stay alert!
  • Hit milestones (10, 25, 50, 100, 200) for fun animations
  • Your best score is saved locally — beat your record!

💡 Features

  • 3 lives per game — each miss costs one life
  • Milestone celebrations at 10, 25, 50, 100, 200 points
  • Share your score on Twitter after game over
  • Works on desktop and mobile
