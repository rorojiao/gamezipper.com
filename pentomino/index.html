Welcome to Pentomino!

Pentomino is a classic tiling puzzle invented by Solomon Golomb in 1954. Your goal: place every piece so the board is fully covered — no overlaps, no gaps.

  • Click a piece in the right tray to pick it up.
  • Press R to rotate the held piece 90°.
  • Press F to flip the held piece.
  • Click a cell on the board to place the piece.
  • Press H for a hint, U to undo, Esc to drop.

There are 12 unique pentominoes — F, I, L, N, P, T, U, V, W, X, Y, Z — each made of exactly 5 squares. Higher tiers use more pieces on bigger boards.
