# BENCHMARK: Pizza Maker Puzzle

## Top Competitor: Good Pizza Great Pizza (TapBlaze)
- Downloads: 50M+ (Google Play)
- Rating: 4.6/5 (1.2M reviews)
- Core loop: Receive customer order → prepare pizza (crust→sauce→cheese→toppings) → bake → serve → earn coins
- Monetization: Rewarded ads for coins, in-app purchases for upgrades
- Key engagement: Order variety, time pressure, ingredient unlock progression

## Other Competitors:
- Pizza Hero: 10M+ downloads, arcade-style pizza making
- Papa's Pizzeria (Flipline): 5M+, Flash-era classic, order management
- Cooking Fever (pizza section): 100M+ total, multiple restaurant types

## Game Design Decisions (from benchmark):
1. Circular pizza grid (sectors/wedges) — visually intuitive
2. Order tickets show required ingredients with icons
3. Time limit creates urgency (not too punishing)
4. Difficulty curve: simple orders → complex multi-topping → time pressure
5. Scoring: accuracy (correct toppings) + speed (time bonus) + combo (streak)
6. 30 levels across 5 tiers: Classic→Gourmet→Rush→Master→Legend
7. Features: ingredient shop unlock, daily challenge, achievements

## Our Differentiators:
- Puzzle-focused (order-matching, not time-management)
- Ingredient placement matters (position on pizza matters for some levels)
- Progressive complexity (new mechanics each tier)
- Single-file, offline, no ads blocking gameplay
