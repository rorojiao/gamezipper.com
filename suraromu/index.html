How to Play Suraromu
Goal: Draw a single closed loop through the grid.

Rules:
• Tap between two cells to draw a line segment
• Tap again to erase
• The loop starts and ends at the open circle
• The loop must pass through every gate exactly once
• Gates are gaps between black cells
• Numbers in black cells show crossing order from the start
