How to Play Trebuchet Trajectory

Trebuchet Trajectory is a counterweight siege physics puzzle. Your goal is to hit the red target with a launched projectile by choosing the right combination of power (counterweight setting) and launch angle. The projectile follows a realistic parabolic arc affected by gravity (9.81 m/s²), and later levels introduce obstacles — walls and barriers that block the projectile's flight path.

Controls: Drag the Power slider to set counterweight velocity, and the Angle slider to set the launch elevation. A dotted preview trajectory appears so you can fine-tune before firing. Press 🚀 Launch (or Spacebar/Enter) to fire. Use ◀ Prev / ▶ Next to navigate levels, and ↻ Reset to clear dials. On desktop, arrow keys also work: Left/Right for power, Up/Down for angle.

Tips & Strategy

  • Read the dotted preview: The faint red dashed line shows exactly where your projectile will land. Align the end of the trajectory with the target flag before firing.
  • Watch for obstacles: From Tier 3 onward, gray walls block your path. Choose a steeper or shallower angle to arc over or around them — high power + steep angle can overshoot.
  • Use the overshoot/undershoot messages: The status line tells you if you're too far or too short by pixel distance, so you know which direction to adjust.
  • Higher tiers = more dials: Later levels offer 5–7 power and angle options each, giving finer control but harder combinations.
