How to Play
① Two room types. Each room is marked with a moon 🌙 (line must go straight) or sun ☀ (line must turn at least once inside).
② Draw the path. Tap any edge between two cells to draw a line segment. Tap again to erase. The line connects cell centers, forming one continuous path.
③ Visit every cell. The path must enter every cell exactly once. It will have two endpoints (cells where the line starts and stops).
④ Alternate room types. As the path crosses from one room to another, room types must alternate: moon, sun, moon, sun…
⑤ Tips. Snake paths often work. Use Check for instant feedback. Hints reveal a correct edge — you have 3 per level.
