Rules:
• Shade some cells to form square black blocks (1×1, 2×2, 3×3).
• Blocks must NOT touch each other orthogonally (only diagonally).
• Same-size blocks must NOT see each other along a row/column without a different-size block in between.
• Each number shows how many of its 5-cell window (itself + 4 neighbors) must be black.
Controls: Click to place a 1×1 block · Right-click to erase · Use the size selector to place 2×2 or 3×3 blocks.
