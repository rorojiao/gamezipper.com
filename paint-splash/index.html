Paint Splash — Free Color Sliding Puzzle

Play Paint Splash free online! Slide colored blocks to fill the canvas with paint. A creative and satisfying color puzzle experience.

How to Play

  • Swipe or drag the colored blocks across the grid.
  • Each block paints the cells it slides over in its color.
  • Fill the entire grid using all blocks to complete the level.
  • Each level has a unique solution — think before you swipe!

Game Features

  • 🎨 Artistic color-filling mechanics
  • 🧩 Unique puzzle design per level
  • 🌈 Satisfying paint trail animations
  • 📱 Smooth touch controls

Tips & Tricks

  • Plan your path — once paint is laid, it can't be undone.
  • Look for dead-end paths and fill those first.
  • Work outward from corners inward.

How do you play Paint Splash?

Slide rows and columns of colorful paint tiles to align 3 or more matching colors. When tiles of the same color line up, they burst with a paint splash and clear from the board. Plan your slides carefully to create chain reactions and clear the board efficiently!
