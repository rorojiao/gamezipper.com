1. Tap any unlocked level on the grid.

2. Read the target list — silhouettes show what to find.

3. Tap each hidden object in the scene. Wrong taps count as misses.

4. Find all targets under target time for ⭐⭐⭐, under par for ⭐⭐, or just solve for ⭐.

How to Play Hidden Picture Puzzle

Each scene is full of small cartoon icons. Look at the "FIND THESE" list at the bottom and tap the matching item in the scene to collect it. Find all three before the timer runs out to win the level and earn up to three stars. Levels get progressively busier and the targets get subtler as you advance through the five difficulty tiers.

Tips & Strategy

Scan the target list first so you know what to look for, then sweep the scene systematically — top to bottom or left to right — rather than randomly tapping. Use the hint button if you get stuck, but hints are limited so save them for tougher levels. Daily streaks build a habit and unlock a small bonus when you keep going.
