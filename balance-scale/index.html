Balance Scale — Free Online Coin Weighing Puzzle

Balance Scale is a classic logic puzzle where you must find the counterfeit coin among identical-looking coins using a balance scale and a limited number of weighings. The fake coin is either heavier or lighter than the rest — use deduction to pinpoint it before you run out of weighings!

How to Play Balance Scale

  • Select coins and place them on the left and right pans of the balance scale, then press the Weigh button.
  • Observe the result: Left heavier, Right heavier, or Balanced.
  • Use the result to narrow down which coin is fake. If the pans balance, all coins on them are genuine.
  • Continue weighing with your remaining suspects until you identify the counterfeit coin.
  • Mark the counterfeit coin and submit your answer. You have a limited number of weighings per level!

Tips & Strategy

  • Always try to divide suspects into three groups — put ⅓ on each pan and keep ⅓ off. This maximizes information from each weighing.
  • If a pan tips, the fake is on the scale. If balanced, the fake is off the scale — eliminating a third of your suspects.
  • Track which coins have been on which side. A coin that was on the heavier side twice is likely the heavy fake.
  • Use known-genuine coins as reference weights to test individual suspects.
  • Fewer weighings used = more stars earned. Plan ahead before each weigh!
