⚓ How to Play Ships Finder

A single-player logic puzzle — find the hidden fleet!

• Numbers around the grid show how many ship cells are in each row/column.

• Tap a cell to cycle: empty → ship → water → empty.

• Ships never touch — not even diagonally. Mark cells around a known ship as water.

• Fleet: match the exact ship counts shown below.

• When your marks satisfy every clue, press Check to win!
