# BENCHMARK: Shape Fold Puzzle

## Competitor Analysis

### Primary: Shape Fold (Poki)
- **Developer**: Studio indie (multiple Shape Fold versions exist)
- **Gameplay**: Rigid panels connected by hinges, fold up/down to form target shapes
- **Categories**: Animals, objects, geometric shapes
- **Monetization**: Interstitial ads between levels, banner ads
- **USP**: Satisfying tactile feel of folding rigid panels vs paper

### Secondary: Fold games (mobile)
- **Folded Fusion, Paper Fold Master**: Various mobile folding puzzle clones
- **Feature set**: 50-200 levels, daily challenges, hint systems
- **Downloads**: Fold puzzle category ~10-50M total across titles

### Differentiation Strategy
| Feature | Competitors | GameZipper Shape Fold |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| Mechanic | Hinged panels | Hinged panels (same core) |
| Levels | 50-200 | 30 handcrafted across 5 tiers |
| Star system | Sometimes | Yes — move-count based |
| Daily challenge | Sometimes | Yes |
| Hint system | Yes | Yes (limited hints) |
| Undo | Sometimes | Yes |
| Cost | Free+ads / IAP | Free + Monetag ads |
| Platform | Mobile app | Browser (no download) |

### Key Success Factors
1. Tactile feel: smooth fold animations with easing
2. Visual clarity: clear hinge indicators, target preview
3. Progression: easy → hard curve with new mechanics introduced gradually
4. Polish: shadows, highlights, satisfying SFX on fold
5. SEO: "shape fold", "fold puzzle", "folding puzzle" — low competition keywords
