Goal

Draw rectangles on the grid so every cell belongs to exactly one room — and each room contains exactly one circle.

●
●

Circles are your clues. They tell you where rooms must reach.

How to Draw

Tap & drag from one corner of a room to the opposite corner. Lift your finger (or release the click) to lock the room.

Or simply tap two cells to define a rectangle between them.

●

Two completed rooms shown above — each contains exactly one circle.

Rules

• Every cell must belong to exactly one room (full coverage, no gaps, no overlaps).
• Every room must contain exactly one circle.
• Rooms are rectangles — any size from 1×1 to N×N.

Tools & Stars

Undo removes the last room. Clear wipes the board. Hint (3 per level) flashes a correct room.

Earn 3 stars by solving fast with no hints.
