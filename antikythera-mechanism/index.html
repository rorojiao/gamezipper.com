How to Play

Goal: Turn the crank to find the position where ALL dial pointers align with their target markers.

Controls:

  • Tap CW to turn the crank clockwise
  • Tap CCW to turn counterclockwise
  • Each turn moves ALL dials simultaneously

Dials: Each dial rotates at a fixed gear ratio. For example, a ratio of 2 means the dial moves 2 positions for every 1 crank turn.

Stars: Solve in the minimum number of moves for 3 stars!

Inspired by the ancient Antikythera Mechanism, a 2000-year-old Greek astronomical computer that predicted eclipses using interlocking bronze gears.
