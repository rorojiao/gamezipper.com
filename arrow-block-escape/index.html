How to Play

Tap to Slide

Every cube has an arrow on its top. Tap a cube — if the cell in its arrow direction is open, it slides out and vanishes.

Rotate the View

Drag the stack, use the on-screen arrows, or press ←→ to rotate and ↑↓ to tilt. Find cubes hidden behind others.

Win

Clear every cube within the move limit. Fewer wasted taps earn more stars. Blocked taps still cost a move, so plan ahead!

Power-ups

Hint reveals a removable cube. Bomb removes any cube. Shuffle re-randomizes arrows but stays solvable.
