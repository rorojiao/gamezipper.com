Anagram Word Puzzle - Free Online Unscramble Game

Play anagram word puzzle online for free. Unscramble 6 letters to find all hidden 3-6 letter words across 30 challenging levels. A brain-training word game perfect for fans of word puzzles, anagram solvers, and spelling games.

How to Play Anagram

  • You are given 6 scrambled letters. Tap or click letter tiles to select them in order.
  • Form valid English words that are 3 to 6 letters long.
  • Press Enter or tap Back to submit your word. Use the Shuffle button to rearrange tiles.
  • Find all required words to complete the level and earn stars.
  • Progress through 30 levels across 5 difficulty tiers — from Beginner to Master.

Tips & Strategy

  • Start with 3-letter words — they're the easiest to spot and help you warm up.
  • Look for common prefixes and suffixes (re-, un-, -ing, -ed, -er) to build longer words.
  • Use Shuffle when stuck — a new arrangement often reveals words you missed.
  • Vowels are key: count how many you have and think about where they can go.
  • Longer words score more points: 3 letters = 10 pts, 4 = 20 pts, 5 = 50 pts, 6 = 100 pts.
