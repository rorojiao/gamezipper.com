How to Play

1. Grow. Tap the glowing seed at the bottom. A tree climbs and forks toward the sun.

2. Prune. Drag across any branch to cut it. Stray pieces fall away, freeing growth for the branches you keep.

3. Bloom. Guide branch tips into the golden light to bloom flowers. Avoid the red zones.

4. Stars. Prune efficiently — leftover growth budget earns extra stars.
