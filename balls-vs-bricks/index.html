⚪ Balls vs Bricks

Aim a stream of bouncing balls, crush every numbered brick. Collect +1 balls, chain bombs & splitters, dodge indestructible blocks. 30 levels + endless!

How to Play

Aim: Press and drag downward below the glowing launcher (slingshot-style). A dotted line previews the angle & wall bounces.

Shoot: Release to fire all your balls along that path. They bounce off the left, right & top walls, then fall off the bottom and reload.

Bricks: Each hit lowers a brick's number. Clear all numbered bricks to win the level.

Specials: 💣 Bomb clears a 3×3 area on death. ⇄ Splitter spawns 2 bonus balls. ＋1 adds a permanent ball. ∅ Indestructible blocks the path.

Keys: Space/Enter fire · R restart · U undo · M mute · Esc menu.

How to Play Balls vs Bricks

Goal: Destroy every numbered brick by firing a stream of bouncing balls. Aim by dragging downward below the launcher (slingshot pull-back), watch the dotted preview, then release to shoot. Balls ricochet off the left, right, and top walls and fall off the bottom to reload.

Bricks: The number is its HP — each ball hit lowers it by 1. At 0 the brick shatters. +1 icons grow your stream permanently. 💣 Bombs wipe a 3×3 area, ⇄ Splitters release bonus balls, and ∅ indestructible blocks must be pathed around.

Endless: Bricks descend one row every shot — survive as long as possible! Clear tougher bricks for 3 stars.

Keyboard: Space/Enter fire · R restart · U undo · M mute · Esc menu.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I aim and shoot in Balls vs Bricks?Press and drag downward anywhere below the launcher — like pulling back a slingshot. A dotted trajectory line shows your firing angle and predicted wall bounces. Release to launch all your balls along that path.
What do the +1 ball powerups do?Grabbing a +1 icon permanently adds one ball to your stream for the rest of the level. Fly your balls through them to snowball your firepower and crush bricks faster.
What are bomb and splitter bricks?Bomb bricks (orange 💥) explode and destroy every brick in a 3×3 area when destroyed. Splitter bricks (cyan ⇄) spawn two extra balls that fire off at an angle. Indestructible blocks (gray ∅) can never be destroyed — aim around them.
Is Balls vs Bricks really free with no download?Yes! It's 100% free, runs entirely in your browser, and needs no download or sign-up. It works on desktop, mobile, tablets, and Chromebooks — even on school networks.
How do endless mode and the daily challenge work?In endless mode, a new row of bricks descends every turn and HP ramps up — survive as many rows as you can. The daily challenge is a seeded endless run, the same for everyone each day, so you can compare your score.
