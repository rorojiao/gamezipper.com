Bolt Jam 3D — Free 3D Screw Puzzle Game

Play Bolt Jam 3D free online! Unscrew the bolts in the right order to free all the items. A satisfying 3D mechanical puzzle game.

How to Play

  • Tap a bolt to unscrew it and remove the piece it holds.
  • Remove pieces in the correct order to avoid jams.
  • If a piece is blocked, unscrew others first to free it.
  • Clear all pieces to complete the puzzle.

Game Features

  • 🔩 Realistic 3D bolt unscrewing mechanics
  • 🧩 Increasingly complex puzzle designs
  • 😌 Satisfying and relaxing gameplay loop
  • 📱 Intuitive tap controls

Tips & Tricks

  • Always start with bolts that have only one piece to remove.
  • Look for bolts at the edge — they usually go first.
  • Think 2 moves ahead before tapping.
