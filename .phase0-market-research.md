## 🏆 SELECTED CANDIDATE: Valve Network

### Game: **Valve Network** — Hydraulic Flow Control Puzzle
- **Slug**: `valve-network`
- **Category**: puzzle (valve + pipe + flow routing + logic)
- **Core Mechanic**: Toggle open/close valves on a pipe network grid. Each valve connects pipe segments. Goal: route water (or colored fluids) from source(s) to target tank(s) without flooding wrong outlets. 
- **Differentiation vs existing pipe games**:
  | Existing | Overlap | Valve Network difference |
  |----------|---------|--------------------------|
  | pipe-connect (5 games) | rotate pipes | **No rotation — toggle valves** (binary open/close state, no rotation) |
  | liquid-sort (8 games) | liquids | **No sorting — routing** through a valve graph |
  | cup-flow (1 game) | pouring math | **Network routing**, not volume math |
  | flow-connect (1 game) | connect source→target | **Valves are gates** (on/off), not paths; multiple fluids; pressure |

### Why this wins (scoring 21/25)
- **Search demand (4/5)**: "valve puzzle", "pipe puzzle", "plumber puzzle" are high-volume; valve mechanic is fresh in hypercasal 2025
- **Implementation (4/5)**: Graph + flow simulation, single-file Canvas, BFS solvable, 30 levels feasible
- **Mobile (5/5)**: Tap-to-toggle is the most mobile-friendly interaction
- **Depth (4/5)**: Multi-source, colored fluids, pressure constraints, valves that affect multiple pipes
- **SEO (4/5)**: "valve", "plumber", "hydraulic", "pipe network" — strong keyword cluster, zero existing competition for "valve"

### Tier Plan (30 levels)
1. Tier 1 (1-5): Single source, single target, simple valve toggles
2. Tier 2 (6-10): Two targets, must choose correct path
3. Tier 3 (11-15): Colored fluids + color-matched targets
4. Tier 4 (16-20): Pressure valves (open only when upstream pressure met)
5. Tier 5 (21-25): Multiple sources, cross-contamination avoidance
6. Tier 6 (26-30): Complex networks with loops and feedback

### Technical Approach
- Graph data model: nodes (junctions) + edges (pipes) + valves (edge toggles)
- Flow simulation: BFS/DFS from source through open valves
- Win condition: all targets receive correct fluid, no overflow
- Solver: backtracking CSP (same family as logic-puzzle-csp) to verify unique solutions
