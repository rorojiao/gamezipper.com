Lights Out - Free Online Grid Puzzle Game

Play Lights Out online for free — the classic grid puzzle game where you toggle lights to turn them all off. With a 5x5 grid and 27 hand-crafted levels from Beginner to Master, every puzzle has a guaranteed solution. Perfect for fans of logic puzzles, light switching games, and mathematical brain teasers. How to Play Lights Out

  • The grid has lights that are on or off. Your goal is to turn all lights off.
  • Click or tap a light to toggle it — but it also toggles the 4 adjacent lights (up, down, left, right).
  • Each press affects a plus-shaped pattern centered on the clicked cell.
  • Turn off every light on the grid to solve the puzzle.
  • Tackle 27 levels ranging from Beginner to Master difficulty.
  • Every puzzle is guaranteed solvable — it just takes the right sequence.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use the chase the lights technique: work top to bottom, pressing lights below any that are still on in the row above.
  • The last row determines whether you've solved it — learn the specific patterns needed to clear it.
  • Pressing the same light twice cancels out — avoid redundant moves.
  • Order doesn't matter for the final state, but systematic solving helps you track progress.
  • If you get stuck, restart and try the chase method from a different starting point.
  • Master-level puzzles may require solving from the bottom up or using known parity patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lights Out free to play?

Can I play Lights Out on mobile?

Yes — the game is fully touch-friendly. Tap any light to toggle it and its neighbors.

What's the best strategy for Lights Out?
