How to Play

Tap a tile to toggle it and its 4 neighbors (up, down, left, right). Turn all lights OFF to win the level.

1. Tap any tile. Watch the chain reaction.
2. Every tap flips up to 5 lights. Plan ahead!
3. Use Chase Strategy: tap a tile below any lit cell to clear each row from the top.
4. HINT shows the optimal next move. UNDO steps back. RESET restarts the puzzle.

Earn 3★ for matching the optimal move count, 2★ within 5 moves, 1★ for completing.
