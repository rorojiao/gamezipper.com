❓ How to Play

🎯 Goal

Drag stickers from the tray at the bottom onto the canvas to complete each picture. Place every sticker correctly to win!

🕹️ Controls

Drag: Touch/mouse a sticker in the tray and drag it onto the canvas.
Rotate: Tap a sticker in the tray or press the 🔄 button to spin it 90°.
Snap: When near the right spot, the sticker glows and snaps into place automatically.

⭐ Stars

Beat the target time for 3 stars. Hints reduce your star rating.

💡 Hints

Use a hint to flash the outline of the next sticker's position. Each hint used lowers your potential stars by one.

🔄 Layering

Place background stickers first. Foreground stickers layer on top automatically.
