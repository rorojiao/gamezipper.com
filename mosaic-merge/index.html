How to Play

Goal: Reach the target tier or score before running out of moves.

Select: Tap a mosaic tile to select it. It glows when active.

Merge: Tap an adjacent tile of the SAME tier. They merge into the next tier up!

Move: Tap an empty adjacent cell to slide the tile there. Different-tier tiles swap places.

Tiers: Tiles evolve from 1 to 6. Each merge creates a higher tier.

Scoring: Each merge awards points = new tier times 10. Plan chains for big scores!

Stars: 3 stars = solve well under move limit. 2 stars = within limit. 1 star = just barely.

Tip: Use empty cells to reposition tiles. Think ahead — every move counts!
