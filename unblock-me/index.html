Play Unblock Me free online. Slide blocks to free the red block and guide it to the exit in this classic sliding puzzle. 50+ levels with hints, undo, and star ratings. No download required.

How to Play

  • Slide blocks horizontally or vertically by clicking and dragging (or tapping and swiping).
  • Each block moves only in its orientation direction — horizontal blocks slide left/right, vertical blocks slide up/down.
  • Clear a path for the red block to reach the exit (usually on the right side).
  • Blocks cannot overlap or leave the board.
  • Solve in the minimum number of moves to earn 3 stars.
  • Use Hints if stuck, or Undo to revert moves.

Tips & Tricks

  • Work backwards: Visualize what the board needs to look like for the red block to exit, then figure out how to get there.
  • Clear the exit path first: Focus on removing blocks directly between the red block and the exit.
  • Minimize moves: Each unnecessary move costs a star. Plan multi-step sequences before executing.
  • Use corners wisely: Push non-essential blocks into corners and edges where they won't interfere.
  • Don't rush: Unblock Me rewards patience. Take time to study the board before making your first move.
