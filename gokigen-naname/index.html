How to Play

  1. Click or tap any cell to cycle through empty, back-slash, and forward-slash. Every cell must end up with exactly one diagonal.
  2. Numbers at the corners count how many of the surrounding cells' diagonals touch that point. The clue must match exactly.
  3. A 0 means no diagonal may touch that corner. A 4 means all four surrounding cells must touch it.
  4. Avoid closed loops. If four or more diagonals form a closed cycle, the puzzle is invalid.

You win when every clue is satisfied (turns green) and every cell is filled.
