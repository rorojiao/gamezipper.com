How to Play

1
Study the pattern strip at the top. It shows a sequence of shapes that follow a hidden rule.
2
Figure out the rule. What changes between each shape? It could be color, shape type, size, rotation, count, or a combination.
3
Pick the answer from four choices below. Tap the one that correctly continues the pattern.
4
Use hints wisely. The Hint button removes two wrong answers. Build streaks for bonus points!
