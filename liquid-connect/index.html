How to Play Liquid Connect

Objective

Connect each colored liquid source to its matching target by rotating pipe tiles to form continuous paths.

Controls

  • Tap or click a pipe tile to rotate it 90 degrees clockwise
  • All colored sources must be connected to their matching targets

Pipe Types

  • Straight - connects two opposite sides
  • Curve - connects two adjacent sides
  • T-Junction - connects three sides
  • Cross - connects all four sides
  • Empty - blocks flow

Stars

Complete within par moves for 3 stars. Use hints and undo freely.
