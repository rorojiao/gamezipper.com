How to Play

  • Compare the two side-by-side images and look for subtle differences.
  • Tap or click on a difference when you spot one.
  • Find all differences in a level to advance to the next.
  • Use the Hint button if you're stuck (limited per level).
  • Zoom in on mobile to examine fine details more closely.
  • Complete the daily challenge for bonus rewards.

Tips & Tricks

  • Scan systematically: Don't randomly glance — sweep your eyes left-to-right, top-to-bottom across both images.
  • Focus on edges: Differences are often at the boundary of objects — missing lines, changed colors, or altered shapes.
  • Check colors carefully: Subtle hue shifts are common. Compare matching areas side by side.
  • Look for missing items: Objects present in one image but absent in the other are classic differences.
  • Save your hints: Only use hints when truly stuck. Finding differences yourself builds better observation skills.
  • Play daily: Regular play sharpens your visual scanning speed over time.
