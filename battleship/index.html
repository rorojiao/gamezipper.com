HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Sink all enemy ships before they sink yours!

Placement Phase

Place your 5 ships on your grid. Click a ship to select it, then click on your grid to place it. Click the ship again or press R to rotate before placing. Use "Random" for automatic placement.

Ships

Carrier (5 cells), Battleship (4 cells), Cruiser (3 cells), Submarine (3 cells), Destroyer (2 cells)

Battle Phase

Click on the enemy grid to fire. You'll see HIT or MISS feedback. In Salvo mode, you get one shot per living ship each turn.

Difficulty Levels

Easy: Random guessing
Medium: Hunts around hits
Hard: Uses probability and strategy

Controls

Click to place/fire. R key or click ship to rotate. Touch supported.
