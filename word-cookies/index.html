How to Play

Swipe through the circular letter wheel to connect letters and form words. Adjacent letters can be connected.

Release to submit. Correct words fill into the crossword blanks above. Invalid attempts shake the wheel.

  • Swipe through 2+ adjacent letters
  • Shuffle rearranges the letters
  • Hint reveals the first letter of a remaining word
  • Find all words to earn up to 3 stars
  • Find bonus words for extra coins

About

Word Cookies features 30 handcrafted levels across 5 difficulty tiers. Tier 1 has 4-letter wheels with short words; Tier 5 has 7-letter wheels with longer words.
