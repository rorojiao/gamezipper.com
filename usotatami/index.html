Lying Tatami — Nikoli region division puzzle

Click between two cells to toggle a border. Each strip must be 1-cell-wide with one clue (number ≠ length).
How to play: Divide the grid into 1-cell-wide strips. Click the edge between two cells to draw/erase a border. Each strip must contain exactly one clue, and the clue number must NOT equal the strip's length (it lies!). No four strip corners may meet at any point. Keys: 1 draw, 2 erase, H hint, R restart, Enter check, Esc menu.
