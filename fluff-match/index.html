How to Play

1. Slide rows: Drag left or right on a row to shift all creatures in that row.

2. Slide columns: Drag up or down on a column to shift all creatures in that column.

3. Match 3+: When 3 or more creatures of the same color line up, they pop!

4. Chain reactions: Popping creatures causes others to fall, which can trigger more matches for bonus points!

Special pieces: Locked fluffs must be matched to unlock. Mega Fluffs (2x2) explode in a 3x3 area. Rainbow fluffs match any color!
