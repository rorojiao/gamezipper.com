Peg Blast - Free Online Aim & Bounce Puzzle Game

Play Peg Blast online for free — the addictive Peggle-style aim-and-bounce peg puzzle! Aim your cannon, launch balls that ricochet off pegs under gravity, and clear every orange target peg across 30 levels. Perfect for fans of Peggle, physics bounce games, and aim-and-shoot puzzles. No download, mobile-friendly.

How to Play Peg Blast

  • Move your cannon left and right to aim your shot.
  • Launch a ball — it falls under gravity and bounces off pegs it hits.
  • Hitting a peg removes it after the ball settles or the turn ends.
  • Clear all orange target pegs to complete the level.
  • You have a limited number of balls — use them wisely.
  • Blue and other colored pegs are optional but often block your path — clear strategically.

Tips & Strategy

  • Aim for clusters — a single ball can ricochet through many pegs, clearing them efficiently.
  • Bank shots off walls and pegs can reach orange targets that are hard to hit directly.
  • Clear blocking pegs first if they're shielding orange targets underneath.
  • Save power pegs (if available) for moments when you're stuck or low on balls.
  • Predict the bounce trajectory — pegs act as small circular bumpers.
  • Some levels reward patience — let the ball settle naturally rather than rushing the next shot.
