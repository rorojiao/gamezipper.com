## Top Candidate: Jumping Shell — 22/25

### Mechanic
Nesting-doll platformer puzzle. Player is a creature wearing multiple shell layers. 
Press jump to jump; press shed to drop one shell layer (max 1-5 layers per level). 
Each level requires planning: shed shells in the right spots to fit through narrow gaps, 
reduce weight to activate light switches, or create platforms from shed shells to reach higher areas.

### Why It's a Gap
- No nesting/layering/stacking platform puzzle in the 570-game catalog
- Unique mechanic not covered by any existing game (stickman-swing is arcade, 
  wood-block-puzzle is static block placement, unblock-me is sliding)
- 10M+ plays on Poki, appears on front page of puzzle category
- Single-button core control (jump/shed) = perfect for mobile/touch

### Score Breakdown (22/25)
- Market demand (5/5): 10M+ plays, Poki front page, proven viral
- Unique mechanic gap (5/5): Zero overlap with existing catalog
- Buildable HTML5 (4/5): Medium complexity — platformer physics + nesting layers
- Mobile friendly (4/5): One-button control, touch-first
- SEO value (4/5): "Jumping Shell" has brand search volume
