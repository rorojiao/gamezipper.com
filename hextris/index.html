How to Play

A / D
Or Left / Right arrows — rotate the hexagon to slide columns beneath the falling block.
HOLD
On mobile, hold the left half to rotate left, right half to rotate right.
SPACE
Pause the game at any time.
x3
Line up 3 or more same-colored blocks in a column or around a ring to clear them.
COMBO
Clear within 3 seconds to raise the multiplier. Cascading clears build chain bonuses.
BOMB
A bomb wipes out every block of its color across all columns when it lands.
RAIN
Rainbow blocks match any color — perfect for completing tricky lines.
