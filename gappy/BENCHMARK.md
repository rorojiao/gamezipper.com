## Competitor Analysis

### Nikoli Original (gappy)
- Published as official Nikoli puzzle
- Rules: Fill 1-N in each row and column; same numbers can't be king-adjacent (share edge or corner)
- Print puzzle, no digital version widely available

### Similar Games on Market
- Sudoku (Latin square with 3x3 boxes) — 1B+ downloads, massive market
- Futoshiki (Latin square with inequalities) — popular on puzzle apps
- Suguru/Tectonic (number placement in regions) — we have suguru
- Kropki (Latin square with dot markers) — we have kropki

### Differentiation
- Gappy's king-move adjacency constraint is UNIQUE — no other Latin square variant on GameZipper uses it
- Creates a visual puzzle that "looks like Sudoku but plays differently"
- The diagonal constraint creates interesting patterns and reasoning chains

## Unique Selling Points
1. Only Diagonal Latin Square browser game on GameZipper
2. Clean, simple rules (easy to learn, hard to master)
3. 30 unique-solution levels across 5 difficulty tiers
4. Visual feedback for constraint violations (highlight conflicts)
5. Full feature set: hints, undo, 3-star, level select, keyboard
