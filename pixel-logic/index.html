How to Play

What is Nonogram?

Nonogram (also known as Picross or Griddler) is a logic puzzle where you fill in cells on a grid based on number clues to reveal a hidden picture.

Controls

  • Left click / Tap — Fill a cell
  • Right click / Long press — Mark X (cell is empty)
  • Drag — Fill or mark multiple cells in a row/column

Reading the Clues

Numbers next to each row and above each column indicate groups of consecutive filled cells. For example, "3 2" means there is a group of 3 filled cells followed by a group of 2, with at least one empty cell between them.

Tips

  • Completed rows/columns have their clues grayed out
  • Use hints sparingly — each costs 100 points!
  • Enable error checking for a gentler experience
  • Your progress is saved automatically
