Color Lines — Free Online Classic Match-5 Puzzle

Color Lines is the beloved classic puzzle where you move colored balls on a grid to form lines of five or more of the same color. Each move adds new random balls to the board — plan ahead to clear lines before the grid fills up! Simple to learn, endlessly addictive.

How to Play Color Lines

  • Click a ball to select it, then click an empty cell to move it there. The ball must have a clear path through empty cells.
  • Arrange 5 or more balls of the same color in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to clear them.
  • After each move that doesn't clear a line, 3 new balls appear on the board in random positions.
  • The game ends when the grid is full and no more moves are possible.
  • Keep clearing lines to maximize your score!

Tips & Strategy

  • Focus on building longer lines (6, 7, or more) — they're worth significantly more points.
  • Keep paths open! A ball can only move through empty cells, so don't block your routes.
  • Watch the preview of upcoming balls — plan where to place them before they appear.
  • Try to set up multiple lines that share colors, so clearing one helps build another.
  • Avoid scattering colors randomly — concentrate on 1-2 colors at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Color Lines free to play?

Yes! Color Lines is 100% free to play online in your browser. No download or signup needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Absolutely — tap to select a ball and tap again to move it. Fully touch-optimized for mobile.

How many balls do I need to clear a line?

You need at least 5 balls of the same color in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). Longer lines give more points!

Why can't I move my ball?

Balls can only move through a clear path of empty cells. If the path is blocked by other balls, the move isn't allowed. Plan your routes!
