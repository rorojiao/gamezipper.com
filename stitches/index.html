How to Play

Goal: Connect every pair of neighbouring regions with exactly one stitch.

Stitch: A line between two cells that share an edge (horizontally or vertically adjacent) AND belong to different regions.

Rules:
• Each pair of neighbouring regions gets exactly ONE stitch.
• No cell can be an endpoint of more than one stitch.
• Border numbers = how many stitch endpoints are in that row/column.

Controls: Click an edge between two cells to place/remove a stitch. Or click a cell then click an adjacent cell of a different region.

Keyboard: 1 = Place, 2 = Erase, H = Hint, R = Restart, Enter = Check, Esc = Menu.
