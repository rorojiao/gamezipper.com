Crankshaft Linkage

Rotate crankshafts to drive sliders into target zones. Timing belts couple cranks — fewer moves, more stars.

How to Play

  • Goal: Move all sliders into their green target zones.
  • Controls: Tap CW / CCW buttons to rotate each crank group by 45°.
  • Timing Belts: Cranks linked by a dashed belt rotate together.
  • Stars: Solve in fewer moves for higher star ratings.
  • Keyboard: Arrow keys or A/D to rotate. Number keys 1-3 to select group.
