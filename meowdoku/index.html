❓ How to Play

🐱 Goal: Place cats on the grid so every row, column, and colored region has exactly one cat.

🚫 Rule: No two cats can be adjacent — not even diagonally!

👆 Tap a cell to place or remove a cat.

💡 Hints show a correct cat position (limited uses).

❤️ You have 3 hearts per level. Wrong placements cost a heart.

⭐ Earn stars based on your score percentage. Unlock cute cats with stars!
