How to Play

  • White moves first — then players alternate turns
  • Click a piece to select it, then click a valid square to move
  • Each piece has different movement rules (Queen moves any direction, Knight moves in L-shape, etc.)
  • Put the opponent's King in check to threaten capture
  • The game ends when the King is in checkmate (no escape) or stalemate (no legal moves)
  • Use Undo to take back a move, or New Game to restart

🎯 Chess Tips for Beginners

  • Control the center: Move your pawns to e4, d4, e5, or d5 early to dominate the board.
  • Develop pieces: Get your knights and bishops into play before moving the same piece twice.
  • King safety: Castle early to protect your king and activate your rook.
  • Think ahead: Before each move, ask yourself: what does my opponent want to do?
  • Don't rush: Take your time. Chess rewards patience and planning over speed.
