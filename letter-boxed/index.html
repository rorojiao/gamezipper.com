How to Play

1. Letters sit on 4 sides of a box, each side a different color.

2. Tap letters to spell a word. Adjacent letters in your word must come from different sides.

3. Words must be at least 3 letters and real English words.

4. After submitting a word, your next word must start with the last letter of the previous word.

5. Use all 12 letters to win. Fewer words = higher score!
