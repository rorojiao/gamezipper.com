Akichiwake - Shading Division Logic Puzzle

🍁 Akichiwake
Divide into two regions • Logic puzzle

🎮 Play

Completed: 0 / 30 levels
Rules: Divide the grid into two connected regions — shaded (dark) and unshaded (light). Each numbered cell shows how many of its 4 orthogonal neighbors share the same color.

Controls: Click a cell to cycle: Empty → Shaded → Unshaded → Empty. Right-click or long-press to cycle backwards.

Tips: Clue "4" means all neighbors are the same color. Clue "0" means none are. Both regions must be connected to win.
