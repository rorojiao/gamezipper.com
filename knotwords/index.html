How to Play

Knotwords is a crossword puzzle where you deduce letters from clues.


Bundles: Colored groups of cells share the same set of letters. Look at the letter bank for each bundle color.


Goal: Fill every cell so that all words across and down are valid English words.


Hints: Use "Reveal" to show a letter, or "Check" to highlight errors.


Scoring: Earn up to 3 stars per level. Faster times and fewer hints = more stars!
