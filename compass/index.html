How to Play
  • Goal: Shade cells so every compass's numbers are satisfied.
  • Each compass shows four numbers: how many shaded cells lie in the North, East, South, West directions (in a straight line to the grid edge).
  • Tap an empty cell to shade it; tap again to clear.
  • White cells must all stay connected (touching up/down/left/right).
  • No two shaded cells may touch orthogonally.
  • Use Hint if stuck (3 per level, costs points). Press Check to verify.
