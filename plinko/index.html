How to Play

Objective

Drop balls from the top and score as many points as possible by landing in high-value slots.

Controls

  • Click/tap at the top to choose drop position
  • Drag left/right to aim the drop
  • Arrow keys to move, Space to drop

Classic Mode

Score points with 10 balls. Reach the target score to earn 3 stars. Complete each level to unlock the next.

Peggle Mode

Clear all orange pegs within 10 balls. Blue pegs are obstacles. Center slot gives a free ball!

Challenge Mode

Special mechanics per level: moving pegs, bumpers, multipliers, teleport pegs, and gates. Score high with 10 balls!
