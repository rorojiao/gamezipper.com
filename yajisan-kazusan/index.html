How to Play

🎯 Goal: Shade cells black according to clues.

⬜ White Rule: White cells must never be orthogonally adjacent (diagonal OK).

🔢 Clue Rule: A number shows black cells in that direction from the adjacent cell.

⬛ Black Freedom: Black cells can form any shape, even diagonally connected.

💡 Tip: Use the arrows to see which direction each clue applies to.
