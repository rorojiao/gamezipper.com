Sand Balls - Free Dig & Roll Physics Puzzle

Play Sand Balls online for free. Dig through sand to guide colorful balls into matching containers. With 20+ satisfying physics-based levels, it's perfect for fans of digging games, physics puzzles, and casual brain teasers.

How to Play Sand Balls

  • Drag through the sand to dig tunnels and channels.
  • Guide colorful balls into the matching colored container at the bottom.
  • Use gravity and physics — balls roll down the paths you create.
  • Match each ball to its correct color container to complete the level.
  • Progress through 20+ levels of increasing complexity.

Tips & Strategy

  • Plan your dig path before starting — think about where each color needs to go.
  • Dig smooth slopes — steep drops can send balls flying off course.
  • Use walls and barriers in the sand to separate different colored balls.
  • Dig from top to bottom — let gravity do the work.
  • Some levels have obstacles — dig around them carefully.
