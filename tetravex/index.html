How to Play Tetravex
Each square tile has 4 numbers — one on each edge (top, right, bottom, left).

Drag tiles from the tray onto the grid so that adjacent edges match. When two tiles share an edge, both numbers on that edge must be equal!

Use logic: corners and borders have fewer neighbors, so they're often easiest to place first.
