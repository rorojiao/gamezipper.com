## DECISION: Plug Master (#520)

**Slug**: `plug-master`
**Concept**: Connect colored plugs to matching sockets by drawing paths. No crossing lines.
**Why this game**:
1. **Zero overlap** - no similar game in 552-game catalog (plug/socket/wire/cable all return NONE)
2. **2024 viral hit** on mobile H5 platforms (Plug Master, Pin & Pull variants)
3. **Simple mechanic** = quick to prototype, easy mobile touch controls
4. **High engagement** - 30 levels of increasing grid complexity creates natural retention loop
5. **Ad-friendly** - short solve times (15-60s) = frequent ad opportunities between levels

**Core Mechanic**:
- Grid of pegs with colored plugs on edges
- Draw a path from each plug to its matching colored socket
- Paths cannot cross each other
- Fill every plug-socket pair to clear the level

**Difficulty Curve**:
- Tier 1 (Beginner): 3x3 grid, 2 pairs (levels 1-6)
- Tier 2 (Easy): 4x4 grid, 3 pairs (levels 7-12)
- Tier 3 (Normal): 5x5 grid, 4-5 pairs (levels 13-18)
- Tier 4 (Hard): 6x6 grid, 5-6 pairs + obstacles (levels 19-24)
- Tier 5 (Master): 7x7 grid, 7-8 pairs + walls (levels 25-30)

**Monetization**: Interstitial ads between levels, rewarded ad for hints
