❓ How to Play

Goal: Get the GOLD cargo branch (branch 1) to rest at the dashed target height after a settle step.

Pulley (○ at top of each branch): tap to flip its engagement state.
Engaged pulley (●): its branch joins the shared axle — its height participates in equalization.
Disengaged pulley (○): its branch hangs free at its current height, ignored during equalization.

Settle rule: after each flip, all ENGAGED branches snap to floor(average of their heights). Plan your flips so the cargo ends up exactly at the target.

Win: cargo branch is at the exact target height. Cargo branch may be either engaged or disengaged at the end.
Moves vs Par: solve in the declared par for 3 stars, par+2 for 2 stars, more for 1 star. Tier 1-2: Single Hoist 3-4 branches, target 3-4 of max 4-5. Tier 3-4: Beam Balance 5 branches, deeper flip sequences. Tier 5-6: Grand Crane 6 branches, ceiling-pushing targets, master-level sequences.
