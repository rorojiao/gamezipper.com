# Aqua Digger — Competitive Benchmark

## Primary Competitor: Where's My Water? (Disney)
- **Publisher**: Disney Interactive
- **Downloads**: 100M+ (Google Play)
- **Rating**: 4.5★
- **Core Mechanic**: Dig through dirt to route water to Swampy the alligator's bathtub
- **Key Features**:
  - Grid-based digging (swipe to remove dirt)
  - Water physics simulation (gravity, pressure, flow)
  - Multiple water types (clean, dirty, poison)
  - Collectibles (rubber ducks, mystery items)
  - Hazards (fire, poison, algae)
  - 3-star rating per level
  - 500+ levels across multiple chapters
  - Character story/narrative
- **Monetization**: Free-to-play with IAP (level packs, hints)
- **Art Style**: Cartoon, colorful, character-driven

## Secondary Competitors

### Happy Glass (Lion Studios)
- **Mechanic**: DRAW lines to guide water into glass (different from digging)
- **GZ Coverage**: YES (happy-glass exists)
- **Difference**: Drawing-based, not digging-based

### Sugar Sugar (CrazyGames/Poki)
- **Mechanic**: DRAW lines to guide sugar particles
- **GZ Coverage**: YES (sugar-sugar exists)
- **Difference**: Drawing-based, particle physics

### Dig This (Voodoo)
- **Mechanic**: Dig through terrain to guide balls/objects to goal
- **Similar**: Grid-based digging mechanic
- **Difference**: Object-based, not water-flow

## Feature Parity Requirements for Aqua Digger

### Must-Have Systems (from Where's My Water?):
1. **Grid-based digging** — click/drag to remove dirt cells
2. **Water flow physics** — cellular automaton, gravity-based
3. **Multiple cell types** — dirt, rock, water source, goal, poison, fire, duck
4. **30 levels** — 6 tiers, progressive difficulty
5. **3-star rating** — based on water efficiency + duck collection
6. **Progress save** — localStorage with level completion + stars
7. **Level select** — grid showing locked/unlocked/completed levels
8. **Tutorial** — first 2 levels guide player
9. **Restart** — quick level restart
10. **Audio** — dig sound, water flow, splash, success

### Unique Selling Points:
- Clean modern GameZipper aesthetic (dark neon theme)
- No character story overhead (pure puzzle focus)
- Fire/Poison hazard mechanics for added depth
- Switch/valve mechanics in later tiers
- Smooth 60fps Canvas rendering
- Fully responsive (mobile + desktop)
