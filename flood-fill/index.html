Flood Fill - Free Online Color Puzzle Game

Play Flood Fill online for free — a satisfying color puzzle game where you spread colors across a grid to fill every cell in the minimum number of moves. With 30 levels, daily challenges, and star ratings, it's perfect for fans of flood fill puzzles, color games, and casual brain teasers. No download required.

How to Play Flood Fill

  • The grid starts with cells of different colors. Your goal is to make the whole grid one color.
  • Tap a color button to change the top-left region's color — it absorbs all adjacent cells of that color.
  • Each move, your connected region grows by absorbing matching neighboring cells.
  • Fill the entire grid within the move limit to win.
  • Use fewer moves than the par to earn more stars.
  • Complete 30 levels plus daily challenges for endless fun.

Tips & Strategy

  • Plan ahead — think 2-3 moves in advance rather than reacting to the current board.
  • Aim to absorb the largest possible region each move — bigger absorption means faster filling.
  • Don't waste moves on small color pockets early — they'll get absorbed naturally later.
  • Watch the edges and corners — isolated color regions there are the hardest to reach.
  • If you're over the move limit, restart and try a different color sequence.
  • In daily challenges, speed matters — practice pattern recognition to solve faster.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flood Fill free to play?

Yes! Flood Fill is 100% free to play in your browser. No download or signup required.

Can I play Flood Fill on mobile?

How many levels does Flood Fill have?

30 handcrafted levels with progressive difficulty, plus daily challenges that offer fresh puzzles every day.

How do star ratings work?

You earn stars based on how few moves you use. Solve under the par limit for 3 stars, at par for 2, and over par for 1.

What are daily challenges?

Daily challenges are procedurally generated puzzles that refresh every day, giving you unlimited new content beyond the main levels.
