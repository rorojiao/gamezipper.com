### Mechanic (spec for Phase 3 build)
- **Core loop**: Drag pre-cut stickers from a tray → place onto canvas guide → snap to correct position → complete picture
- **Visual**: Beautiful canvas template with faded outline guides. Vibrant sticker tray at bottom. Satisfying snap animation + particle burst on correct placement.
- **Sticker types**:
  - Fixed position (drag to correct spot)
  - Rotation required (tap to rotate before placing)
  - Layered (place background stickers before foreground)
- **Difficulty progression**:
  - T1 (Sticker Fun): 3-4 large stickers, no rotation, generous snap zones
  - T2 (Picture Time): 5-6 stickers, some rotation, tighter snap
  - T3 (Sticker Craft): 7-9 stickers, rotation + layering
  - T4 (Master Artist): 10-12 stickers, multi-layer scenes
  - T5 (Sticker Wizard): 13-15 stickers, complex layered scenes with precise placement
- **Levels**: 30 handcrafted levels across 5 tiers (6 per tier)
- **Scoring**: Time-based 3-star system. Hints available (show ghost outline of next sticker).
- **Features**: Undo (remove last placed sticker), level select, localStorage progress save

### Distinct from catalog (3-layer gate confirmed)
- `/jigsaw-puzzle/`: grid-cut interlocking pieces — DIFFERENT (stickers are arbitrary shapes, no interlocking)
- `/tangram/`: 7 fixed geometric pieces forming silhouettes — DIFFERENT (stickers are pre-designed decorative shapes)
- `/color-by-number/`: painting numbered regions — DIFFERENT (physical sticker placement, not painting)
- `/poly-art-3d/`: 3D polygon art — DIFFERENT (2D sticker placement, no 3D rotation)
- `/perler-beads/`: pixel bead placement on grid — DIFFERENT (free-form sticker positioning)
