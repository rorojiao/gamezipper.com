How to Play Gomoku

Rules

Gomoku is a strategy game for two players. Black always moves first. Players take turns placing one stone on any empty intersection. The first player to get 5 stones in an unbroken row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins. If the board fills up with no winner, it is a draw.

Controls

  • Click or tap an intersection to place your stone
  • Use Undo to take back your last move (VS AI only)
  • Switch between VS AI and 2 Player modes anytime
  • Adjust AI difficulty or board size in Settings

Strategy Tips

  • Control the center — center positions give more options in every direction
  • Build open threes — three in a row with both ends open is very hard to block
  • Create forks — two simultaneous threats force a guaranteed win
  • Block opponent's fours — always block immediately when they have four in a row
  • Think ahead — plan several moves in advance, just like chess
