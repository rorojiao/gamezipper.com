How to Play
Goal: Connect adjacent numbers that sum exactly to the target shown at the top of the screen.
Swipe: Press on a number, then drag to any neighboring cell — including diagonals. Each cell can only be used once per swipe. Release to submit.

Power-ups:
· Shuffle — Re-randomize the board numbers.
· Undo — Step back through your swipe moves.
· Hint — Briefly highlight one valid path.
Stars: Finish quickly to earn 3 stars. Each hint or shuffle costs a star.
