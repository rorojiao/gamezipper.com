How to Play

1. Select a piece from the tray below

2. Tap an empty grid cell to place it

3. Tap a placed mirror to rotate it (/ or \\)

4. Press Fire Beam to test your solution

5. The laser must hit ALL targets to win

Pieces: Mirrors redirect the beam. Splitters divide it. Walls block it. Color filters change the beam color.
