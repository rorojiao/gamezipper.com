## R22 Decision: Crossword Upgrade (Option 1 — Iterative Upgrade)

### Why Crossword?
- **Massive SEO demand**: "crossword puzzle" = 2.7M monthly searches (Google Trends)
- **Only 12 puzzles** vs standard 30 — severely under-developed
- **Missing localStorage save** — players lose progress on refresh
- **No audio at all** — no SFX, no BGM
- **Separate game.js (28KB)** — clean architecture, easy to expand
- **91 word entries across 12 puzzles** — room for 250+ words across 30 puzzles
- **No star ratings tied to performance** (timer exists but no star system)
- **5×5 to 9×9 grid range** — can expand to 10×10+ for expert tier

### Upgrade Plan
1. Expand from 12 → 30 puzzles (5 tiers × 6 puzzles: 3 easy, 3 medium, 3 hard, 3 expert, 3 master)
2. Add localStorage save (completed puzzles + best times + hints used)
3. Add Web Audio API SFX (correct letter, wrong letter, puzzle complete, hint)
4. Add ambient BGM (toggleable)
5. Add 3-star rating system (based on time + hints used)
6. Add visual level select grid (replace dropdown)
7. Add how-to-play overlay (first visit)
