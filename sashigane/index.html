How to Play

Goal: Divide the grid into L-shaped regions of exactly 3 cells.
Each region is formed by a 2×2 block with one cell removed (an "L").
Circles: Each region contains exactly one circle.
Black circle (●) is at the corner of the L.
White circle (○) is at one tip of the L.
How to play: Tap internal edges to draw walls. Cells connected without walls form regions. Check your solution when done.
Example (6×6 grid with 12 regions):
┌───┬───┬───┬───┬───┬───┐ │ ● │ │ │ │ ● │ │ ├───┼───┼───┼───┼───┼───┤ │ │ ○ │ │ ● │ │ │ └───┴───┴───┴───┴───┴───┘
(Each L is 3 cells. The black dot is the corner, white is a tip.)
