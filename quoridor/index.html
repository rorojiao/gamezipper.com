🧱 How to Play Quoridor

Goal: Be the first to move your pawn to the opposite side of the board. Blue (you) races to the bottom row. Red (AI) races to the top row.

On your turn, choose ONE action:

♟ Move pawn — one square in any direction (N/S/E/W). If the opponent is directly adjacent and the square behind them is empty, you can jump over them!

🧱 Place a wall — a fence spanning 2 cells, horizontal or vertical. You have 10 walls. Walls cannot overlap, and must never fully enclose a player.

Puzzle Mode: 30 hand-crafted challenges. Find the winning move in 1 turn. 3★ = optimal without hints, 2★ = any win, 1★ = hint used.

vs AI Mode: Free play against 3 difficulty levels (Easy / Medium / Hard).
