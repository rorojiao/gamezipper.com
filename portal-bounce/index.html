Portal Bounce - Free Online Portal Puzzle Game

Play Portal Bounce online for free — the addictive portal-placement brain puzzle! Place warp portals to redirect a bouncing ball through stars to the goal. With 30 handcrafted levels across 6 tiers, it's perfect for fans of portal puzzles, physics bounce games, and redirect-and-route brain teasers. No download, mobile-friendly.

How to Play Portal Bounce

  • A ball bounces continuously under gravity — you don't control it directly.
  • Tap or click on valid surfaces to place an entry portal (blue) and exit portal (orange).
  • The ball warps from the entry portal to the exit portal, redirecting its path.
  • Collect all stars and guide the ball to the goal to complete the level.
  • You can reposition portals to adjust the ball's trajectory mid-bounce.
  • Progress through 6 tiers of increasing complexity and new mechanics.

Tips & Strategy

  • Portal momentum matters — the ball exits the orange portal with the speed and direction it entered the blue one.
  • Place portals on walls and ceilings to redirect the ball upward or sideways.
  • Think about the ball's trajectory arc — where will it land after exiting the portal?
  • Some levels require timing — place or move portals as the ball bounces.
  • Collect all stars before reaching the goal for a perfect score.
  • If the ball gets stuck in a loop, reposition a portal to break the cycle.
