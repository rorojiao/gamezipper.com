Power Wash Puzzle - Free ASMR Cleaning Game

Play Power Wash Puzzle online for free. Draw spray paths to blast away dirt and reveal clean surfaces. With 30 satisfying ASMR cleaning levels across 5 tiers, it's perfect for fans of cleaning games, ASMR games, and relaxing puzzles.

How to Play Power Wash

  • Draw a path on the surface to aim your power washer spray.
  • The spray blasts away dirt along the path you draw.
  • Clean the entire surface to complete the level and earn stars.
  • Use Undo (Ctrl+Z) to fix mistakes, Hints (H key) if stuck, and Restart (R key) to try again.
  • Progress through 30 levels across 5 difficulty tiers for increasingly complex cleaning challenges.

Tips & Strategy

  • Plan your spray path before drawing — long continuous strokes are more efficient than short ones.
  • Clean edges and corners first — they're easy to miss.
  • Watch your water/energy usage — efficient paths earn more stars.
  • Use Undo freely — it doesn't cost anything and helps you perfect your route.
  • If a level feels impossible, use a Hint to see the optimal path.
